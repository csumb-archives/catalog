Global Studies B.A. ~ Careers and Graduate Study

Related Careers

Global Studies graduates have achieved notable success in foreign relations, government, non-governmental organizations, international organizations, organized labor, international service learning, development studies, education, politics, and business.

Graduate Study

Global Studies graduates have been accepted at and have attended a variety of institutions of higher learning including American University; Monterey Institute of International Studies; UC Santa Cruz; UC Davis; New York University; Stanford Law School; and the Institute for Social Studies, The Hague, Netherlands.

They have achieved notable success in the following fields of graduate study: business administration, global studies, international relations, law, political science, public administration, and Teaching English as a Second Language.
