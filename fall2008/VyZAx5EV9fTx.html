Executive MBA ~ Distinguishing Features

The EMBA program is a comprehensive management program.

You'll complete the program entirely online, less one one-week in-person summer session.

The EMBA program follows a fixed curriculum.

You can complete the program in just 24 months.

10-week courses are offered during the fall, winter, spring, and summer terms.
