Do you offer programs in human resources?

If you want to pursue a career in human resources, we offer the following pathway:

  • Business Administration B.S.
    • Hospitality Management Concentration
    • Hotel/Resort Management Concentration
    • Management and International Business Concentration
  • Collaborative Health and Human Services B.A.
    • Community Health Concentration
    • Social Work Concentration
  • Social and Behavioral Sciences B.A.
    • Anthropology Concentration
    • Political Economy Concentration
    • Sociology Concentration
    • Social History Concentration
