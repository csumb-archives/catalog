Do you offer history?

If you want to pursue a career in history, we offer the following pathways:

  • Hispanic Cultures and History Minor
  • Human Communications B.A.
    • History, Oral History, and New Media Concentration
    • Single Subject History and Social Sciences Waiver
  • Japanese Culture and History Minor
  • Social and Behavioral Sciences B.A.
    • Archaeology Concentration
    • Social History Concentration
    • Subject Matter Preparation Program in History/Social Science for the Single Subject Teaching Credential
