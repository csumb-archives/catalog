Tuition & Fees ~ Fall 2008 Payment/Refund Deadlines

Payment Deadlines

August 1, 2008 ~ Last day to pay registration fees, otherwise your classes will be dropped if you have not completed the FAFSA

August 4, 2008 ~ Starting today, payment or a Financial Aid fee deferment is required prior to registration.  This includes students who's enrollment was cancelled.

November 3, 2008 ~ Last day of Fall 2007 fee deferment for financial aid students ~ Last day to have fees paid without a $20 administrative late fee and/or a $20 late housing fee ~ Last day to have fees paid without a Business & Finance hold placed on account

Refund Deadlines

August 22, 2008 ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses and receive a full refund of mandatory fees

September 8, 2008 ~ Last day to drop from fulltime to part-time and receive a full refund of the state fee difference (Nonresident students also receive full refund of tuition)

September 18, 2008 ~ Last day for Open University students to drop and receive a prorated refund

October 30, 2008 ~ Last day to officially withdraw or cancel all courses and receive a prorated refund of mandatory fees.

Registration Deadline

August 22, 2008 ~ Last day to register without being assessed a $25 late registration fee

Installment Payment Plan

First installment due with signed contract ~ Installments are due on or before the listed due date ~ $20 Administrative Late Fee is assessed for each late payment made

September 7, 2008 ~ 2nd installment due

October 7, 2008 ~ 3rd installment due

See  list on the Campus Service Center website.

Financial Holds

Business and Finance (BF) holds are placed on accounts with balances. For financial aid fee deferred students, a BF hold will be placed on your account following the deferment deadline.
