Student Disability Resources at CSU Monterey Bay

Student Disability Resources

.

Student Disability Resources (SDR) promotes the empowerment, wellness, and full integration of students with disabilities into campus life and the campus community.

Further, we seek to provide academic and personal support services to students with disabilities in order for them to attain success at California State University, Monterey Bay.
