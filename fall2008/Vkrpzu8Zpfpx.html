Kinesiology B.S.

Professions in kinesiology and wellness studies continue to rate among the fastest growing in the world. Individuals, corporations, and government entities have come to recognize that the wellbeing of individuals creates the foundation for the wellbeing of everything else, including our society and economy. By providing you with broad knowledge, skills, abilities, and attitudes, our Kinesiology major will prepare you to pursue a number of the highly demanded professions.
