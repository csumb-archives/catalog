Tuition & Fees ~ Fall 2005 Payment/Refund Deadlines

April 1, 2005 ~ First day Installment Payment Plan (IPP) contracts available for Fall 2005 (excluding CalStateTEACH)

July 15, 2005 ~ Second payment due for Fall 2005 Installment Payment Plan (excluding CalStateTEACH) ~ Last day to apply for the Fall 2005 Installment Payment Plan (first and second payments due, excluding CalStateTEACH)

August 15, 2005 ~ Third and final payment due for Fall 2005 Installment Payment Plan (excluding CalStateTEACH)

August 19, 2005 ~ Last day to register without a $25 late registration fee ~ Last day for students without a financial aid fee deferral to pay registration fees without a $20 late administrative fee ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses and receive a full refund of mandatory fees ~ Last day to apply for financial aid and have fees deferred for Fall 2005 semester

September 6, 2005 ~ Last day to drop from fulltime to part-time and receive a full refund of the state fee difference (nonresident students also receive full refund of tuition)

September 16, 2005 ~ Last day for Open University students to drop and receive a prorated refund

October 27, 2005 ~ Last day to officially withdraw or cancel all courses and receive a prorated refund of mandatory fees

November 1, 2005 ~ Last day of fall 2005 fee deferment for financial aid students ~Last day to have fees paid without a Business & Finance or Meal Plan hold placed on account ~ Last day to have fees paid without a $20 Late Administrative Fee or $20 Late Housing Fee
