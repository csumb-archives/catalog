Do you offer a program studying trade?

If you want to pursue a career in trade, we offer the following pathways:

  • Business Administration B.S.
    • Accounting Concentration
    • Agribusiness Management Concentration
    • Entrepreneurship/Intrapreneurship Concentration
    • Information Technology/e-Commerce Concentration
    • Management and International Business Concentration
  • Business Minor
  • Global Studies B.A.
  • Global Studies Minor
