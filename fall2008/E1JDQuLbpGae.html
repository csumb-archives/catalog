International Programs ~ Distinguishing Features

Exploration ~ There are opportunities to study in places a world away from CSUMB such as Buenos Aires, Granada, or Beijing.

Access ~ Our international programs and domestic exchange programs range in cost, and many offer scholarships and financial aid options.

Opportunity ~ Studying abroad gives you the chance to distinguish yourself from others and to take classes that aren't offered at CSUMB.

Diversity ~ Diversify your perspective and your experiences by studying in a different environment.

Captivating ~ After your overseas experience, you'll have a new way of seeing the world, your community, yourself, and your future.
