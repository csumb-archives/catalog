Do you offer programs in education?

If you want to pursue a career in education, we offer the following pathways:

  • Global Studies B.A.
  • Health and Wellness Minor
  • Human Communication B.A.

    • Single Subject History and Social Sciences Waiver Concentration

  • Human Development Minor
  • Japanese Language and Culture Minor
  • Japanese Culture and History Minor
  • Liberal Studies B.A. Teaching Pathway
  • Management and Information Technology M.S.
  • Mathematics B.S.
    • Secondary Mathematics Education Concentration
  • Mathmatics Minor
  • Multiple Subject Credential
  • Music Minor
  • Special Education Credential
  • Service Learning Leadership Minor
  • Single Subject Credential
  • Social and Behavioral Sciences B.A.
    • Subject Matter Preparation Program in History/Social Science for the Single Subject Teaching Credential
  • Spanish Language and Hispanic Cultures Minor
  • Teledramatic Arts B.A.
  • World Languages and Cultures B.A.
  • Writing and Rhetoric Minor
