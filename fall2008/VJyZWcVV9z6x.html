Financial Aid ~ Fee Deferment

Financial Aid applicants are granted a fee deferment to allow additional time for award processing. The deferment expires on November 1 for the fall semester and April 1 for the spring semester. All outstanding charges become due when all financial aid awards have been paid. If you have outstanding university charges after these deadlines, CSUMB will assess late charges and place registration holds on your student record.
