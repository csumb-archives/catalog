Kinesiology B.S. ~ Academic Advising and Support Services

KIN students craft their academic programs and career development strategies with guidance from faculty, staff, and seasoned mentors from the local community.

KIN faculty members hold several advising workshops each semester immediately before course registration. Designated KIN faculty and staff members meet individually with majors, pre-majors, and prospective majors throughout the academic year and during much of the summer.

Our students also take advantage of universitywide services relating to health and wellness, including:

  • Academic Skills Achievement Program (ASAP)
  • Migrant Student Support Services (MSSS)
  • Office of International Programs (OIP)
  • Student Disability Resources (SDR)
  • Undergraduate Advising
  • Personal Growth and Counseling Center (PGCC)
    • for students who want and/or need assistance with anything from personal issues to optimizing decision-making, from problem-solving and conflict resolution to stress-management.

You may also contact our office to speak with one of our faculty members by either emailing the department at kinesiology@csumb.edu or by calling 831-582-4259 to schedule an appointment.
