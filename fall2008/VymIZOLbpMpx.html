Welcome to the Campus Service Center!

New Hours
Monday: 8:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m.
Tuesday-Friday: 8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.

We are located in the Student Services Building 47, First Floor

The Campus Service Center ("The CSC") provides forms, information, and front counter support for Admissions & Recruitment , Records & Registration,  Financial Aid, and Cashiering. The CSC distributes parking permits (Prices), Otter ID Cards and paychecks. The CSC is able to provide information and services with its access to student records in the Banner system and a fully functional cashiering system.
