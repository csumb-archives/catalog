Tuition & Fees ~ Spring 2005 Payment/Refund Deadlines

November 1, 2004 ~ First day Installment Payment Plan (IPP) contracts available for Spring 2005 (excluding CalStateTEACH)

December 15, 2004 ~ Second payment due for Spring 2005 Installment Payment Plan (excluding CalStateTEACH) ~ Last day to apply for the Spring 2005 Installment Payment Plan (first and second payments due, excluding CalStateTEACH)

January 15, 2005 ~ Third and final payment due for Spring 2005 Installment Payment Plan (excluding CalStateTEACH)

January 21, 2005 ~ Last day to register without a $25 late registration fee ~ Last day for students without a financial aid fee deferral to pay registration fees without a $20 late administrative fee ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses and receive a full refund of mandatory fees ~ Last day to apply for financial aid and have fees deferred for Spring 2005 semester

February 7, 2005 ~ Last day to drop from fulltime to part-time and receive a full refund of the state fee difference (nonresident students also receive full refund of tuition)

February 18, 2005 ~ Last day for Open University students to drop and receive a prorated refund

April 1, 2005 ~ Last day of the Spring 2005 fee deferment for financial aid students ~ Last day to have fees paid without a Business & Finance or Meal Plan hold placed on account ~ Last day to have fees paid without a $20 Late Administrative Fee or $20 Late Housing Fee

April 8, 2005 ~ Last day to officially withdraw or cancel all courses and receive a prorated refund of mandatory fees
