Health and Wellness Minor ~ Overview

In the Health and Wellness minor, you study the breadth and depth of various dimensions covered in the field of wellness education. You'll learn about personal well-being and skill enhancement that you can implement into your chosen major. If you want to live optimally and help the well-being and self-responsibility of others , this minor is for you.

Your Health and Wellness advisor will help you choose the courses that best fulfill your interest and are applicable to you major and/or your life path.

Integration with Majors

Regardless of major, the Health and Wellness Minor expands your base of knowledge and understanding of health and wellness and prepares you to evolve both personally and professionally.
