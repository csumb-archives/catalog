Business Administration B.S. ~ Careers and Graduate Study

Business graduates have entered and achieved notable success in advertising, agribusiness marketing, banking, finance, management/management training, marketing, nonprofit management, operations management, retail management, sales, small business startup, technology management, electronic commerce, and entrepreneurship.

They have also gone to graduate programs, including the Management and Information Technology M.S. and Executive M.B.A. at CSUMB.
