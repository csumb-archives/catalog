Environmental Science, Technology & Policy B.S. (formerly ESSP)

If you have an interest in science or technology, care about people and the environment, and want to pursue a career or advanced graduate study in environmental science, resource management, habitat restoration, marine science, science education, scientific research, or related fields, then this is the CSUMB major for you!

Because of our major's emphasis on practical, real-world learning, our graduates have been highly successful at getting great jobs and gaining admission to some of the nation's top graduate and professional schools.

You will find the official information about the Environmental Science, Technology & Policy major right here. Simply use the left-side navigation or the matching links at the bottom of this page.

For more detailed information including a look at student work, faculty bios, research partnerships, and internship opportunities, please visit the Environmental Science, Technology & Policy major website.
