Outdoor Education Minor ~ Home

No place in the world can beat the Monterey Bay region when it comes to diverse outdoor recreation opportunities. Our Outdoor Education minor leverages our natural resources to allow you to hone your outdoor and recreation knowledge and skills in preparation for a variety of careers and a lifetime of enjoyment.

You will find the official information about the minor right here. Simply use the left-side navigation.

For additional information please visit the Kinesiology Department website.
