Computer Science & Information Systems Minor ~ Overview

The Computer Science & Information Systems Minor provides you with an opportunity to explore your particular interests in the development of software applications, computer networking, and information systems. After taking introductory classes in these areas,  you will focus on one area to acquire depth and expertise to prepare you for further study or a career.

Integration with Majors

CSIS allows you to tailor your minor curriculum so that it can be integrated with many other major programs at CSUMB. You will be able to find many applications for the skills you acquire in this minor, to any major from Business to Music, and a host of others.

We recognize that we have an obligation to provide high quality educational opportunities to all CSUMB students who want a deeper understanding of the information systems discipline in order to enhance their learning experience and career opportunities.
