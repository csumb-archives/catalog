Mathematics, B. S. ~ Student and Alumni Quotes

At CSUMB we were lucky enough to have a lot of one-on-one teaching experiences. With the smaller class sizes, you are also able to find study groups easily. The small class brings the students together; they are more willing to work together. Also, the professors have a great understanding of the material that they are teaching. Sometimes they go above and beyond the text to help you understand the concepts being taught. My overall experience in the math department was nothing less than excellent.

~ DOUGLAS LEMON '07, GRADUATE STUDENT, UC SANTA BARBARA

Studying and working in the CSUMB mathematics department has been enlightening and inspiring, thanks to all the support and individual attention received from all of the instructors and staff. Working in such a tight-knit group has been a great experience in helping build my knowledge of mathematics along with building lasting friendships.

~ CASEY BLAKEMAN '07, GRADUATE STUDENT, University of SAN FRANCISCO

The CSUMB mathematics and statistics department is a wonderful program to be a part of during undergraduate education. Every faculty and staff member in the department cares about the students and their education tremendously. The professors quickly become your friends as well as your instructors. I couldn't have come across a better math program for undergraduate study than the CSUMB math and statistics department.

~ PAM JOHNSON '07, GRADUATE STUDENT, USC

As a returning student, small class sizes and the ability to interact with instructors put CSUMB on my checklist. Professor Hu's command of mathematics and infectious enthusiasm for the subject also drew me to CSUMB. He amply demonstrated his commitment to student success by establishing independent study courses for winter and summer semesters that enabled students to meet their graduation date goals. Professor Hu also provided valuable insight and encouragement as I completed my research Capstone-my first such research in a pure mathematics context, and it left me with a taste for doing more!

~ WILLIAM HOSTROP '06, HIGH SCHOOL MATH TEACHER, MONTEREY, CA

I had a wonderful experience in the Math department. I found all the professors to be truly supportive, understanding, caring, and flexible. I felt welcomed and at ease. The department also offered all the courses I needed in order to graduate in two years.

~ THIENANH KHONG '07, MIDDLE SCHOOL MATH TEACHER, SANTA CRUZ COUNTY

The great courses offered by the Mathematics and Statistics Department at CSUMB provide valuable content that prepares you for the work force and future studies. I work as a data analyst and a lot of the programming I use I learned through math courses. The teachers are eager to assist student with Capstone experiences that not only provide educational benefits to senior students, but also give students resources and abilities they can offer future employers.

~ CHRISTINA SCHMUNK '06, DATA ANALYST, DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE
