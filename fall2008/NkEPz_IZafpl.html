Visual and Public Art B.A. ~ Home

Our Visual and Public Art major helps you develop lifelong skills that bring together studio and community art approaches to artistic production, exhibition, and education. You can learn painting and murals; sculpture, installation, and performance art; arts education and museum studies; and photography and digital art. Students and practitioners work together to learn skills of historical analysis, individual aesthetics, community collaboration, art production, evaluation, and art distribution. Come join us as we create public art that is meaningful to you, represents the hopes and dreams of local communities, and serves as a bridge among cultural and social groups.

You will find the official information about the VPA major right here. Simply use the left-side navigation or the matching links at the bottom of this page.

For more detailed information including galleries of student work, please visit the Visual and Public Art Department website.
