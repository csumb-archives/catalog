Teledramatic Arts and Technology B.A.~ Learning Environment

TAT facilities and resources provide the infrastructure and support to realize creative and technologically innovative ideas. These include streaming media facilities; TAT-TV cable channel; a production studio with green screen; a digital media computer lab, and uncompressed edit suites on a high speed network supported by the latest software applications including editing, DVD, motion graphics, web, and 2D/3D animation production; film and HD video cameras; lighting and sound equipment; wireless and GPS-enabled PDAs and computer tablets; and projectors.

TAT presents media and technology supported productions and screenings in venues across campus, including the TAT Broadcast Studio, World Theater, and Black Box Cabaret. Instruction is held in classrooms and labs with media support, wireless Internet, and computer support.
