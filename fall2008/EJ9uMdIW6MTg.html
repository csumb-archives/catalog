Teledramatic Arts and Technology B.A. ~ Distinguishing Features

Collaborative, project-based courses give you a strong foundation in media practice while encouraging experimentation and innovation.

Cross-disciplinary learning experiences prepare you for the workplace of the 21st century.

Community-based learning partners you with social and cultural organizations and local schools.

Professional and industry guest speakers in lectures and workshops bring their knowledge and experience to campus.

Internships with professional organizations, companies, and festivals provide learning experiences and career development opportunities.

Independent study opportunities provide one-on-one mentoring experiences and advanced learning working with faculty on research and creative projects.
