If you transferred into CSUMB from a California community college as an AS-T certified student in Computer Science, you must complete the following courses for your bachelor's degree in Computer Science (CS) at CSUMB. If you are unsure about your transfer status, please contact your advisor.

Download the CCC TMC for Computer Science

Complete one of the following courses: (if American Institutions was not completed prior to transfer)

  • SBS 350: Domination & Resistance: US
  • SBS 385: Environmental History of CA

Complete all of the following courses:

  • CST 300: Major ProSeminar
  • MATH 170: Discrete Mathematics
  • CST 337: Computer Architecture
  • CST 205: Multimedia Design & Progmng
  • CST 373: Ethics in Comm & Tech
  • CST 400: Capstone Project Planning
  • CST 462S: Computer Sci and Community OR CST 361S: Technology Tutors

Complete one of the following Concentrations:

software engineering concentration

Complete all of the following courses:

  • CST 338: Software Design
  • CST 336: Internet Programming
  • CST 438: Software Engineering

nettwork & security concentration

Complete all of the following courses:

  • CST 311: Intro to Computer Networks
  • CST 312: Network Security
  • CST 412: Network Administration

Information systems concentration

Complete all of the following courses:

  • CST 363: Intro to Database Systems
  • CST 336: Internet Programming
  • CST 365: Business & Information Tech

game development concentration

Complete all of the following courses:

  • CST 320: Intro to Digital Games
  • CST 438: Software Engineering

All students complete FOUR of the following CS electives courses from the list below not already used to fulfill their Concentration:

  • CST 306: Game Engine Programming
  • CST 311: Intro to Computer Networks
  • CST 312: Network Security
  • CST 320: Intro to Digital Games
  • CST 334: Operating Systems
  • CST 336: Internet Programming
  • CST 338: Software Design
  • CST 363: Intro to Database Systems
  • CST 365: Business & Information Tech
  • CST 370: Design&Analysis of Algorithms
  • CST 412: Network Administration
  • CST 394: Internship
  • CST 438: Software Engineering
  • CST 463: Database System Administration
  • CST 495: Special Topics

None of the above elective courses can be double counted towards your concentration choice or towards a second concentration.
