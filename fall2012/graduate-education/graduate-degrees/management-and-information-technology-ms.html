In this era of a global marketplace empowered by rapidly advancing technology, leaders must possess both business acumen and technological insight. Those who become savvy in both management and technology will truly empower themselves to succeed.

To provide that level of preparation, MSMIT blends the teaching of core management disciplines and the science of information technology. It prepares you to assume managerial positions in high-tech companies or launch new businesses with a skillset that enables you to effectively lead a workforce of ethnic, cultural, and gender diversity. MSMIT offers both on-campus courses and complete online couses to maximize the flexibility and students' learning experience.

For more detailed information including a look at student work and faculty bios, please visit the MSMIT pages on the School of Information Technology and Communications Design website.

IMPORTANT NOTICE:
MSMIT program is not taking any new students. Planning is underway to accept applications for Fall 2012 or Spring 2013 soon. If you are interested in the program, please monitor this website and the MSMIT website for updates. Please address all inquiries to msmit@csumb.edu.
