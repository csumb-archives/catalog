Are you a fully employed business professional ready to accelerate into an executive management position? Through online courses, the executive track of the M.B.A. program allows you to complete 48 units of required courses within 24 months. A fixed curriculum, graduate business cohort program, the MBA offers courses through Extended Education in fall, winter, spring, and summer terms. When admitted you become a member of an approximately 25-student cohort that progresses through the program in a lock-step manner.

You will find all of the official information about the program right here. Simply use the navigation or the matching links at the bottom of this page.

You can also explore more detailed information on the School of Business website or at CSUMB.EDU/mba.
