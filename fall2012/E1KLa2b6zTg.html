The world is shrinking, and we need global citizens who possess the awareness and expertise to make that a positive. The Global Studies Minor covers both international and multicultural issues, furthering understanding of current global affairs and politics, the functioning of the global economy, and your location in the world.

You will find all of the official information about the minor right here. For more detailed information including a look at student work, faculty bios, projects, and events, please visit the Global Studies Department website.
