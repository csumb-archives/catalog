Required Courses

  • Complete both of the following:
  • SW 405: Social Work Practice (4 units)
  • HDEV 351: Lifespan Dev: Transit/Chng (4 units)
  • And choose one of the following electives:
  • SW 415: Social Work with Child/Youth (4 units)
  • OR
  • SW 425: Social Welfare Policy (4 units)

This concentration is part of the following major. View the major for requirement information.

Collaborative Health & Human Services
