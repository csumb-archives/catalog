Community Health

Required Courses

  • Complete both of the following:
  • PH 420: Multicult Health Ed & Promot (4 units)
  • PH 430: Global Women's Health (4 units)
  • And choose one of the following electives:
  • PH 440: Food Policy, Culture & Hlth (4 units)
  • OR
  • PH 450: Health Systems & Policy (4 units)

Public Administration Emphasis

The Public Administration emphasis orients students to basic fundamental principles and practices of public and nonprofit administration, focusing on the essential elements of program planning, design and evaluation, public budgeting, community economic development and applications of technology in the public and nonprofit sectors. 

Required Courses

  • Complete all of the following:
  • CHHS 412: Public Budgeting & Finance (4 units)
  • CHHS 422: Program Planning (2 units)
  • CHHS 432: Program Evaluation (2 units)
  • And choose one of the following electives:
  • BUS 330: Human Resources Management (4 units)
  • BUS 433: Mgmt of Nonprofit Organization (4 units)
  • CHHS 442: Community Econ Devel Policy (4 units)
  • CHHS 452: Tech in Public/Nonprofit (4 units)

Social Work

Required Courses

  • Complete both of the following:
  • SW 405: Social Work Practice (4 units)
  • HDEV 351: Lifespan Dev: Transit/Chng (4 units)
  • And choose one of the following electives:
  • SW 415: Social Work with Child/Youth (4 units)
  • OR
  • SW 425: Social Welfare Policy (4 units)
