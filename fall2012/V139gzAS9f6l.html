VIET 101: Begin Vietnamese I Assessment

Description
Alternative assessment for the first semester of Vietnamese. Freshmen and sophomore transfers attempting to satisfy the Language ULR through assessment must enroll in VIET 101, 102, 201, and 202 concurrently, except for international students who may enroll only in VIET 202. Junior and senior transfers attempting to satisfy the Language ULR through assessment, must enroll in VIET 101, 102, and 201, except for international students who may enroll only in VIET 201 or 202.

4
