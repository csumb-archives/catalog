The Psychology major emphasizes the scientific, experimental approach to the study of behavior and mental processes.  By emphasizing psychology's science-based identity, we expose you to a broad range of psychological theory, methods, research, and application.  To this end, the Psychology major at CSUMB fulfills all ten recommendations detailed by the American Psychological Association Board of Educational Affairs in "Undergraduate Psychology Major Learning Goals and Outcomes."
