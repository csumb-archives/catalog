If you transferred into CSUMB as an AA-T-certified student in Sociology, you must complete the following courses for your bachelor's degree in Social and Behavioral Sciences at CSUMB. If you are unsure about your transfer status, please talk to an advisor. 

SBS 300: Major/ProSem:Theo/Meth/SBS

SBS 300L: Professional Skills Soc Sci

SBS 328: Social Science Theory Module

SBS 366: Rsrch Mthd for SBS:Quant Emph

SBS 366L: Rsrch Mthd SBS:Quan Emph Lab

One additional SBS research methods course

SBS 338: Social Psychology

SBS 330: Crime & Violence

SBS 350: Domination & Resistance: US

At least 12 more units of advisor-approved 300-level courses in SBS, PSY, HDEV, CHHS, GS, or SW. One of these courses may double-count as upper-division GE and MLO III/Concentration. If not, then you must take an upper-division GE course for one of your electives below.

SBS 400: Senior Capstone Seminar I

SBS 404S: Service Learning Capstone (or other approved upper-division service learning course)

SBS 402: Senior Capstone Seminar II

SBS 405: Assessment Lab/Grad/Srs

(Total of 47 units for SBS major with sociology concentration)

8 units of world language (if requirement not already met)

1 unit of electives if world language requirement not met; 9 units of electives if world language already met.

Total units to degree after transfer: 60
