--- layout: archive title: Requirements for AA-T certified students in History CSUMB Catalog year: fall2012 ---

If you transferred into CSUMB as an AA-T-certified student in History, you must complete the following courses for your bachelor's degree in Social and Behavioral Sciences with a concentration in Social History at CSUMB. If you are unsure about your transfer status, please talk to an advisor. 

SBS 300: Major/ProSem:Theo/Meth/SBS (GWAR)(4 units)

SBS 300L: Professional Skills Soc Sci (GWAR)(2 units)

SBS 315: Growth & Conflict/US to 1920 (MLO III)(4 units)

GS 316: Historical Approaches (MLO II)(4 units)

SBS 342: Economic Thought (MLO III)(4 units)

SBS 362: Rsrch Mthd for SBS: Qual Emph (MLO II)(4 units)

SBS 362L: Rsrch Mthd SBS: Qual Emph Lab (MLO II)(1 units)

SBS 328: Social Science Theory Module (MLO I)(2 units)

SBS 350: Domination & Resistance: US (MLO III, D2)(4 units)

SBS 385: Environmental History of CA (MLO III)(4 units)

SBS 400: Senior Capstone Seminar I (MLO IV)(3 units)

SBS 404S: Service Learning Capstone (MLO IV, D4)(2 units)

SBS 402: Senior Capstone Seminar II (MLO IV)(3 units)

SBS 405: Assessment Lab/Grad/Srs (MLO IV)(1 unit)

Any adviser-approved history course from GS, HCOM, JAPN, MPA, SBS, SPAN, TAT, or VPA (4)

World Language (8 units)

Electives (6 units)
