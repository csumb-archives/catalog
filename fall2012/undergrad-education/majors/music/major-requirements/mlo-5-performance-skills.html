Students express themselves artistically in at least one major performance area at a level appropriate for the particular music emphasis.

Required Courses

  • Complete at least 6 units in the following courses
  • MPA 211: Beginning Guitar (1 unit)
  • MPA 212: Intermediate Guitar (1 unit)
  • MPA 213: Jazz Blues Guitar (1 unit)
  • MPA 214: Guitar Ensemble (1 unit)
  • MPA 309: Global Percussion (1 unit)
  • MPA 310: Gospel Choir (2 units)
  • MPA 313: Music & Performance (4 units)
  • MPA 315: Performance Study Intermediate (1 unit)
  • MPA 320: Chorale (2 units)
  • MPA 330: Voice Training (2 units)
  • MPA 332: Voice Training II (2 units)
  • MPA 340: Music Prod & Artistic Dev (2 units)
  • MPA 350: Advanced Performing Band (1 unit)
  • MPA 405: Ensembles I (1 unit)
  • MPA 406: Ensembles II (1 unit)
  • MPA 415: Performance Study Advanced (1 unit)
  • MPA 420: Advanced Voice Ensemble (1 to 2 units)
