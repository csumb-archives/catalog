To be well prepared for the Business B.S. degree program, we recommend you take the following pre-requisite courses prior to starting your upper division coursework in the major. You also should speak with your advisor about what you've taken so far and what your goals are in order to map out an academic plan that works for you. 

Preparation for Community College Transfer Students

General Education requires one lower division math class but Business requires that you complete two math classes:

  • Finite Math 1 or Precalculus
  • Finite Math 2 or Statistics

Prior to starting the Business Administration major core coursework at CSUMB, students must complete the following classes:

  • Macroeconomics and Microeconomics, which can be taken in any order.
  • Financial Accounting and Managerial Accounting.  You must pass Financial accounting prior to taking Managerial Accounting
  • Business Law
CSUMB Lower-Division Requirements for the Business Administration Major
  • MATH 115: Finite Mathematics or MATH 130: Precalculus
  • MATH 116: Finite Mathematics II or STAT 100: Introduction to Statistics
  • Micro Economics*
  • Macro Economics*
  • BUS 203: Financial Accounting
  • BUS 205: Managerial Accounting
  • BUS 206: Business Law
  • BUS 299: Decision Making w/ Excel
     
* See a Business Administration advisor about which are the appropriate Micro Economics and Macro Economics courses for you to take.
