CSUMB is collaborating with established nursing programs at the four community colleges in the area – Monterey Peninsula, Hartnell, Cabrillo and Gavilan – in a unique way that compliments and enhances the educational resources that already exist in our region.

Students will begin the program at one of the four partnering regional community colleges, spend time in a “blended” learning environment while completing their studies at the Community College and CSUMB. Students will receive an associate’s degree from their community college and a bachelor of science in nursing from CSUMB.
