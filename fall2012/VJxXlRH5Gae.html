The College of University Studies and Programs brings together nondegree academic programs that support students and faculty in teaching and learning, first year experience, academic planning, and service learning.

These programs reflect the CSUMB Vision in their commitment to access, retention, personal and academic planning, and a 21st-century liberal education.

Each program has an official catalog description, and full descriptions of the programs may be found on their program websites. These can be reached by clicking on the links provided below.
