To prepare well-rounded students who thrive when faced with the constantly changing demands of the modern workplace, we offer interdisciplinary programs that connect several traditional fields of study in meaningful ways. As a result, our academic programs provide clear pathways to over 200 careers.

Because you likely have a career or two in mind, we provide a Career/Academic Fields listing on our A-Z Directory so that you can look up potential career choices and see which CSUMB programs will prepare you for those fields. If you've been thinking in terms of fields of study rather than specific careers, you'll find those listed, too.

To use the Career/Academic Fields listing:

  • Locate a field of interest.
  • Look below it for related CSUMB academic programs.
  • Click a program name to read more about.

Don't know what you want to do with your life? CSUMB Career Development will work with you to identify your interests and strengths to find an ideal career.
