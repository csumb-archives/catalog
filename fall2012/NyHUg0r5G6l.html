Whatever your major is, a minor in Computer Science and Information Systems will  open many doors for you and greatly expand your career options. Whether you want to learn to develop or adapt software, create new computer applications, understand  networks, or master information systems including databases, the Computer Science & Information Systems Minor provides the opportunity.

You will find all of the official information about the minor right here. For more detailed information including a look at student work and faculty bios, please visit the School of InformationTechnology and Communication Design website.
