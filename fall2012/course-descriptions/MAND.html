MAND 101: Begin Mandarin I ASMT

Description
Alternative assessment for the first semester of Mandarin. Freshmen and sophomore transfers attempting to satisfy the Language ULR through assessment must enroll in MAND 101, 102, 201, and 202 concurrently, except for international students who may enroll only in MAND 202. Junior and senior transfers attempting to satisfy the Language ULR through assessment, must enroll in MAND 101, 102, and 201, except for international students who may enroll only in MAND 201 or 202.

4
