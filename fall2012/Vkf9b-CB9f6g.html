Want  to help positively shape the next generation as a middle school or high school teacher?

Whether you want to teach English, math, science, social studies, or foreign language, CSUMB's single subject credential program will prepare you well. We offer both traditional and internship options. You will find all of the official information about both right here.

For detailed information on the application process, field placement, and more, please visit the Single Subject website at http://csumb.edu/singlesubject
