Major ProSeminar

You must complete the Major ProSeminar course for your chosen major. You typically do this at the start of your junior year. Completion of the course constitutes official acceptance into your major.

Major Learning Outcomes

All CSUMB majors have a set of MLOs and requirements that you must satisfy through aligned courses or alternate assessment as designated by your major.

Senior Capstone

You must complete a Senior Capstone project per the specific requirements of your major. This Capstone must demonstrate your cumulative learning as it culminates your degree program.
