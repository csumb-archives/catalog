Sport Management minor helps you prepare  to become a coach or sports administrator. This minor focuses on either management or coaching, and covers multiple aspects of sport including ethics, leadership, coaching, law, risk management, event management, training and fitness, team building, competitive preparation, licensing and marketing. Coupled with any major, this minor is a winner!
