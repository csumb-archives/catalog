Within the Academic Affairs administrative division, four colleges oversee CSUMB's academic operations. Each college includes several academic units that deliver degrees, minors, and other academic offerings. The university currently offers a total of 22 undergraduate degrees with over 30 included concentrations, more than 20 minors, eight graduate degrees, and several teacher certification options. These pages indicate the alignment between these offerings and the colleges and academic units that deliver them.
