New Content: 

If you transferred into CSUMB as an AA-T-certified student in Journalism, you must complete the following courses for your bachelor's degree in Human Communication at CSUMB. If you are unsure about your transfer status, please talk to an advisor.

Download the CCC TMC for Journalism

Complete ALL of the following courses:

  • HCOM 300: Major ProSeminar (2 units)
  • HCOM 475: Senior Capstone (4 units)
  • HCOM 312: Cooperative Argumentation-Theory and Practice (4 units)
  • HCOM 307S: Soc Impct Of Mass Media SL (6 units)
  • HCOM 330: Intro Creative Writing (4 units)
  • SBS 350: Domination and Resistance: US since 1880 (4 units) (Fulfills American Institutions Requirement, GE Area D2)

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 306: Gender and Communication (4 units)
  • HCOM 309: Interracial Communication (4 units)
  • HCOM 310: Free Speech & Resp (4 units)

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 301: Ways Of Knowing (4 units)
  • HCOM 406: Philosophy According to Movies (4 units)

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 338: Multicultural Adolescent Lit (4 units)
  • HCOM 342: Feminist Theories & Methods (4 units)
  • HCOM 343: Race and Gender Justice (4 units)
  • HCOM 344: Chicana/Latina Experiences (4 units) (RTI)
  • HCOM 345: Chicano Life & Culture (4 units)
  • HCOM 348: Race, Colonialism, and Film (4 units) (RTI)

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 322: Asian American Literature (4 units)
  • HCOM 323: American Drama (4 units)
  • HCOM 324: African American Narratives (4 units)
  • HCOM 325: 20th Cent Narr Amer Immgr (4 units) (RTI)
  • HCOM 326: Jewish Holocaust Literature (4 units)
  • HCOM 327: Survey American Literature (4 units) (RTI)
  • HCOM 335: Amer Ethnic Lit & Culture (4 units)
  • HCOM 336: Poetry and Gender (4 units)
  • HCOM 337: Women's Literature (4 units)

Complete THREE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 307: Social Impact of Mass Media (4 units)
  • HCOM 307S: Soc Impct Of Mass Media SL (6 units)
  • HCOM 310: Free Speech & Resp (4 units)
  • HCOM 315: Media for Social Change (4 units)
  • HCOM 316: Media Ethics (4 units)
  • HCOM 319: Global Communication and Culture (4 units)
  • HCOM 334: Fiction/Creative NonFiction Wr (4 units)
  • HCOM 352: History According To Movies (4 units)
  • HCOM 370: Media and the Military (4 units)
  • HCOM 371: Community Journalism Studies (4 units)
  • HCOM 385: Reporting (4 units)
  • HCOM 387: Media Production Lab (4 units)
  • HCOM 388: Investigative Reporting (4 units)
  • HCOM 389: Otter Realm Workshop (4 units)

Upon prior consent of instructor:

  • HCOM 390: Magazine Writing (4 units)
  • HCOM 406: Philosophy According to Movies (4 units)
  • HCOM 486: Mass Media Internship (4 units)
  • HCOM 487: Mass Media Internship (2 units)
  • HCOM 487S: Community Media Project SL (6 units)

Students must also demonstrate second language proficiency through the 201 level.Read more about the Language Proficiency Requirement. If you need to begin language study at the 101 level, consult your department chair to determine your 60 unit pathway.
