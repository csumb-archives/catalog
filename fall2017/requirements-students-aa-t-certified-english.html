If you transferred into CSUMB as an AA-T-certified student in English, you must complete the following courses for your bachelor's degree in Human Communication at CSUMB. If you are unsure about your transfer status, please talk to an advisor.

Download the CCC TMC for English

To complete the requirements of the HCOM degree students must complete at least one course which is designated as RTI -Research/Theory Intensive.

Complete ALL of the following courses:

  • HCOM 300: Major ProSeminar (2 units)
  • HCOM 312: Cooperative Argumentation-Theory and Practice (4 units)
  • HCOM 475: Senior Capstone (4 units)
  • SBS 350: Domination and Resistance: US since 1880 (4 units) (Fulfills American Institutions Requirement, GE Area D 2)
  • HCOM 339S: Creative Writing and Service Learning (6 units)

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 315: Media for Social Change (4 units)
  • HCOM 316: Media Ethics (4 units)
  • HCOM 328: Latina Life Stories (4 units)
  • HCOM 329: Auto/Biografias (4 units)
  • HCOM 350: Oral Hist/Comm Mem (4 units)

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 306: Gender and Communication (4 units)
  • HCOM 309: Interracial Communication (4 units)
  • HCOM 310: Free Speech & Resp (4 units)

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 301: Ways Of Knowing (4 units)
  • HCOM 406: Philosophy According to Movies (4 units)

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 338: Multicultural Adolescent Lit (4 units)
  • HCOM 342: Feminist Theories & Methods (4 units) (RTI)
  • HCOM 343: Race and Gender Justice (4 units)
  • HCOM 344: Chicana/Latina Experiences (4 units) (RTI)
  • HCOM 345: Chicano Life & Culture (4 units)
  • HCOM 348: Race, Colonialism, and Film (4 units) (RTI)

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 322: Asian American Literature (4 units)
  • HCOM 323: American Drama (4 units)
  • HCOM 324: African American Narratives (4 units)
  • HCOM 325: 20th Cent Narr Amer Immgr (4 units) (RTI)
  • HCOM 326: Jewish Holocaust Literature (4 units)
  • HCOM 327: Survey American Literature (4 units) (RTI)
  • HCOM 335: Amer Ethnic Lit & Culture (4 units)
  • HCOM 336: Poetry and Gender (4 units)
  • HCOM 337: Women's Literature (4 units)

Writing & Rhetoric

Complete THREE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 301: Ways Of Knowing (4 units)
  • HCOM 306: Gender and Communication (4 units)

  • HCOM 310: Free Speech & Resp (4 units)

Creative Writing & Social Action
