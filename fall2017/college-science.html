Mission

To empower the CSUMB learning community by providing:

  • A broad-based, integrated approach to the study of science, technology and mathematics
  • Application of technology in the study of digital design and interactive media
  • A framework for making informed and ethical choices in the information age

Programs

The College of Science houses several academic units and administers a variety of academic offerings including undergraduate and graduate degrees, minors, and certificate programs as listed below. The academic units within the college organize learning experiences thematically and topically with an emphasis on finding workable and creative solutions to important local, regional, national, and global problems. Through their studies, students gain the knowledge and practical skills necessary to adapt and succeed while making useful contributions in a rapidly changing world.
