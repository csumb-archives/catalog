CSUMB awards a variety of credit and non-credit certificate programs which are available through both campus and Extended/Continuing education units. Many certificate programs are designed in cooperation with professional societies and industries to provide either entry level knowledge and skills or professional growth and development. Most certificate programs are comprised of multiple courses, and the certificate is awarded upon successful completion of all required coursework by matriculated students.
