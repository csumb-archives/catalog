Guidelines for Summer and Alternative Sessions Add/Drop Period:

  • Classes less than four weeks must be added/dropped prior to the start of the session
  • Classes four/five weeks long must be added/dropped within the first two days of the session
  • Classes eight/ten weeks long must be added/dropped within the first three days of the session
