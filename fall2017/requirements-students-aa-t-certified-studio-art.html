If you transferred into CSUMB from a California community college as an AA-T-certified student in Studio Art, you must complete the following courses for your bachelor's degree in Visual and Public Art at CSUMB. If you are unsure about your transfer status, please talk to an advisor.

Download the CCC TMC for Studio Arts

Complete ALL of the following courses:

  • VPA 300: VPA Major ProSeminar (4 units)
  • VPA 302: VPA Visiting Artist Series (2 units) (taken twice)
  • VPA 307: Diverse History of Contemporary Art (4 units) OR VPA 308: Ways Of Seeing (4 units)
  • VPA 310: Mixed Media (4 units)
  • VPA 309: Materials and Methods (2 units)
  • VPA 320S: Museum Studies (5 units) OR VPA 306S: Digital Public Art (5 units)
  • VPA 313: Regional Art History (4 units)
  • VPA 405: Senior Capstone (4 units) (to be taken twice in the senior year)

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • SBS 350: Domination and Resistance: US since 1880 (4 units)
  • SBS 385: Environmental History of California (3-4 units)

Students must demonstrate second language proficiency through the 201 level.Read more about the Language Proficiency Requirement.

If additional coursework is necessary to complete the required 60 units, students may select from the following recommended courses:

  • VPA 301: Beginning Painting (4 units)
  • VPA 312: Vis Arts Ed For Teachers (4 units)
  • VPA 324: Museum Conservation & Display (4 units)
  • VPA 304: Painting & Mural (6 units)
