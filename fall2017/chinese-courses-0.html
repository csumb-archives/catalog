CHIN 101: Intro Chinese Lang & Culture

Covers the basic skills of conversing, reading, and writing Mandarin Chinese. Includes conceptual structures which underlie Chinese discourse, sentence structure, and the cultural and social conventions that characterize daily use. Emphasizes Chinese sound system with attention to tone system, mastery of Pin-yin Romanization system, and Chinese characters. (Credit/NoCredit Available)

Units: 4 — 4

CHIN 102: Beg Chinese II (Mandarin)

Continues work on the four basic skills within a proficiency format. Emphasizes building lexicon in spoken and written forms, and using context and strategies to ascertain meaning without knowing the structures and lexicon of an authentic text. (Credit/NoCredit Available) (Prereq: CHIN 101: Intro Chinese Lang & Culture (4 units))

Units: 4 — 4
