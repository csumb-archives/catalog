Through online courses the M.B.A. program allows you to complete 48 units of required courses within 24 months. A fixed curriculum, graduate business cohort program, the MBA offers courses through Extended Education in Fall, Spring A, Spring B, and Summer terms. When admitted you become a member of an approximately 25-student cohort that progresses through the program in a lock-step manner.

For additional information visit mba.csumb.edu.

Required Courses

Complete all of the following courses:

  • BUS 601: Business Communications Fundamentals (3 units)
  • BUS 603: Macro Economics (3 units)
  • BUS 604: Micro Economics (3 units)
  • BUS 605: MBA Research Methods (3 units)
  • BUS 626: Marketing and Entrepreneurship (6 units)
  • BUS 631: Global Business (6 units)
  • BUS 632: Organization & Leadership (6 units)
  • BUS 652: MBA Finance (3 units)
  • BUS 661: Innovation and Technology Management (6 units)
  • BUS 681: MBA Accounting (3 units)
  • BUS 691: Business Strategy (6 units)

Learning Outcomes

  1. Understand, analyze, and evaluate business conditions by applying business knowledge in the core functional business areas.
  2. Evaluate business situations and create written business documents to achieve results.
  3. Evaluate business situations and orally present the synthesized findings.
  4. Evaluate and create business opportunities by applying critical thinking processes.
  5. Analyze and evaluate business problems using quantitative data and methods.
