International Programs ~ Home

Studying overseas can change your life. It creates understanding. It builds connections. It opens doors to the world beyond your community, state, and country. It helps turn you into a global citizen. And that's just what our world needs in this era of shrinking borders and increasing interconnectedness.

The CSUMB Office of International Programs offers a range of study-abroad and exchange programs including the impressive CSU International Programs that give you access to over 70 universities in 18 countries.

You will find all of the official information about our international programs right here. Simply use the left-side navigation. For more detailed information and resources, please visit the CSUMB.EDU/international website.
