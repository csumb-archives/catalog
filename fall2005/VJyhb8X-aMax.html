> About CSUMB

About CSU Monterey Bay

Ideally situated between the Salinas Valley and Monterey Bay, CSUMB’s 1,387-acre campus brings together promising students and accomplished faculty in the heart of a premiere California location.

Guided by a powerful Vision Statement, CSUMB has educated and served the diverse population of California and its immediate Tri-County region since being established in 1994 on part of the former Fort Ord Army base. The university particularly emphasizes access to quality higher education for traditionally underserved and low-income populations.
