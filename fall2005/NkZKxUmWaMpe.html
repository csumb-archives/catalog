Telecommunications, Multimedia, and Applied Computing, B.S.~ Recommended Preparation

High School
Recommended

As many math and computing courses as available at your school, including pre-calculus

Community College
Recommended

At least three semesters of a second language

Completion of General Education requirements

As much math as possible, including discrete math and calculus, especially if you are considering the technical specialization

Computer programming language

Computer networks

Experience with media tools

CSUMB Lower-Division
Required

CST 101 Technology Tools (or equivalent)

Recommended

Complete lower-division University Learning Requirements in consultation with a Peer Advisor in the TMAC Major

Mathematics,  especially MATH 130 Pre-Calculus
