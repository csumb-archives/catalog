Business Administration, B.S. ~ Faculty and Student Quotes

We seek to create an environment within which students can discover where and how they can make a difference in life, whether in business or the nonprofit sector. Most of our graduates can point to truly transforming experiences, usually through a combination of hands-on experience and faculty-assisted reflection and learning based on that experience. It works!
~ Dr. Sandy Hale, Professor

I feel fortunate to have been a member of the first graduating class from CSUMB where I acquired the personal and professional strength required to be a successful woman entrepreneur during a time of great global change. My experiences at CSUMB taught me that I CAN do anything.
~ Amy Perreira, Alumna and Entrepreneur

The School of Business teaches the most advanced and effective approaches to business in today's world. With excellent opportunities for paid internships in marketing, finance, and the computer industry, the experiences are unbeatable. The Business faculty and staff create a very comfortable learning environment and all the value of a small private school with a public school bottom line.
~ Henry Yim, Alumnus, Monterey, California
