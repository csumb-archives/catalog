Teledramatic Arts and Technology, B.A.~ Careers, Awards, Graduate Study

Related Careers

TAT graduates have pursued successful careers in fields such as movie and television production and postproduction, acting, directing, script writing, cinematography, editing,  radio broadcasting, theatre production, multimedia production, broadband content preparation and digital distribution, education, and other teledramatic industries.

The organizations hiring them have included CBS Television, KSBW News, Santa Cruz X Radio Station, El Teatro Campesino Theatre Company, Western Stage Theatre Company, and the International School of Monterey.

Awards

TAT graduates have received awards  from the Director's Guild of America , College Emmy Award, Mill Valley Film Festival, CSU Media Arts Festival,  CSU Student Research Award, San Diego Latino Film Festival, Cannes Film Festival Emerging Filmmaker's Program, and others.

Graduate Study

TAT graduates have attended a variety of graduate institutions including the American Film Institute (AFI), UCLA Film and Television School,  Dublin University Film Program, and CSU Hayward Media Arts.

They have continued their education in many fields of graduate and professional study including filmmaking, editing, directing, and media arts.
