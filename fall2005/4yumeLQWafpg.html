Community Participation ULR ~ Approved Courses

Courses currently approved to fulfill the Community Participation ULR are listed below along with the timeframe during which each has been approved. Successful completion of a course within the timeframe indicated fulfills the ULR.

To ensure continued alignment between courses and learning outcomes, the Community Participation learning community reevaluates course approvals regularly. Though most courses listed here will continue to be approved beyond the timeframe indicated, you should verify that a course satisfies the ULR on the  Banner student information system at the time of registration. Courses no longer satisfying the ULR, can be viewed in the archive of previously approved courses.

The listing of a course as approved does not indicate that it will be offered in any given semester. Check current offerings on the CSUMB.EDU/schedule website or check the course descriptions to see how frequently a course is offered.

ASMT 433 ~ Assessment of the COMMUNITY PARTICIPATION ULR ~ 1 credit
Approved from Fall 2000 through Spring 2007

HCOM 211S ~ Reading, Writing, and Critical Thinking ~ 6 credits
Approved from Fall 2004 through Spring 2007

HPWE 361S ~ Foundations of Wellness Through Community Participation Service Learning ~ 6 credits
Approved from Spring 2000 through Spring 2007
(first as WRSI 361S, then HWI 361S, then HPWE361S)

SL 200S ~ Introduction to Service in Multicultural Communities ~ 4 credits
Approved from Fall 1999 through Spring 2007

SL 201S ~ Introduction to Service in Multicultural Communities ~ 4 credits
Approved from Fall 2002 through Spring 2007
