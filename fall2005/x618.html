Earth Systems Science & Policy, B.S.~ Home

An innovative interdisciplinary program, the ESSP Bachelors of Science program offers education and training in science, economics, environmental policy, and spatial-data analysis.  Our goal is to prepare students for entry-level careers in a wide array of resource assessment and resource management positions.

You will find all of the official information about the Earth Systems Science & Policy major right here. Simply use the left-side navigation.

For more detailed information including a look at student work, faculty bios, research partnerships, and internship opportunities, please visit the Division of Science and Environmental Policy website.
