Telecommunications, Multimedia, and Applied Computing, B.S. ~ Overview

Purpose of the Major

The TMAC program at CSUMB offers a unique integration of technology and design, unlike other universities where students are often forced to choose between those areas. Our graduates possess a unique blend of creativity and technical understanding of computers, networks, communications, and media. Through hands-on experience you will learn about computers and their information processing design, including the design and development of software; transmission of information between machines and people; mathematical tools used to organize and interpret information; media tools and design principles for developing informative and thought-provoking aesthetic products; and new media's representation of information.
