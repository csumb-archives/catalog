College of University Studies and Programs

The mission of the College of University Studies and Programs (CUSP) is to enhance students' ability to be effective, self-directed learners and socially responsible community members.  Through the college, all CSUMB students pursue their own academic goals, shape their own development as productive members of a multicultural society, and forge an ethical sense of service to others while acquiring the knowledge of a liberal education.  The college implements the CSUMB Vision that "values service through high quality education."
