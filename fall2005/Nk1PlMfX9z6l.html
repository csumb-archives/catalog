Social and Behavioral Sciences, B.A. ~ Academic Advising and Support

The Social and Behavioral Sciences faculty is deeply committed to the teaching philosophy of mentorship. Early in the program (in SBS308), your occupational and educational aspirations will be assessed. You will select  or be assigned  an academic advisor based on shared interests and aspirations. You will consult and work with your assigned professor throughout your career with us.
