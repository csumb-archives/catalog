World Languages and Cultures, B.A. ~ Academic Advising and Support

All WLC fulltime faculty members function as advisors for WLC majors to ensure that they are on the right pathways toward completion of the general conditions, prerequisites, and MLOs required by the major. Once enrolled in the WLC 300 Major ProSeminar, you will be assigned a faculty advisor for the major.

Faculty advisors work collaboratively to support and guide you through the curriculum and toward your academic goals. They advise you on developing and creating your academic plan, and devising an Individual Learning Plan (ILP) that outlines how you will achieve the Major Learning Outcomes.
