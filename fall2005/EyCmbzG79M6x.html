Marine Science, M.S.~ Distinguishing Features

CSUMB is part of a consortium that operates Moss Landing Marine Laboratories. CSUMB's Master of Science in Marine Science is administered through MLML.

CSUMB is the closest consortium campus to MLML. The shore lab facility, marine operations, and main MLML campus are less than a half-hour's drive from CSUMB.

The MLML curriculum provides extensive field and laboratory work for advanced study.

MLML and CSUMB are ideally situated for graduate opportunities in research and advanced courses in marine sciences.
