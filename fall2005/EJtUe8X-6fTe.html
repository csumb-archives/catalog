Frequently Asked Questions ~ Feedback

We value your feedback, so we invite you to ask questions or tell us what you think about the CSUMB.EDU/catalog website, print catalog, or anything else catalog-related that comes to mind.

If you have questions, we ask that you first check our FAQ page to see if we have already provided an answer. Your taking the time to do this will ensure that we have more time to answer questions that have not already been covered . Thanks in advance for your consideration!
