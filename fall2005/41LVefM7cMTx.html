Collaborative Health and Human Services, B.A. ~ Home

If you aspire to serve your community and fellow human beings, our CHHS major will prepare you superbly. It will open the door to career success and personal fulfillment in health, social work,  public policy, public safety, nonprofit management, and more. Open that door to your future today!

You will find all of the official information about the Collaborative Health and Human Services major right here. Simply use the left-side navigation.

For more detailed information including a look at internship and fieldwork opportunities and faculty bios, please visit the Health, Human Services, and Public Policy Department website.
