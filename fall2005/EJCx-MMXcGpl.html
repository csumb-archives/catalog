Education, M.A. Curriculum and Instruction ~ Learning Environment

The rich mix of peoples and cultures in the Monterey Bay area is reflected in the populations of our region's schools. Whether you are teaching in one of those schools already or connecting with the larger community as part of your graduate studies, you will undoubtedly find appropriate groups for your collaborative research and practical teaching experiences.

You will do your on-campus learning in small, interactive classes housed in spacious, modern facilities on our technologically advanced "21st CSU campus for the 21st century." Plurality and teaching excellence have been commitments since the university's inception.
