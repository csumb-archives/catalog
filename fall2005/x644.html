Visual and Public Art, B.A. ~ Distinguishing Features

Our Visiting Artists Series allows you to learn in the company of professional artists through lectures and applied projects.

Service learning courses involve  you in planned public art such as mural painting, digital posters, site-specific art, and other projects in several local communities.

Our Digital Art Lab is one example of the advanced technology found in our studios and across our campus.

You will learn in small peer groups working with nationally known faculty in public art, murals, digital media, installation, sculpture, museum practices, community arts, and arts education.

VPA encourages and supports you in independent projects that connect self and society.
