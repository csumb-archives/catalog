Teledramatic Arts and Technology, B.A.

Everyone loves a good story, and people respect great storytellers. If you dream of being a teller of good stories—whether on stage, in film, via digital media, across the airwaves, or through another new or established medium—then our Teledramatic Arts and Technology major is perfect for you. It prepares you for any of the professional fields of art and entertainment by integrating media, technologies, and artforms into one cohesive teledramatic experience.

You will find all of the official information about the TAT major right here. Simply use the left-side navigation. For more detailed information including student websites internship opportunities, and equipment inventories, please visit the Teledramatic Arts and Technology Department website.
