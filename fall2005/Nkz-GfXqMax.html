Music, B.A. ~ Typical Pathways

Entering CSUMB as a Transfer

If you enter CSUMB as a junior transfer or choose to complete your General Education requirements (ULRs) before selecting and starting your major, the following would be a suggested pathway that you might follow to complete the requirements for this degree.

Contemporary Music Concentration

Semester 1 (15-16 credits)
HCOM 403 or CHHS 350  ~ 4 credits ~ applies to MPA MLO #4
MPA 103, Musicianship & Theory III  ~ 4 credits ~ applies to the major core courses
MPA 300, Major ProSeminar ~ 2 credits ~ applies to the major core courses
MPA 337, Music History I 500-1750  ~ 4 credits ~ applies to MPA MLO #1
Any MPA Ensemble, Performing Band or Chorus Course ~ 1-2 credits ~ applies to MPA MLO #5.

Semester 2 (12-13 credits)
MPA 315, Performance Study  ~ 1 credit ~ applies to MPA MLO#5
MPA 334, Survey of Electronic Music  ~ 2 credits ~ applies to the concentration and MPA MLO #3
MPA 338, Music History II 1750-1900  ~ 4 credits ~ applies to MPA MLO #1
MPA 342, Score Reading ~ 2 credits ~ applies to the concentration and MPA MLO #3
MPA 401, Conducting ~ 2 credits ~ applies to the concentration
Any MPA Ensemble, Performing Band or Chorus Course ~ 1-2 credits ~ applies to MPA MLO #5

Semester 3 (12-13credits)
MPA 336, Jazz History  ~ 4 credits ~ applies to the concentration and MPA MLO #1
MPA 360, Survey of World Music  ~ 4 credits ~ applies to the concentration and MPA MLO #1
MPA 415, Performance Study ~ 1 credit ~ applies to MPA MLO #5
MPA 440, Master Class ~ 0 credits ~ applies to the major core courses
MPA 475, Senior Capstone (Prep)  ~ 2 credits ~ applies to the major core courses
Any MPA Ensemble, Performing Band or Chorus Course ~ 1-2 credits ~ applies to MPA MLO#5

Semester 4 (16 credits)
MPA 306, Music & Computers  ~ 4 credits  ~ applies to MPA MLO #6
MPA 317S, Community Involvement Service Learning   ~ 4 credits ~ applies to MPA MLO #2
MPA 335, Reflections on American Music  ~ 4 credits ~ applies to the major core courses and to MPA MLO #1
MPA 345, Improvisation  ~ 2 credits ~ applies to the concentration and MPA MLO #5
MPA 440, Master Class ~ 0 credits ~ applies to the major core courses
MPA 475 , Senior Capstone  ~ 2 credits ~ applies to the major core courses
