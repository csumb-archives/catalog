Business Administration, B.S. ~ Home

Want to discover how to make a difference in business or the nonprofit sector? Looking for a major that will give you a solid foundation of business knowledge and skills plus important personal attributes that will give you a competitive advantage on the job? In our Business Administration major, you will develop leadership, entrepreneurship, and ethics that will lead to success in your career, contribution to your community, and fulfillment in your life.

You will find all of the official information about the Business Administration B.S. right here. Simply use the left-side navigation.

For more detailed information including a look at student work, faculty bios, partnerships, and school events, please visit the School of Business website.
