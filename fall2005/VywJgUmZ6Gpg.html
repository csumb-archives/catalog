> Undergraduate Education

Undergraduate Education

To complete your undergraduate education and earn a Bachelor of Arts or Bachelor of Science degree from CSUMB, you must satisfy all of the following State of California and CSUMB campus-specific requirements.

State of California Requirements
  • General Education Breadth Requirements
  • American Institutions
  • Graduation Requirement in Writing
  • Minimum Credit Requirements
  • Minimum Grade Point Average
CSUMB Campus-Specific Requirements
  • University Learning Requirements
  • Major Requirements including Major Learning Outcomes (MLOs)
  • Graduation Learning Outcomes (GLOs)
