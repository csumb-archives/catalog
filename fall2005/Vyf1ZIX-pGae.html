Human Performance and Wellness, B.S.

The human performance and wellness professions continue to rate among the fastest growing in the world as people, corporations, and government entities have come to recognize that the wellbeing of individuals creates the foundation for the wellbeing of everything else including our economy and society. By providing you with broad knowledge, skills, abilities, and attitudes, our HPW major will prepare you to pursue any of these highly demanded professions from alcohol treatment to youth sports administration.

You will find all of the official information about the Human Performance and Wellness major right here. Simply use the left-side navigation.
