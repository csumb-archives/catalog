Human Development Minor ~ Careers and Graduate Study

Graduates who have completed the Human Development minor in conjunction with a CSUMB major have entered and achieved notable success in the following careers: elementary education ~ secondary education ~ childcare ~ social work ~ guidance counseling ~ coaching ~ special education ~ recreation services ~ senior services.
