Service Learning ~ Home

Service learning is a hallmark of CSUMB's academic program. Through service learning each year, thousands of CSUMB students are involved in the community as mentors, teachers, environmental activists, policy advocates, and technology tutors. Students are making a difference in the community while developing a lifelong commitment to service and social justice.
