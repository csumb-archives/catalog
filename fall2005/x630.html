Telecommunications, Multimedia, and Applied Computing, B.S. ~ Home

The way that we communicate, conduct business, learn, and see the world will continue to change at an incredible pace due to ever-advancing technology. To keep up, employers need people who truly grasp the complexities of technology so that they can keep up as the changes occur. Our Telecommunications, Multimedia, and Applied Computing major develops that depth of knowledge. It provides broad-based understanding of current technologies and applications, and merges this knowledge with design, communication, and management skills. As a TMAC major, you will develop a customized university experience pertinent to your career ambitions and the needs
of a global, multicultural society.  

You will find all of the official information about the TMAC major right here. Simply use the left-side navigation. For more detailed information including a look at student work and faculty bios, please visit the School of Information Technology and Communications Design website.
