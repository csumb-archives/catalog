Management and Information Technology, M.S.~ Distinguishing Features

Interdisciplinary collaboration between technology and business promotes a structure of shared responsibility, mutual authority, and accountability for success in integrating diverse academic traditions.

Technology infusion integrates high-tech tools as resources to learners within in-class and field-learning experiences.

MSMIT incorporates ethical reflection and practice by focusing on the ethical dimensions of topics within the curriculum and related subjects.

MSMIT infuses multicultural and global awareness, as international and multicultural topics are examined through exercises, lectures, and discussions. MSMIT students from around the world provide valuable insight into the perspectives of different cultures on business and technology.

Evening and online courses meet the needs of students working fulltime.
