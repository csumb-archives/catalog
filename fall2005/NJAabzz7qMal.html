Human Development Minor ~ Learning Environment

You will engage in a variety of learning experiences. Direct instruction, individual assignments, and group work allow you to formulate an understanding of applied topics, and analyze and critique general theory in relation to local situations. You will experience a variety of media, practice developing professional strategies, and engage in problem solving. The rich cultural life of the diverse communities nearby extends and enriches the course curriculum.
