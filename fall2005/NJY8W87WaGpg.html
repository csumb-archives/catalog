Education, M.A. Special Education ~ Distinguishing Features

This program honors the working special education professional by providing a high quality MAE program that can be completed with 30 credits of coursework.

CSUMB values special education certification as a viable pathway for completion of a Master of Arts in Education.

The MAE with Special Education Emphasis provides you with a solid background in legal and policy issues related to advocacy in the field.

We develop your technological sophistication, including your skills in applying technology to educational environments and your abilities to critique its uses.

Coursework is framed within a pluralism paradigm, and all of the courses are taught from a multicultural perspective.
