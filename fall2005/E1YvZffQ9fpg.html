Special Education Credentials ~ Learning Environment

The learning environment at CSUMB is intimate, reflective, and participatory.

Reciprocal interactions between students and instructors create a positive, compassionate, and uplifting educational experience. Careful attention is paid to pedagogy that reflects the cultural and ethnic diversity of the students in the local schools.
