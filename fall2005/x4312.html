Vibrancy ULR ~ Approved Courses

Courses currently approved to fulfill each portion of the Vibrancy ULR are listed below along with the timeframe during which each has been approved. Successful completion of a course within the timeframe indicated fulfills the designated portion of the Vibrancy ULR.

To ensure continued alignment between courses and learning outcomes, the Vibrancy learning community reevaluates course approvals regularly. Though most courses listed here will continue to be approved beyond the timeframe indicated, you should verify that a course satisfies the ULR on the  Banner student information system at the time of registration. Courses no longer satisfying the ULR, can be viewed in the archive of previously approved courses.

The listing of a course as approved does not indicate that it will be offered in any given semester. Check current offerings on the CSUMB.EDU/schedule website or check the course descriptions to see how frequently a course is offered.

NOTE: HPWE was formerly known as HWI from Fall 2000  through Spring 2004, as WRSI from Spring 1997 through Fall 2000, and RLWSS from Fall 1995 through Fall 1996. Thus, the course prefix indicated below has varied while the number has remained consistent throughout the approved years.
