Education, M.A. Special Education ~ Home

To assume leadership roles in schools and in agencies serving students and families with disabilities, educators must possess enhanced knowledge in curriculum, pedagogy, and research. Our Master of Arts in Education with Emphasis in Special Education provides that while also fostering commitment to excellence and social justice in our schools.
