Teledramatic Arts and Technology, B.A. ~ Distinguishing Features

TAT provides interdisciplinary learning through hands-on instruction.

Service Learning courses partner with social and cultural organizations, and  local schools so you can work with community partners on real-world projects.

Professional and industry guest speakers in classes, workshops, and lecture series bring their knowledge and experience to campus.

Internships with local radio, TV organizations, media production companies, and festivals provide learning experiences and career development opportunities .

Independent study opportunities to work with faculty on their research and creative projects provide one-on-one mentoring experiences and advanced learning.
