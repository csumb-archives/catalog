Integrated Studies Special Major, B.A. ~ Distinguishing Features

In designing a learning plan that positions you for career success, you develop close working relationships with faculty members representing your academic disciplines and the ISSM program.

Your team of advisors works with you from initial advising, to the ProSeminar where you will develop your Individual Learning Plan, through to your Senior Capstone where you will publicly display your cumulative learning.

You use the whole university as your resource as you draw on varied faculty expertise, courses, and programs to create a unique and personally meaningful academic degree process.

Including the Major ProSeminar and independent studies, ISSM courses serve as the glue connecting outcomes and learning experiences that you borrow from other majors.
