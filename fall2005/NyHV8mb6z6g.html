Filing Period Duration

Each campus accepts applications until capacities are reached. Many campuses limit undergraduate admission in an enrollment category because of overall enrollment limits. If applying after the initial filing period, consult the campus admission office for current information.

If any enrollment categories are not filled during the initial filing period, CSUMB will continue to accept applications until capacity is reached. Enrollment priority within the extended period will be in the order that applications are received. Contact Admissions & Records to confirm deadlines and policies, or visit the CSUMentor.edu website for current deadlines.
