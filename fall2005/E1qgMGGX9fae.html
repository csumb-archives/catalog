Admissions ~ Provisional Freshman Admission

CSUMB may provisionally admit you as a first-time freshman applicant based on academic preparation through your junior year of high school and planned for your senior year. If so admitted, the campus will monitor to ensure that you complete your senior year of studies satisfactorily, including the required college preparatory subjects, and graduate from high school. You are required to submit an official transcript after graduation to certify that all coursework has been satisfactorily completed. The campus may rescind the admission decision if you are found not to be eligible after your final transcript has been evaluated.

 

Note: Adopted at the California State University system level and mandated for inclusion in all CSU campus catalogs , gray-colored text applies to  all 23 campuses in the system. Black text pertains uniquely to CSUMB.
