Liberal Studies, B.A. ~ Related Careers and Graduate Study

Related Careers

Liberal Studies graduates have entered and achieved notable success in elementary and middle school teaching; school administration; adult education; counseling; childcare; preschool teaching; recreation services; and social services.

The organizations hiring them have included Monterey Peninsula Unified School District; Hayward Unified School District; Hollister School District; Monterey Peninsula College; and CSUMB.

Graduate Study

Liberal Studies graduates have attended a variety of graduate institutions including Tufts; Kansas State; Bethany College; Pacific; UC Santa Barbara; UC Santa Cruz; Southeastern Louisiana; Chapman; San José State; CSU Northridge; and CSU Sacramento.

They have entered and achieved notable success in the following fields of graduate and credential study: education; professional multiple subject teacher education; special education; counseling; social work; health education; recreation; and senior services.
