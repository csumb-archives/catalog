Are you highly motivated and self-directed? Do you have a specific career in mind, but can't find exactly the right major to prepare you for it? If you have the drive and determination to create your own pathway to your promising future, ISSM will provide all of the structure and support that you need. It allows you to combine the best of all other CSUMB academic programs to customize an undergraduate education perfect for you.

Additionally, this major includes a 2nd track: Interdiscipilnary Track II, which includes Liberal Arts based classes which are already designed for you and a major advisor from Integrated Studies. Some examples of graduate school career destinations from this trach include : law, medicine,psychology, the arts and sciences,social work, history, business, theology, criminology, journalism, and many others.

You will find the official information about the Integrated Studies Special Major right here. Simply use the left-side navigation or the matching links at the bottom of this page.
