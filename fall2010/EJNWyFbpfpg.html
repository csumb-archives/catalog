Students use arithmetical, algebraic, geometric, and statistical methods to solve problems.

Students estimate and check answers to mathematical problems in order to determine reasonableness, identify alternatives, and select optimal results.

Students interpret mathematical models such as formulas, graphs, tables, and schematics, and draw inferences from them.

Students represent mathematical information symbolically, visually, numerically, and verbally.

Students recognize and describe the limits of mathematical and statistical methods.
