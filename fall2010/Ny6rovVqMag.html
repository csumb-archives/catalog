High School
Recommended
  • As many theory and harmony classes as your schedule allows
Community College
Recommended
  • Two years of musicianship
  • One year of music theory
  • Two years of ensemble experience, if you plan on a performance concentration
CSUMB Lower-Division
Required
  • You must complete an application and undergo an audition/interview prior to acceptance into the program. We conduct auditions throughout the year both on-site and via audio/videotape.
Recommended
  • Complete all lower-division University Learning Requirements.
