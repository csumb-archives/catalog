Including the ability to develop production skills to produce and present public and individual artworks, projects or exhibitions.

 

Required Courses

  • Complete three of the following
  • VPA 301: Beginning Painting (4 units)
  • VPA 303: Beginning Sculpture (4 units)
  • VPA 304: Painting & Mural (6 units)
  • VPA 305: Media Culture (4 units)
  • VPA 306S: Digital Public Art (4 units)
  • VPA 309: Materials and Methods (2 units)
  • VPA 310: Mixed Media Drawing (4 units)
  • VPA 315: Life Drawing (4 units)
  • VPA 316: Photography as Art (2 units)
  • VPA 316L: Photography as Art Lab (2 units)
  • VPA 326: Photography as Art II:Alternat (2 units)
  • VPA 326L: Photography as Art II:Alternat (2 units)
  • VPA 330: Tile and Mosaic in Public Art (4 units)
  • VPA 401: Installation & Performance Art (4 units)
  • VPA 403: Sculpture and 3D Design (6 units)
