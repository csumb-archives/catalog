Biography

Dr. Drew Waters received his Masters of Music in Jazz and Contemporary Media from the Eastman School of Music and his Ph.D. in Performance and Composition from New York University. With his extensive experience as a bassist, performer, composer, recording engineer and educator, Drew is excited to be a part of a school with such a bright future.
