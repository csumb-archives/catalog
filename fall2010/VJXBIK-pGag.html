If you have a background in music and want to increase your knowledge and skills though majoring in another field, then the Music Minor will serve you well. The curriculum covers instrumental, vocal, theory, aural perception, and music analysis. Whether you hope to make music part of your career or just a part of your life, this minor will strike a chord with you.

You will find all of the official information about the minor right here. Simply use the left-side navigation. Please visit the Music and Performing Arts Department website for more detailed information including a look at student work, faculty bios, and MPA events.
