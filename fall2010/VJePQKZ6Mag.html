Prerequisites for Admission
  1. CSUMB application on file in CSUMB Admissions & Records with official sets of all transcripts
  2. Multiple Subject Teacher Credential Program Application on file in Credentials Office with official sets of all transcripts
  3. Two or three letters of recommendation from persons who have seen you teaching or working with culturally diverse children

    Letters should be mailed directly to:

    Multiple Subject Teacher Program

    CSU Monterey Bay

    100 Campus Center, Building 3

    Seaside, CA 93955
  4. CBEST taken

    Attach original test results or copy of Emergency Permit.

    For test registration deadlines and procedures, visit the cbest.nesinc.com website.
  5. CSET passed, all 3 subtests
    Attach original test results.

    For test registration deadlines and procedures, visit the cset.nesinc.com website.
  6. Coursework or life experience providing knowledge of people with cultural or linguistic background different from the applicant's (see FAQ's sheet)
  7. Fulfilled U.S. Constitution requirement
  8. Fulfilled foreign language requirement
