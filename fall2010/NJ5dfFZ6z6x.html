Demonstrate the ability to initiate and maintain a flow of information between sender(s) and receiver(s) through the use of critical listening, and oral and written communication skills; communicate basic and complex ideas to a variety of groups; appreciate the organizational and cultural contexts within which communication occurs; and conduct professional quality oral and electronic presentations.

Required Courses

  • Complete all of the following
  • CHHS 300: Major ProSeminar (4 units)
  • CHHS 400A: Senior Capstone-A (2 units)
  • CHHS 400B: Senior Capstone-B (2 units)
