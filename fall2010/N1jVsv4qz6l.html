Requirement View

Because we have built our academic programs on an model, that fullfills the American Psychological Association recommendations for quality undergraduate programs, your  Individual Learning Plan will achieve your personal target outcomes while also satisfying University Learning Requirements, Graduation Learning Outcomes, and Major Learning Outcomes. Given that, you should consider the following pathways only as possibilities.

These pathways serve as tools, not prescribed programs of study. Before enrolling, you can use them to get a feel for what you can do in this program. After enrolling, you and your advisors can use them as a starting point for building your personalized your ILP.
