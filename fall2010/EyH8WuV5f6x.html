In this digital age, no matter your career path, your employment options will likely increase if you have web, instructional, animation, publication, or multimedia design skills. Even if you can't use the skills at work, they will serve you well in your personal pursuits. The Communication Design Minor lets you pursue your particular design interests as an added dimension of your academic preparation.

You will find all of the official information about the minor right here. Simply use the left-side navigation. For more detailed information including a look at student work and faculty bios, please visit the School of Information Technology and Communication Design website.
