Student Activities enhances the collegiate experience for CSUMB students through co-curricular programming. We provide students with the opportunity to apply what they learn in the classroom to programs and events presented campus-wide throughout the academic year. We encourage students to participate in programs and events in order to enhance their personal, social and academic growth. The department's professional staff provides a healthy balance of challenge and support in order for the student body to get the most out of their experience at CSUMB. 

Student Activities presents and supports a wide array of student-centered programming at CSUMB. Get involved in everything from Otter Days, leadership conferences, student organizations, cultural enrichment programs, Multicultural Greek Council, Associated Students and much, much more!
