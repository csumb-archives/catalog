The following is a typical three-year pathway that satisfies requirements for MSW degree completion.

Foundation Year, Fall: Semester 1 (8 units) 

SW 500: Master Seminar  (Hybrid)
Applies to SW LO-11

SW 510: Foundations of SW Profession
Applies to SW LO-1, SW LO-2, SW LO-5, SW LO-8 & SW LO-9

SW 540A: Cross-Cultural HBSE I
Applies to SW LO-4 & SW LO-10

Foundation Year, Spring: Semester 2 (9 units)

SW 540B: Cross-Cultural HBSE II
Applies to SW LO-4 & SW LO-7

SW 550: Social Work Research
Applies to SW LO-3 & SW LO-6

SW 570: Social Policy and Analysis
Applies to SW LO-2 & SW LO-8

Second Year, Fall: Semester 3 (9 units)

SW 520: SW GPI: Micro & Mezzo
Applies to SW LO-2, SW LO-3, SW LO-4 & SW LO-10

SW 560: Adv Res Methd: Needs Assess
Applies to SW LO-6, SW LO-9 & SW LO-11

SW 596A: GP Field Practicum & Sem I
Applies to SW LO-1, SW LO-9, SW LO-10 & SW LO-11

  
Second Year, Spring: Semester 4 (12 units)  

SW 530: SW GPII: Macro
Applies to SW LO-2, SW LO-3, SW LO-4, SW LO-5, SW LO-8, SW LO-9 & SW LO-10

SW 596B: GP Field Practicum & Sem II
Applies to SW LO-1, SW LO-8, SW LO-10 & SW LO-11 

SW 610A: Intro to BH Policy & Svcs or SW 610B: Intro to CYF Policy & Svcs
Applies to SW LO-2, SW LO-4, SW LO-5, SW LO-7, SW LO-8 & SW LO-12 

SW 640: Adv Conflict Resolution
Applies to SW LO -9, SW LO-10 & SW LO-12
 

Summer (Optional): Depending on student need (3 units)

SW 650: Collaboration/Ethics/Ldrship
Applies to SW LO-2, SW LO-8, SW LO-9, SW LO-11 & SW LO-12

SW 660 to SW 668 
  
Third Year, Fall: Semester 5 (11 units)  

SW 600A: Appl SW Research Prj I
Applies to SW LO-3, SW LO-5, SW LO-6 & SW LO-10 

OR

SW 699
Applies to SW LO-3, SW LO-6 & SW LO-11

SW 620A: Adv Clinical Prac I: BH or SW 620B: Adv Clinical Prac I: CYF 
Applies to SW LO-2, SW LO-4, SW LO-5, SW LO-7 & SW LO-10 

SW 650: Collaboration/Ethics/Ldrship (Hybrid) if not taken in summer

SW 696A: AP Field Practicum & Sem I (Monthly)
Applies to SW LO-1 & SW LO-10 

Third Year, Spring: Semester 6 (11 units)

SW 600B: Appl SW Research Prj II
Applies to SW LO-3, SW LO-5, SW LO-6, SW LO-10 & SW LO-11

OR

SW 699
Applies to SW LO-3, SW LO-6 & SW LO-11

SW 630A: Adv Clinical Prac II: BH or SW 630B: Adv Clinical Prac II: CYF  
Applies to SW LO-2 & SW LO-4

SW 660 to 668 (if not taken in summer)

SW 696B: AP Field Practicum & Sem II (Monthly)
Applies to SW LO-1 & SW LO-10
