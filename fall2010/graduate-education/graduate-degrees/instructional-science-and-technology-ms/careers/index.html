Related Careers

Master of Science in Instructional Science and Technology (MIST) graduates are uniquely qualified to enter fields such as e-learning development, corporate training, educational technology, instructional design and other related fields. A valuable instructional technology skill set prepares program graduates to teach at all levels.

Doctoral Study

The MIST program provides a strong background for those graduates who would like to continue their study at the doctorate level.  MIST graduates find abundant opportunities to enter notable Ed.D. and Ph.D. programs.
