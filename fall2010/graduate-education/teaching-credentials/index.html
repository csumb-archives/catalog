CSUMB offers academic programs leading to the Multiple Subject Teaching Credential with ELA/BCLAD, the Single Subject Teaching Credential with ELA/BCLAD, and the Education Specialist Credential in Mild/Moderate or Moderate/Severe Level I and Level II with English Learner Authorization (ELA). This section of the catalog provides specific information regarding admission and curriculum requirements for these programs.

Teacher education programs are subject to changes made by the California Commission on Teacher Credentialing and California Legislature. For the most current information please contact:
