If you plan to become a coach or sports administrator at any level, either as your career or as an avocation, the Sport Management minor helps you prepare. This minor covers multiple aspects of sport including ethics, leadership, coaching, law, risk management, event management, training and fitness, team building, competitive preparation, licensing and marketing. Coupled with any major, this minor is a winner!

You will find the official information about the minor right here. Simply use the left-side navigation.

For additional information please visit the Kinesiology Department website.
