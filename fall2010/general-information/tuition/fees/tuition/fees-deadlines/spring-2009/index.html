Payment Deadlines

January 7, 2009 ~ Last day to pay spring 2009 registration fees. Non-payment of fees will result in disenrollment.

January 8, 2009 ~ Starting today, payment or a Financial Aid fee deferment is required prior to registration. This includes students who's enrollment was cancelled.

April 1, 2009 ~ Last day of the spring 2009 fee deferment for financial aid students ~ Last day to have fees paid without a $20 administrative late fee and/or a $20 late housing fee ~ Last day to have fees paid without a Business & Finance hold placed on account

Refund Deadlines

January 16, 2009 ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses and receive a full refund of mandatory fees

February 2, 2009 ~ Last day to drop from full-time to part-time and receive a full refund of the state fee difference (non-resident students also receive full refund of tuition)

February 12, 2009 ~ Last day for Open University students to drop and receive a prorated refund

March 27, 2009 ~ Last day to officially withdraw or cancel all courses and receive a prorated refund of mandatory fees

Registration Deadline

January 16, 2009 ~ Last day to register without being assessed a $25 Late Registration Fee

Installment Payment Plan

First Installment due with signed contract. Installments are due on or before the listed due date. $20 Administrative Late Fee is assessed for each late payment made.

February 10, 2009 ~ 2nd Installment Due

March 10, 2009 ~ 3rd Installment Due

Financial Holds

Business and Finance holds (BF) are placed on accounts with balances. For financial aid fee deferred students, a BF hold will be placed on your account following the deferment deadline.
