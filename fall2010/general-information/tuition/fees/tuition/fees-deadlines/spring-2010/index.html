Payment Deadlines

January 8, 2010 ~ Last day to pay registration fees, otherwise your classes will be dropped if you have not completed the FAFSA

January 11, 2010 ~ Starting today, payment or a Financial Aid fee deferment is required prior to registration

February 5, 2010 ~ Last day of Spring 2010 fee deferment for financial aid students ~ Last day to have fees paid without a $20 administrative late fee and/or a $20 late housing fee ~ Last day to have all fees paid without a financial hold placed on your account

Refund Deadlines

January 22, 2010 ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses and receive a full refund of mandatory fees

February 5, 2010 ~ Last day to drop from fulltime to part-time and receive a full refund of the State University Fee difference (nonresident students also receive full refund of tuition)

February 12, 2010 ~ Last day for Open University students to drop and receive a prorated refund

April 9, 2010 ~ Last day to officially withdraw or cancel all courses and receive a prorated refund of mandatory fees

Registration Fees

January 22, 2010 ~ Last day to register without being assessed a $25 late registration fee

Installment Payment Plan Deadlines

See the list on the Campus Service Center website.

Financial Holds

Business and Finance (BF) holds are placed on accounts with balances. For financial aid fee deferred students, a BF hold will be placed on your account following the deferment deadline.
