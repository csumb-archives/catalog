TAG 101: Begin Tagalog I Assessment

Description
Alternative assessment for the first semester of Tagalog. Freshmen and sophomore transfers attempting to satisfy the Language ULR through assessment must enroll in TAG 101, 102, 201, and 202 concurrently, except for international students who may enroll only in TAG 202. Junior and senior transfers attempting to satisfy the Language ULR through assessment, must enroll in TAG 101, 102, and 201, except for international students who may enroll only in TAG 201 or 202.

4
