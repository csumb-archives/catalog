You can improve your skills as a writer, speaker, communicator, advocate, and critical thinker through the Writing and Rhetoric Minor. Considering that virtually every professional job description now calls for one or more of these skills, it makes immense sense to couple this program with any major. These skills will also serve you well in your community activities and personal life.

You will find all of the official information about the minor right here. Simply use the left-side navigation. For more detailed information including a look at student work, faculty bios, and events, please visit the
Division of Humanities and Communication website.
