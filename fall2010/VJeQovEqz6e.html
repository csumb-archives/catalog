Our Social and Behavioral Sciences major lets you explore innovative and alternative solutions to human problems using social-science and critical-thinking skills. In the process, you will learn how to contribute to your community and effectively and ethically advocate, while developing valuable skills that will make you highly desirable to employers and graduate schools.

As we like to say around here...

It's all about the outcomes!

You will find the official information about the Social and Behavioral Sciences B.A. right here. Simply use the left-side navigation or the matching links at the bottom of this page.

For more detailed information including a look at student work, faculty bios, projects, and events, please visit the Social and Behavioral Sciences Department website.
