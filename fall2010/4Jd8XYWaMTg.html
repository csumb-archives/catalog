Fulltime: Traditional

In the traditional multiple subjects credential program, you can earn your credential in two consecutive fulltime semesters if all academic and experiential prerequisites have been completed. The following would be a typical pathway that you might follow to complete the requirements as a fulltime student.

Semester/Stage I: 18 credits

ED 374: PE for Elem Sch Child Physical education for the elementary school child (1 credit)

ED 538: Intro to Classrm Tech Level 1 Technology in the classroom, Level I (2 credits)

ED 601: Stage I Student Tchg & Sem MS Stage I student teaching and seminar (3 credits)

ED 609: Math Methods Elementary math methods (3 credits)

ED 611: Teaching and Learning Teaching and learning (3 credits)

ED 612: Pedagogy for Ling Diversity Pedagogy for linguistically and culturally diverse students (3 credits)

ED 616: Lang/Literacy Curriculum I Language and literacy development across the curriculum I (3 credits)

Semester/Stage II: 18 credits

ED 603: Stage II Student Tchg & Sem MS Stage II student teaching and seminar (6 credits

ED 608: Science Meth K-8 Classroom Science and health methods for K-8 classrooms (3 credits)

ED 615: Social Studies C and I K8 Social studies and visual & performing arts methods for K-8 classrooms (3 credits)

ED 617: Lang/Literacy Curriculum II Language and literacy development across the curriculum II (3 credits

SPED 560: Inclusionary Practices Inclusionary practices for students with special needs (3 credits)

Part-Time: CTIP

The CTIP program takes four continuous school terms (including summers) if all academic and experiential prerequisites have been completed. You may need more time.

The following would be a typical pathway that you might follow to complete the requirements through the internship program.

First Summer: 6 credits

ED 575: Teacher's Academy Teachers' academy (3 credits)

ED 611: Teaching and Learning Teaching and learning (3 credits)

Fall Semester: 10 credits

ED 374: PE for Elem Sch Child Physical education for the elementary school child (1 credit)

ED 601: Stage I Student Tchg & Sem MS Stage I student teaching and seminar (3 credits)

ED 612: Pedagogy for Ling Diversity Pedagogy for linguistically and culturally diverse students (3 credits)

ED 616: Lang/Literacy Curriculum I Language and literacy development across the curriculum II (3 credits)

Spring Semester: 12 credits

ED 603: Stage II Student Tchg & Sem MS Stage II student teaching and seminar (6 units)

ED 615: Social Studies C and I K8 Social studies and visual & performing arts methods for K-8 classrooms (3 credits)

ED 617: Lang/Literacy Curriculum II Language and literacy development across the curriculum II (3 units)

Second Summer: 9 credits

ED 608: Science Meth K-8 Classroom Science and health methods for K-8 classrooms (3 credits)

ED 609: Math Methods Elementary math methods (3 credits)

SPED 560: Inclusionary Practices Inclusionary practices for students with special needs (3 credits)
