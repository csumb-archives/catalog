Recognizing recreation as an important part of college education, we offer intramural sports that provide the opportunity to exercise, reduce stress, socialize with friends, meet new people, and experience the thrill of competition in a fun, relaxed setting. All CSUMB students, faculty and staff members, and their spouses can participate in the variety of sports offered for both skilled players and beginners.
