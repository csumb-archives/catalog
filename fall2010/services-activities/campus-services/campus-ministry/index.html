Located in the PGCC, the Campus Ministry Office supports religious organizations and ministries which represent and serve students, faculty, and staff in providing religious activities. Respect for all faiths and spiritual traditions is strongly encouraged. Current campus ministries include: Lutheran, Episcopal and Catholic.
