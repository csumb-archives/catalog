Kinesiology students craft their academic programs and career development strategies with guidance from faculty, staff and seasoned mentors from the local community.

Kinesiology Concentration advisors meet with prospective and current students throughout the year on an as needed basis. Please either email the Kinesiology Department or your advisor directly to set up an appointment.

Take advantage of universitywide services relating to health and wellness, in addition to the services our department can offer.

  • Academic Skills Achievement Program (ASAP)
  • Migrant Student Support Services (MSSS)
  • Office of International Programs (OIP)
  • Student Disability Resources (SDR)
  • Undergraduate Advising
  • Personal Growth and Counseling Center (PGCC)
    • for students who want and/or need assistance with anything from personal issues to optimizing decision-making, from problem-solving and conflict resolution to stress management.
