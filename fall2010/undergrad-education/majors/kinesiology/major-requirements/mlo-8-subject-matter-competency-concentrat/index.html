Complete up to 30 credits within one of the four KIN concentrations (Wellness; Exercise Science; Human Movement; Sport; Outdoor Education & Recreation). This coursework is typically completed simultaneously with the Core Curriculum during your junior and senior year. Contact your Kinesiology concentration advisor for specific course information.

Required Courses

  • Wellness Concentration
  • KIN 366: Behavior Change/Sup Grp Strat (3 to 4 units)
  • and four of the following
  • KIN 362: Fundamentals of Nutrition (3 units)
  • KIN 363: Human Sexuality (3 units)
  • KIN 363L: Human Sexuality Lab (1 units)
  • KIN 364: Ancestral Well-being & Healing (3 units)
  • KIN 365: Models & Dimension of Wellness (4 units)
  • KIN 367: Consumer Advocacy & Well-being (3 units)
  • KIN 368: Holistic Health & Wellness (3 units)
  • KIN 461: Effectively Manage Stress (3 units)
  • KIN 461L: Effectively Manage Stress Lab (1 units)
  • KIN 464: Pattern & Dynamic in Relations (3 units)
  • KIN 465: Sp Top Intern: Health & Well (4 units)
  • CHHS 360: Global Women's Health Issues (4 units)
  • HDEV 351: Lifespan Dev: Transit/Chng (3 units)
  • PSY 310: Biological Psychology (3 units)
  • PSY 329: Health Psychology (3 units)
  • Exercise Science Concentration
  • KIN 310: Athletic Strength & Condition (3 units)
  • KIN 340: Personal Training (3 units)
  • KIN 421L: Essential of Exercise Sci Lab (3 units)
  • KIN 430: Phys & Biomech Aerobic Exer (3 units)
  • KIN 440: Phys & Biomech Anaerobic Exer (3 units)
  • KIN 450: Exer Prescript for Special Pop (3 units)
  • KIN 469: Sp Top Intern: Exercise Sci (3 to 4 units)
  • Human Movement Concentration
  • KIN 372: Socio Persp Sport w/ Dis Emph (4 units)
  • KIN 374: Phys Ed for Elem Sch Child (2 to 4 units)
  • KIN 374L: Phys Ed for Elem Sch Child Lab (1 units)
  • KIN 375: Motor Behavior (4 units)
  • KIN 377: Neurological Impairment (4 units)
  • KIN 466: Sp Top Intern: Human Movement (3 to 4 units)
  • Sport Concentration
  • KIN 335: Fundamentals of Coaching (3 units)
  • KIN 345: Sports Ethics (4 units)
  • AND four of the following:
  • KIN 310: Athletic Strength & Condition (3 units)
  • KIN 380: Modern Sports Administration (3 units)
  • KIN 381: Legal Issues & Sport Liability (3 units)
  • KIN 410: Care/Prevent of Athlet Injury (3 units)
  • KIN 468: Sp Top Intern: Spt Man & Coach (3 to 4 units)
  • Take three different Kinesiology activity courses of your choice.
