Related Careers

Liberal Studies graduates have entered and achieved notable success in elementary and middle school teaching; speech and language pathology; school administration; adult education; counseling; childcare; preschool teaching; recreation services; and social services.

The organizations hiring them have included Monterey Peninsula Unified School District; Hayward Unified School District; Carmel Unified School District; Monterey Bay Charter School; Salinas Union School District; King City Union Elementary School District; Soledad Unified Elementary School District; Manpower Services; Pajaro Valley Unified School District; Boys and Girls Club; City of Monterey; City of Santa Cruz Parks & Recreation; City of Soledad Recreation Services; International School of Monterey; Defense Language Institute; Department of Defense; Hollister School District; Monterey Peninsula College; Hartnell College; and CSUMB.

Graduate Study

Liberal Studies graduates have attended a variety of graduate institutions including CSU Monterey Bay; Tufts; Kansas State; Bethany College; UC Santa Barbara; UC Berkeley; UC Santa Cruz; Southeastern Louisiana; Chapman; San José State; University of Portland; CSU Northridge; and CSU Sacramento.

They have entered and achieved notable success in the following fields of graduate and credential study: education; professional multiple subject teacher education; single subject teacher education; speech and language pathology; special education; counseling; social work; health education; recreation; and senior services.
