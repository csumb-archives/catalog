Visual and Public Art provides each entering major with an Advising Guide that contains complete and highly detailed information about the major and completion of the degree. The Individual Learning Plan created in VPA 300: VPA Major ProSeminar ProSeminar serves as your guide until degree completion.

In the Major ProSeminar, you will prepare your Individual Learning Plan (ILP). You are then assigned a faculty advisor according to your chosen concentration. You meet with an advisor every semester to track progress.

VPA students also help each other through peer mentoring.
