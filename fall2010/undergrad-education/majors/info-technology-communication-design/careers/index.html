Related Careers

ITCD graduates excel in careers utilizing their knowledge and skills in technology, design, communication, collaboration and problem-solving. Our graduates have entered careers and achieved notable success in web design, web development, print design, animation, programming, system administration, network management and security, instructional design, instructional technology, teaching and more.

The organizations hiring them have included Apple, Cisco Systems, Defense Manpower Data Center, SUN, Electronic Games, The Salinas Californian, USA Today, CSUMB and school districts. Some graduates have started their own businesses or work as independent consultants.

Graduate Study

ITCD graduates have attended a variety of graduate institutions including Naval Postgraduate School, UC Santa Cruz and CSUMB.

They have entered and achieved notable success in the various fields of graduate study including management and information technology, computer science, information security and instructional design.
