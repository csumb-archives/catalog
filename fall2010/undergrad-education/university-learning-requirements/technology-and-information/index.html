The Technology and Information ULR requires students to demonstrate comfort with technology and information research and critical thinking skills; and to effectively, creatively, and ethically use tools for the discovery, acquisition, evaluation, manipulation, and presentation of information.

Courses currently approved to fulfill this ULR are listed below.  Links to the ULR's outcomes, assessement and an archived list of courses no longer satisfying the ULR can be found below or in the left-side navigation column.
