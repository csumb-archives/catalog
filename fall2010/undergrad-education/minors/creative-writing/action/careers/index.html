Related Careers

Graduates who complete the Creative Writing and Social Action minor in conjunction with a CSUMB major will likely achieve notable success in the following careers: poet, writer, teacher, publisher, editor, copywriter, computer text designer, freelance writer, science writer, health writer, songwriter and visual/textual artist.
