Biology is one of the most versatile feilds of study you can pursue. The biology minor enhances any major and opens the door to manyof opportunities for graduate study and potential careers. You'll gain a fundamental understanding of core biology concepts and apply that knowledge to electives that fit your academic and career goals.

You will find all of the official information about the minor right here. Simply use the left-side navigation. For more detailed information including a look at related student work, faculty bios, partnerships, and school events, please visit the Division of Science and Environmental Policy website.
