The Teledramatic Arts and Technology minor prepares you for careers or further study in film, television, radio, and interactive media. With this minor, you'll be ready to excel in the global media environment as a professional, artist, and conscientious agent of social change.

TAT provides a highly collaborative, interdisciplinary, production-based environment.
