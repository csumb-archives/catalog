Graduates who complete the Social Work Minor in conjunction with a CSUMB major will likely enter and achieve notable success in social work, criminal justice, case management, client advocacy, or counseling.

The organizations hiring them may include public sector social service agencies, nonprofit social service agencies, county probation departments, and state parole agencies.
