If you're interested in an applied-research career such as classroom teaching, business, human services, social work, gerontology, probation and law enforcement, or health, the Sociology Minor will provide you with a deeper understanding of people and their interactions. It will also provide insights that help you in your other life endeavors including raising your children and interacting in your community.

You will find all of the official information about the minor right here. Simply use the left-side navigation. For more detailed information including a look at student work, faculty bios, projects, and events, please visit the Social and Behavioral Sciences Department website.
