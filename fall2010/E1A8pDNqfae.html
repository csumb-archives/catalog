This degree opens doors to hundreds of exciting careers in a wide variety of biology-related fields. An awesome group of faculty, an incredible location, great facilities, the latest technologies, small class sizes, and a supportive learning environment come together to offer you a first-rate biology education with concentrations in:

  • General Biology
  • Pre-Medical
  • Teacher Preparation

To view official catalog information about the Biology major, simply use the left-side navigation. For additional information about the major, including internships and undergraduate research opportunities, as well as information about other science degrees offered at CSUMB, please visit the Division of Science and Environmental Policy website.
