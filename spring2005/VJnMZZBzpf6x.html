Public Policy, Interdisciplinary M.A.~ Home

Want to improve government quality and responsiveness? Looking to optimize individual, family, and community health and wellbeing? Hoping to work for social justice? Dreaming of learning from someone who has succeeded in the deepest trenches of public policymaking?

Our Masters in Public Policy, offered in collaboration with the Panetta Institute, will set you up to do all of this and more. You will have direct access to the Honorable Leon Panetta, former White House Chief of Staff, and you will benefit from the insights gained from his long and well-respected policymaking career. Whether working in the public, nonprofit, or private sector, MPP will empower you to do more as a professional.

You will find all of the official information about the Public Policy, M.A. right here. Simply use the left-side navigation. For more detailed information including application tips, internship information, and a downloadable brochure, please visit the Health, Human Services, and Public Policy Department website.
