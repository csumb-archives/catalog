Creative and Artistic Expression ULR ~ Approved Courses

Courses currently approved to fulfill the Creative and Artistic Expression ULR are listed below along with the timeframe during which each has been approved. Successful completion of a course within the timeframe indicated fulfills the ULR.

To ensure continued alignment between courses and learning outcomes, the Creative and Artistic Expression learning community reevaluates course approvals regularly. Though most courses listed here will continue to be approved beyond the timeframe indicated, you should verify that a course satisfies the ULR on the  Banner student information system at the time of registration. Courses no longer satisfying the ULR, can be viewed in the archive  of previously approved courses.

The listing of a course as approved does not indicate that it will be offered in any given semester. Check current offerings on the CSUMB.EDU/schedule web site or check the course descriptions to see how frequently a course is offered.

NOTE: Some courses listed here are less than four credits. In order to satisfy this ULR, you must complete four credits of approved coursework.
