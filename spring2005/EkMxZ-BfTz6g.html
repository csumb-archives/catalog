Coastal and Watershed Science and Policy, M.S. ~ Academic Advising and Support Services

Once you are accepted into the M.S. in Coastal and Watershed Science and Policy, you will work closely with a faculty advisor to plan your courses and research. Prospective students may contact the graduate secretary with  initial advising questions:

    Seaside CA 93955
