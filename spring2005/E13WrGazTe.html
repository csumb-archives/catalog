Service Learning Minor ~ Home

Already a national leader as the first public university to require service learning as part of its General Education program, CSUMB became the first university in the country to offer an academic program in service learning leadership with the implementation of the Service Learning Leadership Minor.

The program generates new leaders to work in schools, nonprofits, public agencies, and volunteer organizations as community educators and social-change agents.

Want to make a difference?
Add this minor to your academic plans!

You will find all of the official information about the minor right here. Simply use the left-side navigation. For more detailed information including a look at service learning principles, faculty, and community partners, please visit the Service Learning Institute website.
