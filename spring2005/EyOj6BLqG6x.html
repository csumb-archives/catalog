English Communication ULR ~ Approved Courses

Courses currently approved to fulfill the two levels of the English Communication ULR are listed below along with the timeframe during which each has been approved. Successful completion of a course within the timeframe indicated fulfills the requirement.

To ensure continued alignment between courses and learning outcomes, the English Communication learning community reevaluates course approvals regularly. Though most courses listed here will continue to be approved beyond the timeframe indicated, you should verify that a course satisfies part of the ULR on the  Banner student information system at the time of registration. Courses no longer satisfying part of the ULR, can be viewed in the archive of previously approved courses.

The listing of a course as approved does not indicate that it will be offered in any given semester. Check current offerings on the CSUMB.EDU/schedule website or check the course descriptions to see how frequently a course is offered.

Courses fulfilling the Graduation Writing Assessment Requirement, formerly listed here, are no longer considered part of the English Communication ULR.
