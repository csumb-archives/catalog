Telecommunications, Multimedia, and Applied Computing, B.S.~ Academic Advising and Support

ITCD maintains a Student Services program to help you resolve issues and to direct you to available resources. Our Student Services Coordinator and our Peer Advisor will answer your questions regarding the major, and help you develop an Individual Learning Plan that meets all graduation requirements in the most efficient manner.

You will also be assigned a Faculty Advisor to provide specific advice relative to your specialization in the major.

You will find more information on the ITCD Website.
