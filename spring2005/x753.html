Education, M.A. Special Education ~ Overview

The Master of Arts in Education with Special Education Emphasis prepares strong professionals who have an informed understanding of what constitutes true learning; the knowledge and skills to promote such learning; and the disposition to actively work toward change when policies or practices negatively impact the learning, living, or growth of students with special needs. Students and faculty explore, analyze, and discuss the latest trends and theories in the field of special education in a scholarly environment that honors the free exchange of ideas, thoughts, and insights.

This program is designed for teachers who want to become strong professionals who actively work for excellence and justice in schools. More specifically, the coursework and educational experiences are intentionally structured to help certified teachers become outstanding and effective teachers of the culturally and linguistically diverse students in the schools of this region.

With enhanced knowledge in curriculum, pedagogy, and research, you will be better prepared to assume leadership roles in schools and in agencies serving students and families with disabilities.
