Liberal Studies, B.A. ~ Distinguishing Features

Field-based service learning prepares you for a future career through hands-on service and learning from diverse educational settings, community partners, and social service institutions.

Our Liberal Studies major is a state-approved Elementary Subject Matter Competency teacher preparation program.

Our multidisciplinary and multicultural program focuses on serving the local community and promoting educational opportunities for underrepresented populations.

Broad faculty expertise will prepare you in a wide range of areas related to education.
