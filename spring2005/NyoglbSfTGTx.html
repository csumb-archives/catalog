> Academic Skills Achievement Program

Academic Skills Achievement Program

The Academic Skills Achievement Program (ASAP), open to all CSUMB students, offers peer tutoring services and tutorial workshops to complement coursework in the areas of writing, mathematics, computer technology, science, and languages. You can access these services via ASAP Tutorial Workshops (see the ASAP course descriptions), appointments, study groups, study halls, and drop-in tutoring. ASAP facilities include a computer lab and areas for group and one-on-one study sessions.

ASAP employs student-to-student, collaborative, and active learning experiences in its promotion of academic excellence. By offering you the opportunity to work with academically successful peers, ASAP expands the learning approaches that CSUMB offers. ASAP also employs College Reading and Learning Association (CRLA) certified tutors.
