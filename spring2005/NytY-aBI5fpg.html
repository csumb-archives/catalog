Multiple Subject Credential ~ Learning Environment

The populations of area schools reflect the rich mix of peoples and cultures in the Monterey Bay region. Thus, the many area elementary schools offer superb settings for you to work with children who are diverse in every imaginable way. Meanwhile, you will do your on-campus learning in interactive classes housed in modern facilities on our technologically advanced "21st CSU campus for the 21st century", where plurality and teaching excellence have been commitments since the university's inception.
