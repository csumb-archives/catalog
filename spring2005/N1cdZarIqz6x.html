International Programs ~ Admission

To qualify for admission to the International Programs, you must have upper-division or graduate standing at a CSU campus by the time of departure. Students at the sophomore level may, however, participate in the intensive language-acquisition programs in France, Germany, and Mexico.

You must also possess a current cumulative grade point average of 2.75 or 3.0, depending on the program for which you apply. Some programs also have language study or other coursework prerequisites.

California Community Colleges transfer students are eligible to apply directly from their community colleges.

 

Note: Adopted at the California State University system level and mandated for inclusion in all CSU campus catalogs , gray-colored text applies to  all 23 campuses in the system. Black text pertains uniquely to CSUMB.
