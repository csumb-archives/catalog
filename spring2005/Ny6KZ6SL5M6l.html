Multiple Subject Credential ~ Careers and Graduate Study

Related Careers

Students earning their Multiple Subject Credential at CSUMB have entered and achieved notable success in elementary and middle schools across the state. They have been particularly well prepared to work with the linguistically and culturally diverse student populations in the Central California region.

Graduate Study

Many students earning their Multiple Subject Credential at CSUMB have attended a variety of graduate institutions, including CSUMB, for advanced study. Our Master of Arts in Education program engages practicing teachers in action research projects in their own schools and focused on issues surrouding social justice. There also is a special education pathway in the MAE program.
