Marine Science, M.S. ~ Home

Whether you want to prepare for a career as a marine specialist, scientist, or teacher, you can't beat the combination of CSUMB and Moss Landing Marine Labs. With CSUMB nestled among the dunes at the head of the Monterey Bay and MLML less than a half-hour away right on the shoreline, there is no better place in the world to study the marine sciences! The bay itself has been designated a marine sanctuary. The Monterey Submarine Canyon within offers 1000-meter depths. Within minutes of MLML, Elkhorn Slough remains a relatively unspoiled salt marsh. Sand dunes, rocky intertidal environments, and subtidal kelp forests provide further valuable areas for scientific study.

You will find all of the official information about the Marine Science, M.S. offered by CSUMB in conjunction with MLML right here. Simply use the left-side navigation.

For more detailed information including a look at student work, faculty bios, research partnerships, and internship opportunities, please visit the Division of Science and Environmental Policy website and the Moss Landing Marine Labs website.
