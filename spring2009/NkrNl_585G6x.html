Do you offer a program in middle school education?

If you want to pursue a career in middle school education, we offer the following pathways:

  • Education M.A.
  • Instructional Science & Technology M.S.
  • Liberal Studies B.A. Teaching Pathway
  • Multiple Subject Credential
  • Single Subject Credential

Some pathways may require additional coursework outside the major. Talk to your advisor to find out how best to meet your personal, academic and career goals.
