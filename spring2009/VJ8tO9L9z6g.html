Do you offer biology?

If you want to pursue a career in biology, we offer the following pathway:

  • Biology B.S.
    • General Biology Concentration
    • Pre-Medical Concentration
    • Teacher Preparation Concentration
  • Environmental Science, Technology & Policy B.S.
    • Marine & Coastal Ecology
    • Watershed Systems
    • Science Education
    • Science and Social Justice
    • Environmental Policy

Some pathways may require additional coursework outside the major. Talk to your advisor to find out how best to meet your personal, academic and career goals.
