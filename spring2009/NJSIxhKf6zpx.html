Interdisciplinary Master of Arts with Instructional Science and Technology Emphasis ~ Academic Advising and Support Services

The Interdisciplinary Master of Arts with Instructional Science and Technology Emphasis (IST) provides you with a complete analysis of your academic preparation that serves as the basis for the program.

IST faculty members conduct ongoing advising on an as-needed basis. They maintain office hours that you can use to access appropriate course and academic advising either in person or by telephone. All faculty members also extensively use email for advising.

IST also provides peer advising specific to the program.

IST Student Services can be reached at 831-582-4791 or by email at mist@csumb.edu
