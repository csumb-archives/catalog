Earth Systems Science & Policy Minor ~ Overview

The Earth Systems Science & Policy minor encourages non-Environmental Science, Technology & Policy majors to pursue their interest in science. It formally recognizes that this program has an obligation to provide high quality educational opportunities to all CSUMB students who want a deeper understanding of science in order to make informed contributions to their community on environmental issues.

The Earth Systems Science & Policy minor provides you with an opportunity to explore your particular interests in earth systems science, whether in the physical or biological sciences, the human dimension, or the interaction of science and policy.

Integration with Majors

Rather than being prescriptive, we have chosen to keep this minor flexible and inclusive so that students from all other CSUMB majors can customize the Earth Systems Science & Policy minor curriculum to meet their diverse needs in the environmental sciences.
