Academic Support

The CSUMB Strategic Plan makes a strong commitment to Student Learning and Support for Learning by identifying them as two of four themes guiding university operations. Moreover, CSUMB prides itself in being a student-centered university, and was lauded by the Western Association of Schools and Colleges accreditation team for standing out in that regard. As a result, CSUMB provides a full range of academic support services. These pages describe them briefly, while providing contact information and links to their websites so that you can learn more.
