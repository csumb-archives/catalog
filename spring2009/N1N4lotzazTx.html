Do you offer a program in mental health?

If you want to pursue a career in mental health, we offer the following pathways:

  • Collaborative Health and Human Services B.A.
  • Health and Wellness Minor
  • Kinesiology B.S.
    • Wellness Concentration
  • Psychology Minor
  • Psychology B.A.
  • Social Work Minor

Some pathways may require additional coursework outside the major. Talk to your advisor to find out how best to meet your personal, academic and career goals.
