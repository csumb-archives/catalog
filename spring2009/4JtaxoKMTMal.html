Tuition & Fees ~ Spring 2008 Payment/Refund Deadlines

Payment Deadlines

January 7, 2008 ~ Last day to pay registration fees, otherwise your classes will be dropped if you have not completed the FAFSA

January 8, 2008 ~ Starting today, payment or a Financial Aid fee deferment is required prior to registration

*April 1, 2008 ~ Last day of Spring 2008 fee deferment for financial aid students.

Refund Deadlines

January 18, 2008 ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses and receive a full refund of mandatory fees

February 4, 2008 ~ Last day to drop from fulltime to part-time and receive a full refund of the state fee difference (nonresident students also receive full refund of tuition)

February 11, 2008 ~ Last day for Open University students to drop and receive a prorated refund

April 4, 2008 ~ Last day to officially withdraw or cancel all courses and receive a prorated refund of mandatory fees

Registration Fees

A $25 late registration fee will be assessed to students who register after January 18, 2008.

*A $20 administrative late fee and a $20 housing late fee will be imposed on financial aid students who do not meet the April 1 payment deadline.

Installment Payment Plan Deadlines

See the list on the Campus Service Center website.

Financial Holds

Business and Finance (BF) holds are placed on accounts with balances. For financial aid fee deferred students, a BF hold will be placed on your account following the deferment deadline.
