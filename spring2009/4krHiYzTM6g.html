2007-2008 Academic Year Calendar

Approved by CSUMB President's Cabinet 4/9/07

Summer 2007

  • June 4 ~ Summer 2007 courses begin
  • June 29 ~ End of first summer 2007 four-week (June) session
  • July 2 ~ Second summer 2007 four-week (July) session begins
  • July 4 ~ Fourth of July observed ~ Campus closed
  • July 27 ~ Last day of summer 2007 courses
  • August 10 ~ Grades due ~ End of summer 2007 session

Fall 2007

  • August 20 ~ 2007-2008 Academic Year begins
  • August 20-22 ~ New faculty orientation
  • August 20-24 ~ Returning faculty planning days
  • August 24-26 ~ Fall 2007 Otter Days and Move-In
  • August 27 ~ Fall 2007 courses begin
  • September 3 ~ Labor Day observed ~ Campus closed
  • September 10 ~ Last day to add/drop courses without petition and a "W" grade
  • September 24 ~ Fall enrollment census date
  • November 12 ~ Veteran's Day observed ~ Campus closed
  • November 19-21 ~ Fall break ~ Faculty planning days
  • November 22-25 ~ Thanksgiving holiday ~ Campus closed
  • December 17-23 ~ Assessment period
  • December 25-31 ~ Holidays observed ~ Campus closed
  • January 1 ~ New Year's Day observed ~ Campus closed
  • January 3 ~ Grades due ~ End of Fall 2007 semester for faculty

Winter 2008

  • January 2 ~ Winter 2008 courses begin
  • January 18 ~ Last day of Winter 2008 courses
  • January 25 ~ Winter 2008 grades due

Spring 2008

  • January 16-18 ~ Spring 2008 faculty planning days ~ Pre-instructional activities
  • January 18-20 ~ Spring 2008 Student Orientation and Move-In
  • January 21 ~ Martin Luther King Day observed ~ Campus closed
  • January 22 ~ Spring 2008 courses begin
  • February 4 ~ Last day to add/drop courses without petition and a "W" grade
  • February 18 ~ Spring enrollment census date
  • March 17-21 ~ Spring break
  • March 31 ~ César Chávez Day observed ~ Campus closed
  • May 12-18 ~ Assessment period
  • May 15-16 ~ Capstone Festival
  • May 17 ~ Commencement
  • May 19-22 ~ Spring 2008 faculty planning days
  • May 26 ~ Memorial Day observed ~ Campus closed
  • May 30 ~ Grades due ~ End of Spring 2008 semester for faculty ~ End of 2007-2008 Academic Year
