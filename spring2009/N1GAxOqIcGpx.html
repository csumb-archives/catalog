Executive MBA ~ Overview

The objectives of the EMBA program are to:

  • Provide career advancement opportunities to fully employed and seasoned managers;
  • Help corporations, government agencies and nonprofit organizations achieve organizational effectiveness through professional development for their managers, and;
  • Drive economic development in the Tri-county region by developing a stronger School of Business with more advanced business offerings.
Sponsored By

College of Professional Studies > School of Business

Included Academic Fields

Concentrations Offered

Online Components

This program may be completed through courses that have both online and in-person components (i.e. hybrid courses).The entire EMBA program is online, minus one one-week summer session during the student's second year.
