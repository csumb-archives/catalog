Student Activities

Student Activities serves to enhance student involvement through programs, student organizations, leadership opportunities and experiences for students to apply the practical application of what is learned in the classroom. The staff provides a balance of challenge and support designed to enhance students' educational growth on personal, social and academic levels. Student Activities produces and supports a wide array of student-centered programming at CSUMB. Get involved in everything from Otter Days, CSUMB Goes to Town, leadership conferences, cultural enrichment programs, Multicultural Greek Council, Associated Students and much, much more!
