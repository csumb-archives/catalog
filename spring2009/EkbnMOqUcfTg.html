Kinesiology B.S. ~ Distinguishing Features

Broad-based current and practical knowledge of kinesiology, wellness and human performance, adventure education and their applications in the field

Faculty mentoring and support within the core curriculum and focused guidance within concentrations

Collaboration with other CSUMB and community programs to prepare students for leadership roles within both personal and professional fields

Service learning and internship possibilities provide hands-on learning experiences and facilitate networking in the field

Opportunity to focus on a particular area of interest such as wellness, human movement, exercise science, sports, coaching or outdoor education
