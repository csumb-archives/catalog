Financial Aid Office


FINANCIAL AID DEADLINES

The financial Aid Office observes the following deadlines:

Add/Drop Period

Fall/08 - September 08, 2008. Financial Aid will not be released onto a student account reflecting any change in enrollment. If a student adds or drops courses after the noted date, it is their responsibility to pay adequate fees.

Spring/09- February 02, 2009. Financial Aid will not be released onto a student's account reflecting any change in enrollment. If a student adds or drops courses after the noted date, it is their responsibility to pay adequate fees.

SAP Deadline

Required documentation (appeal, academic plan, ILP, workshop) must be submitted to the Financial Aid Office 30 days from start of term. Actual date for given semester will be published on the Financial aid website.

Consortium Agreements

Consortium Agreements will not be processed after the Add/Drop deadline for the given semester. All required documentation must be submitted to the Financial Aid Office. Documents must be signed and dated by adequate staff.

Census Date

For current financial aid recipients, a tuition percentage will be determined based on the return of Title IV funds regulations after a student withdraws from the University. The Census date for Fall/08 is September 22, 2008, and February 16, 2009 for Spring/09. The percentage calculated is a result of the percentage of the enrollment period completed by the student, as outlined below.
