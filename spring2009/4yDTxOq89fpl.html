Tuition & Fees ~ Summer 2008 Payment/Refund Deadlines

May 16, 2008~ Last day to pay registration fees to avoid enrollment cancellation

May 19, 2008 ~ Starting today, payment required before registration

May 30, 2008 ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses from the first four-week session and receive any refund ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses from the eight-week summer session and receive a full refund of mandatory fees ~ Last day to register in the eight-week and first four-week summer sessions without a $25 late registration fee

June 17, 2008 ~ 2nd payment due for Summer 2008 installment payment plan

June 27, 2008 ~ Last day to register in the second four-week summer session without a $25 late registration fee ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses from the second four-week summer session and receive any refund

July 3, 2008 ~ Last day to officially withdraw or cancel all courses from the eight-week summer session and receive a prorated refund of mandatory fees

July 17, 2008 ~ Third and final payment due for summer 2008 installment payment plan
