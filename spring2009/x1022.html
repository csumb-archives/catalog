Music Minor ~ Distinguishing Features

You acquire vital music and performing arts skills and knowledge in a rapidly changing technological world.

You learn about American forms of music and the value of aesthetic expression.

You engage in critical application tied to historical and cultural events.

You gain an understanding of the meaning of music in the context of social history.
