Course Description Management Directions

You can no longer edit course descriptions in Charlotte. Now that the Academic Senate and Office of the Provost have approved a course approval process that includes control of course descriptions, only that official process can be used to revise an existing course description or to create a new one.

The course approval process calls for involvement of the catalog editors in review of each course description early in the process. Beyond that, the catalog editors no longer play a roll in maintaining the descriptions or placing them into the student information system (currently Banner, and moving to PeopleSoft).

For further information on the official course approval process, please contact the Office of the Provost.
