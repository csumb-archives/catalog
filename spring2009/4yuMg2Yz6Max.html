Kinesiology B.S.

Professions in kinesiology and wellness studies are among the fastest growing in the world. Individuals, corporations and government entities have come to recognize that the wellbeing of individuals creates the foundation for the wellbeing and sustainability of our society and economy. By providing you with broad knowledge, skills and abilities, our Kinesiology faculty and staff will help prepare you to pursue a number of highly demanded and enjoyable professions.

More information about the Kinesiology major can be found right here. Simply use the left-side navigation or the matching links at the bottom of this page to find what you are looking for.

For more detailed information, take a look at the Kinesiology Department website where you can find out more about our program, related careers and our staff and faculty.
