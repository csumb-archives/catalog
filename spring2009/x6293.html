Student Affairs


 

Welcome to CSU Monterey Bay!  As the Interim Vice President for Student Affairs, I invite you to explore our campus community.  You will have many opportunities to connect with faculty and staff, and a variety of student activities, clubs, and other events that will enrich your academic experience, both in and outside the classroom.

Our division involves many departments, programs, and services that provide a distinctive CSUMB experience.  The focus is on academic success, health and wellness, student leadership, civic engagement, career exploration, convenient and quality student services, and personal growth and development.  We envision a student centered and learning focused environment.

The staff members in Student Affairs are here to support you and your success as a student, and to help you realize your goal of becoming a CSUMB graduate!

We hope that you will enjoy navigating this site, and we look forward to your feedback and suggestions for improvement. Also, please feel free to Contact us.

Sincerely,

Ronnie Higgs
Interim Vice President for Student Affairs
California State University, Monterey Bay
