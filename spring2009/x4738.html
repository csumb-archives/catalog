Teledramatic Arts and Technology B.A. ~ Faculty and Student Quotes

The future belongs to those who can imagine it.
~ Luis Valdez, Teledramatic Arts and Technology Founding Director

Imagination and creativity are at the heart of human communication. It comes from an instinctual urge to express in vibrant, living terms the essence of human experience. From the ancient world to contemporary times, we've used imagination and creativity to transmit history, ideas, myths, dreams, and legends as cultural narrative. Creative expression elevates individual experience into the realm of the universal, embracing the spirit of our local communities and the world around us. Digital communication tools give us unprecedented opportunities to create new forms and structures for creative cultural production, empowering a greater range and distribution of voices than ever before. The Teledramatic Arts and Technology Department is playing a leading role in exploring innovative media creation.
~ Michelle Riel, Teledramatic Arts and Technology Associate Professor
