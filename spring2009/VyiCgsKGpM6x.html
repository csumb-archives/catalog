Executive MBA ~ Faculty Quotes

"The School of Business at CSUMB recognizes the continuing need for a quality-based master's degree of business customized to fit the busy lives of executive employees and middle managers. These individuals desire to examine and reflectively integrate their experiences with enduring concepts in business; such that the goal of enhancing their future career potential can be more quickly realized. Often busy executives and middle managers do not have a facilitated environment to achieve this desired goal for career growth, and will more often than not pick up the latest best seller and hope to gain some kernel of wisdom to help them become better leaders or valuable contributors at work." ~ Marylou Shockley, Chair, School of Business

"We believe the newly designed, eight course Executive Master of Business Administration program made available by California State University, Monterey Bay can assist upwardly mobile managers in reaching their desired leadership goals. Our program addresses the study of all major facets of managerial thought and is created for easy access through online delivery to the student's desktop. The summer at-campus week provides students an exceptional opportunity to network with other managers. Many of the topics studied in this program are brought to life during these at-campus weeks through lectures, discussions and technology tours to various businesses in the California Central Coast and San Francisco areas. This 48 credit, two-year program is a "must do" for all managers moving up." ~ Murray Millson, Director, EMBA Program
