Computer Science and Information Technology B.S.

In the 21st Century, technology plays an increasingly important role in our lives. The Computer Science and Information Technology B.S. arms you with an understanding of fundamental computer science concepts and real-world information technology problem solving skills to prepare you for a career in the tech industry.

In addition to the technical skills and abilities, you'll gain an understanding of the human side of computing, the social, ethical and global issues related to technology, and the ability to collaborate and communicate effectively through project-based coursework.

You will find the official information about the major right here. Simply use the left-side navigation or the matching links at the bottom of this page.

For more detailed information including course webpages and faculty profiles, please visit the Information Technology and Communication Design website.
