Mission Statement

The Undergraduate Advising Center (UAC) provides information to help students become self-reliant navigators of their academic and career goals. Its primary emphasis is assisting students during their freshman and sophomore years with academic planning and course selection based on their academic and career goals, university requirements and pathways. The UAC will also assist upper-division students when departmental advising is not available.
