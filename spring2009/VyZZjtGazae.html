Tuition & Fees ~ Spring 2006 Payment/Refund Deadlines

November 1, 2005 ~ First day Installment Payment Plan (IPP) contracts available for Spring 2006

December 15, 2005 ~ Second payment due for Spring 2006 Installment Payment Plan ~ Last day to apply for the Spring 2006 Installment Payment Plan (first and second payments due)

January 15, 2006 ~ Third and final payment due for Spring 2006 Installment Payment Plan

January 20, 2006 ~ Last day to register without a $25 late registration fee ~ Last day for students without a financial aid fee deferral to pay registration fees without a $20 administrative late fee ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses and receive a full refund of mandatory fees ~ Last day to apply for financial aid and have fees deferred for Spring 2006 semester

February 6, 2006 ~ Last day to drop from fulltime to part-time and receive a full refund of the state fee difference (nonresident students also receive full refund of tuition)

February 16, 2006 ~ Last day for Open University students to drop and receive a prorated refund

March 10, 2006 ~ Business and Finance Holds placed on accounts with balances

April 1, 2006 ~ Last day of the Spring 2006 fee deferment for financial aid students ~ Last day to have fees paid without a $20 administrative late fee and/or $20 late housing fee

April 7, 2006 ~ Last day to officially withdraw or cancel all courses and receive a prorated refund of mandatory fees
