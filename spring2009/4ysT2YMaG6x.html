Teledramatic Arts and Technology B.A.

The Teledramatic Arts and Technology major prepares you for careers or further study in film, television, radio, and interactive media.

TAT provides a highly collaborative, interdisciplinary, production-based environment.

TAT graduates excel in the global media environment as professionals, artists, and conscientious agents of social change.

You will find all of the official information about the TAT major right here. Simply use the left-side navigation or the matching links below.

For more detailed information please visit the Teledramatic Arts and Technology Department website.
