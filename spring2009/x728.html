Management and Information Technology M.S. ~ Home

In this era of a global marketplace empowered by rapidly advancing technology, leaders must possess both business acumen and technological insight. Those who become savvy in both management and technology will truly empower themselves to succeed.

To provide that level of preparation, MSMIT blends the teaching of core management disciplines and the science of information technology. It prepares you to assume managerial positions in high-tech companies or launch new businesses with a skillset that enables you to effectively lead a workforce of ethnic, cultural, and gender diversity.

You will find all of the official information about the Management and Information Technology, M.S. right here. Simply use the left-side navigation.

For more detailed information including a look at student work and faculty bios, please visit the MSMIT pages on the School of Information Technology and Communications Design website.

IMPORTANT NOTICE:
The MSMIT program is not accepting applications for admission until further notice. If you are interested in the program, please monitor this website and on the MSMIT website periodically for updates. Please address all inquiries to msmit@csumb.edu.
