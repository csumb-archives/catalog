Kinesiology B.S.

Professions in kinesiology and wellness studies continue to rate among the fastest growing in the world as people, corporations, and government entities have come to recognize that the wellbeing of individuals creates the foundation for the wellbeing of everything else, including our society and economy. By providing you with broad knowledge, skills, abilities, and attitudes, our  Kinesiology major will prepare you to pursue a number of the highly demanded professions.

You will find the official information about the Kinesiology major right here. Simply use the left-side navigation or the matching links at the bottom of this page.

For additional information including a look at faculty bios, please visit the Kinesiology Department  website.
