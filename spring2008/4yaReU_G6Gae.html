Executive M.B.A. ~ Home

Are you a fully employed business professional ready to accelerate into an executive management position? Through hybrid courses that employ the Internet and strategically developed traditional classroom meetings, the Executive M.B.A. program allows you to complete 48 units of required courses within 24 months.  A fixed curriculum, graduate business cohort program, the EMBA offers courses through Extended Education in fall, winter, spring, and summer terms with traditional classroom meeting scheduled during the summer of the student's second year in the program. When admitted you become a member of an approximately 25-student cohort that progresses through the program in a lock-step manner.

You will find all of the official information about the program right here. Simply use the left-side navigation or the matching links at the bottom of this page.

You can also explore more detailed information on the School of Business website.
