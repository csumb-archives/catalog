Do you offer a program in international business?

If you want to pursue a career in international business,including international marketing,  we offer the following pathways:

  • Business Administration B.S.
    • Management and International Business Concentration
  • Business Minor
  • Japanese Culture and History Minor
  • Japanese Language and Culture Minor
  • Spanish Language and Hispanic Cultures Minor
  •  World Languages and Cultures B.A. 
    • Japanese Language and Culture Emphasis
    • Spanish Language and Hispanic Cultures Emphasis
