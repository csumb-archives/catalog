Music, B.A. ~ Learning Environment

The Monterey Bay region has long been an inspiration for musicians and performing artists. The Monterey Reggae, Carmel Bach, Monterey Bay Blues, and Monterey Jazz festivals, along with a multitude of other cultural events, present an off-campus musical extravaganza.

Our small interactive classes are conducted in modern facilities that include multimedia, telecommunications, and computer technologies.

Our multimedia studio, World Theater, University Center, and Music Hall ensure the finest settings for both vocal and instrumental performers.
