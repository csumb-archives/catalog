Culture and Equity ULR ~ Approved Courses

Courses currently approved to fulfill the Culture and Equity ULR are listed below. To ensure continued alignment between courses and learning outcomes, the Culture and Equity learning community reevaluates course approvals regularly. Courses no longer satisfying the ULR can be viewed in the archive of previously approved courses.

The listing of a course as approved does not indicate that it will be offered in any given semester. Check current offerings on the CSUMB.EDU/schedule website or check the course descriptions to see how frequently a course is offered.
