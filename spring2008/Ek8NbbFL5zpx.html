Vibrancy ULR ~ Approved Courses

Courses currently approved to fulfill each portion of the Vibrancy ULR are listed below. To ensure continued alignment between courses and learning outcomes, the Vibrancy learning community reevaluates course approvals regularly. Courses no longer satisfying the ULR can be viewed in the archive of previously approved courses.

The listing of a course as approved does not indicate that it will be offered in any given semester. Check current offerings on the CSUMB.EDU/schedule website or check the course descriptions to see how frequently a course is offered.

NOTE: KIN was formerly known as HPWE from Fall 2005 through Fall 2007, HWI from Fall 2000 through Spring 2004, as WRSI from Spring 1997 through Fall 2000, and RLWSS from Fall 1995 through Fall 1996. Thus, the course prefix indicated below has varied while the number has remained consistent throughout the approved years.
