Student Disability Resources (SDR)

SDR's goal is for students to learn how to manage their disabilities, academic accommodations, and other support services that promote success. SDR assists students who have learning, mobility, vision, and/or psychological disabilities; are deaf or hard of hearing; have other chronic medical conditions that result in disability; and experience temporary disabilities.
