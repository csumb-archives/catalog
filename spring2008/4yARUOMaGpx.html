Do you offer study in digital media?

If you want to pursue a career in digital media, we offer the following pathways:

  • Communications Design Minor
  • Human Communication B.A.
    • History, Oral History, and New Media Concentration
    • Journalism and Media Studies Concentration
  • Telecommunications, Multimedia, and Applied Computing B.S.
    • Visual Design Emphasis
    • Digital Animation Emphasis
    • Interactive Media and Game Design Emphasis
    • Web Design and Development Emphasis
    • Software Development Emphasis
  • Teledramatic Arts B.A.
  • Visual and Public Art B.A.
    • Integrated Media and Photography Concentration
