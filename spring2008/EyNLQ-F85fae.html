Environmental Health Policy Minor ~ Faculty and Student Quotes

The Environmental Health Policy Minor’s interdisciplinary coursework in Collaborative Health & Human Services and Earth Systems Science & Policy, is a perfect combination of applied learning experiences for students who are considering careers with environmental health advocacy organizations and public health agencies. This minor will prepare you for a career that will make a difference in the environment and the population's health.
~ Kim Judson, Professor, CHHS
