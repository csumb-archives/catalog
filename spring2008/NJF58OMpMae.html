Do you offer communications?

If you want to pursue a career in communications or communication studies, we offer the following pathways:

  • Human Communication B.A.
    • Creative Writing and Social Action Concentration
    • Journalism and Media Studies Concentration
    • Writing and Rhetoric Concentration
  • Communications Design Minor
  • Creative Writing and Social Action Minor
  • Human Communication Minor
  • Journalism/Media Minor
  • Telecommunications, Multimedia and Applied Computing B.S. - Design track
  • Writing and Rhetoric Minor
