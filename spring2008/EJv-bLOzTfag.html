Financial Aid ~ Scholarships

Scholarships are a form of financial aid that help pay for your education. Unlike loans, you do not need to repay scholarships. CSUMB administers at least 45 prestigious scholarships ranging from $600 to $5,500 per academic year. CSUMB awards the available scholarships based on academic achievement, leadership, and community service. In order for you to be selected for any of the scholarships you must complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA). The Financial Aid Office administers the scholarships listed. No separate applications are required in order to be considered unless specifically requested. Please visit the Financial Aid Office Scholarship Website for more details.
