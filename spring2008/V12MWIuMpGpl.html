Records & Registration ~ Student Classification

  Lower-division students are:
  • Freshmen who have earned 29 or fewer semester credits
  • Sophomores who have earned 30-59 semester credits

Upper-division students are:

  • Juniors who have earned 60-89 semester credits
  • Seniors who have earned 90 or more semester credits
  • Second baccalaureate students who possess a recognized baccalaureate degree and are enrolled in an undergraduate degree program
  • Post baccalaureate students who possess a recognized baccalaureate degree and are enrolled in a teaching credential program
  • Students with graduate standing who possess a recognized baccalaureate degree and are enrolled in a graduate studies program
