Do you offer a program to study physical education?

If you want to pursue a career in physical education, we offer the following pathways:

  • Health and Wellness Minor
  • Kinesiology B.S.
    • Exercise Science Concentration
    • Human Movement: Adapted Physical Activity Concentration
  • Human Movement: Adapted Physical Activity Minor
  • Sport Management Minor
