Vibrancy ULR ~ Course Archive

 

Courses that once fulfilled the Vibrancy ULR but no longer do are listed below along with the timeframe during which each was approved. Successful completion of a course within the timeframe indicates ULR fulfillment.

NOTE: HPWE was known as HWI from Fall 2000 through Spring 2004, as WRSI from Spring 1997 through Spring 2000, and RLWSS from Fall 1995 through Fall 1996. Thus, the course prefix indicated below may have varied while the number has remained consistent through the approved years.
