Do you offer a program in public health?

If you want to pursue a career in public health, we offer the following pathways:

  • Biology B.S
    • Pre-Medical Concentration
  • Collaborative Health and Human Services B.A.
    • Community Health Concentration
  • Earth Systems Science & Policy B.S.
    • Science and Social Justice Concentration
    • Environmental Policy Concentration
  • Environmental Health Policy Minor
  • Health and Wellness Minor
  • Public Policy M.A.
