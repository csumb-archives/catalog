Do you offer a program studying nonprofit administration?

If you want to pursue a career in nonprofit administration, we offer the following pathways:

  • Business Administration B.S.
    • Entrepreneurship/Intrapreneurship Concentration
    • Management and International Business Concentration
  • Collaborative Health and Human Services B.A.
    • Community Health Concentration
    • Social Work Concentration
  • Education M.A
  • Human Communications B.A.
    • Practical and Professional Ethics Concentration
  • Social and Behavioral Sciences B.A.
    • Social History Concentration
  • Public Policy M.A.
