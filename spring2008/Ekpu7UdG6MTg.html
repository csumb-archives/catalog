International Health Policy Minor ~ Home

If you aspire to work on global health issues or a related area of public policy or health, the International Health Policy Minor will complement your major with courses in international policy and politics, economics, public health, and international women's health and social issues.

You will find all of the official information about the minor right here. Simply use the left-side navigation. For more detailed information including a look at related student work, faculty bios, and internship opportunities, please visit the Global Studies Department and Health, Human Services, and Public Policy Department websites.
