Tuition & Fees ~ Spring 2007 Payment/Refund Deadlines

January 4, 2007 ~ Last day to receive mailed registration payments to avoid disenrollment ~ Last day to sign up for the Installment Payment Plan to avoid disenrollment (1st installment due at time of sign-up)

 

January 5, 2007 ~ Last day to pay registration fees in person at the Campus Service Center to avoid disenrollment ~ Last day to apply for financial aid and have fees deferred to avoid disenrollment

 

January 7, 2007 ~ Last day to pay registration fees online to avoid disenrollment

 

January 8, 2007 ~ Starting today, registration payment required before registering.  This includes students who were previously disenrolled for non-payment

 

January 19, 2007 ~ Last day to register without a $25 late registration fee ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses and receive a full refund of mandatory fees

 

February 4, 2007 ~ Second payment due for spring 2007 Installment Payment Plan

 

February 5, 2007 ~ Last day to drop from full-time to part-time and receive a full refund of the state fee difference (non-resident students also receive full refund of tuition)

 

February 15, 2007 ~ Last day for Open University students to drop and receive a prorated refund

 

March 4, 2007 ~ Third and final payment due for spring 2007 Installment Payment Plan

 

April 2, 2007 ~ Last day of the spring 2007 fee deferment for financial aid students ~ Last day to have fees paid without a $20 administrative late fee and/or a $20 late housing fee ~ Last day to have fees paid without a Business & Finance hold placed on account

 

April 6, 2007 ~ Last day to officially withdraw or cancel all courses and receive a prorated refund of mandatory fees
