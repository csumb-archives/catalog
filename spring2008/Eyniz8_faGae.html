Coastal and Watershed Science and Policy, M.S. ~ Academic Advising and Support Services

As a prospective student, you should contact a faculty member in your research area of interest to discuss openings in the research group and possible thesis topics.

Once you are accepted into the Coastal and Watershed Science & Policy program, you will work closely with a faculty advisor to plan your courses and research. Prospective students may contact the graduate secretary with initial advising questions:
