Business Administration, B.S. ~ Learning Environment

Our campus is an hour from Silicon Valley, the world's greatest concentration of high tech startup companies.

San Francisco, gateway to America's international business connections with the Pacific Rim, is two hours up the coast.

To the south, Mexico provides international study opportunities.

The economic diversity of the California Central Coast—just minutes away from campus—presents a range of excellent opportunities in tourism, value-added agriculture, marine ventures, and much more.

CSUMB is largely a residential campus. The School of Business emphasizes small classes to enhance student learning and strong student-instructor interaction.
