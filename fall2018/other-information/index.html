Information concerning the cost of attending CSUMB is available online. This page includes tuition and fees; the estimated costs of books and supplies; estimates of typical student room, board, and transportation costs; and, if requested, additional costs for specific programs. You can also use the Cost of Attendance Calculator to estimate your costs.
