College of Professional Studies ~ Schools and Departments

The academic divisions, schools, and departments in the College of Professional Studies reflect the university vision and support the commitment to interdisciplinarity, service learning, diversity and technology. Full descriptions of the divisions, schools and departments may be found on their individual websites. These can be reached by clicking on their names below. Official catalog descriptions of their academic offerings may be reached directly by clicking on the title of an offering in the list below.

  • Business Administration B.S.
  • Executive M.B.A.
  • Management and Information Technology M.S. [in conjunction with the School of Information Technology and Communications Design within the College of Science, Media Arts and Technology]
  • Business Minor
