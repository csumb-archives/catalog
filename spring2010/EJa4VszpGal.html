Campus Service Center

The Campus Service Center provides forms, information, and front counter support for admissions, records, transcripts, financial aid, and cashiering. The CSC distributes parking permits, Otter ID Cards and paychecks. The CSC is able to provide information and services with its access to student records and a fully functional cashiering system.
