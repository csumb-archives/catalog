Tuition & Fees ~ Summer 2009

Payment is due prior to registering for your summer courses.

In addition to the State University Fee (SUF), all matriculated students pay *$33 in Campus-Based Fees.

$29
Materials, Services, & Facilities Fee
$4 Associated Student Body Fee
$33 Total Campus-Based Fees

 Also, nonresident students pay an additional $339 per unit for tuition.

  • Note: Summer units do not count toward the 30 unit maximum.

For extended ed course fees, refer to the CSUMB.EDU/extended website.

For additional fee information, visit the CSUMB.EDU/csc website.
