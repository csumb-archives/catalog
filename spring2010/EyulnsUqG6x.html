Tuition & Fees ~ Fall 2009 Payment/Refund Deadlines

Payment Deadlines

July 31, 2009 ~ Last day to pay registration fees, otherwise your classes will be dropped if you have not completed the FAFSA

August 3, 2009 ~ Starting today, payment or a Financial Aid fee deferment is required prior to registration. This includes students who's enrollment was cancelled.

September 4, 2009 ~ Last day of the fall 2009 fee deferment for financial aid students ~ Last day to have fees paid without a $20 administrative late fee and/or a $20 late housing fee ~ Last day to have fees paid without a Business & Finance hold placed on account

Refund Deadlines

August 21, 2009 ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses and receive a full refund of mandatory fees

September 4, 2009 ~ Last day to drop from fulltime to part-time and receive a full refund of the state fee difference (nonresident students also receive full refund of tuition)

September 14, 2009 ~ Last day for Open University students to drop and receive a prorated refund

October 28, 2009 ~ Last day to officially withdraw or cancel all courses and receive a prorated refund of mandatory fees

Registration Deadline

August 21, 2009 ~ Last day to register without being assessed a $25 Late Registration Fee

Installment Payment Plan

First Installment due with signed contract ~ Installments are due on or before the listed due date on contract ~ $20 Administrative Late Fee is assessed for each late payment made

September 7, 2009 ~ 2nd Installment Due

October 7, 2009 ~ 3rd Installment Due

See list on the Campus Service Center website.

Financial Holds

Business and Finance holds (BF) are placed on accounts with balances.  For students with a financial aid fee deferment, BF holds are placed on accounts with balances following the deferment deadline.
