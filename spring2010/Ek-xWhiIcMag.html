Business Minor ~ Degree Requirements

Credits Required

Total credits in the minor: 16 credits of upper-division coursework (grade "C" or above)

Students must complete

Two outcomes and their corresponding core courses (2 courses; 4 units each)
One elective course in one of your selected outcomes (4 units)
One more elective course from any of the outcomes (4 units)


Leadership and Management (Outcome)

Core course: BUS 305 Principles of Management
Electives: BUS 331 Oganizational Behavior, BUS 330 Human Resources Management, BUS 372 Hotel/Resprt Operations Management, BUS 493 International Comparative Management


Marketing (Outcome)

Core course: BUS 306 Fundamentals of Marketing
Electives: BUS 322 Product Management and Marketing for Startups, BUS 323 Service Marketing, BUS 421 Marketing Research, BUS 425 Global Marketing


Finance (Outcome)

Prerequisite: BUS 203 Financial Accounting
Core course: BUS 307 Finance
Electives: BUS 383 Intermediate Accounting I, BUS 384 Intermediate Accounting II, BUS 385 Income Tax I


Information Technology (Outcome)

Prerequisite: BUS 299 Excel Spreadsheet (If BUS 200 not taken for tech/info ULR)
Core Course: BUS 308 Computer Information Systems
Electives: BUS 469 Database Management, BUS 460 E-Com: Emerging Business Models-Issues


Entrepreneurship (Outcome)

Prerequisite: BUS 203 Financial Accounting (required)
Prerequisite: BUS 306 Fundamentals of Marketing (required)
Prerequisite: BUS 307 Principles of Finance (required)
Core Course: BUS 310 Entrepreneurship
Electives: BUS 322 Product Management and Marketing for Startups, BUS 451 Venture Formation and Finance (prereq BUS 310)
