Master of Public Policy ~ Home

Want to improve government quality and responsiveness? Looking to optimize individual, family and community health and well-being? Hoping to work for social justice? Dreaming of learning from experts who have succeeded in the deepest trenches of public policymaking?

Our Master of Public Policy, offered in collaboration with the Panetta Institute, will set you up to do all of this and more. Our practical, results-oriented approach will combine academic tools with experience and insights from successful policymakers at all levels. Whether working in the public, nonprofit or private sector, MPP will empower you to do more.

You will find all of the official information about the Master of Public Policy right here. Simply use the left-side navigation. For general information about the MPP Program's academic home, please visit the Department of Health, Human Services and Public Policy website. For detailed information including application tips and sample forms, internship information and a downloadable brochure, please visit the Master of Public Policy homepage.
