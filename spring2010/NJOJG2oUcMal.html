Student Disability Resources

SDR promotes: the empowerment, wellness and full integration of students with disabilities into campus life; universal design for learning; inclusive environments; and disability studies. SDR assists students who have learning, mobility, vision and/or psychological disabilities; are deaf or hard of hearing; have other chronic medical conditions that result in disability; and those with temporary disabilities. SDR's primary goal is for students to learn how to successfully manage their disabilities, academic and housing accommodations, courses, transportation and other support services.
