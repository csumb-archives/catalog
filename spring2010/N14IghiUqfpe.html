Do you offer a program in special needs care?

If you want to pursue a career in special needs care, we offer the following pathways:

  • Collaborative Health and Human Services B.A.
  • Human Development Minor
  • Kinesiology B.S.
    • Human Movement: Adapted Physical Activity Concentration
  • Social Work Minor

Some pathways may require additional coursework outside the major. Talk to your advisor to find out how best to meet your personal, academic and career goals.
