Liberal Studies B.A. ~ Teaching Pathways

The Teaching Pathway options are designed to meet California State requirements for subject matter competency for those desiring to attain a multiple subects credential to teach in K-8 schools. You take sequences of courses across academic disciplines that prepare you to teach the subjects usually taught in elementary school and to pass the California Subject Examination Test: Multiple Subjects (required for entrance into a teaching credential program).
