Information Technology and Communication Design B.S. (formerly TMAC)

The way that we communicate, conduct business, learn and see the world will continue to change at an incredible pace due to ever-advancing technology. To keep pace, employers need people who truly grasp the complexities of technology so that they can remain competitive as changes occur. Our Information Technology and Communication Design major develops skills in visual design, web design, interactive media, and animation. The program also provides broad-based understanding of current technologies and applications, and merges this knowledge with design, communication, and management skills. ITCD majors develop a customized university experience that is pertinent to their career ambitions and the needs of a global, multicultural society.

You will find the official information about the ITCD major here. Use the left-side navigation or the matching links at the bottom of this page.

For more detailed information including a look at student work and faculty bios, please visit the School of Information Technology and Communication Design website.
