This CSUMB.EDU/catalog website provides comprehensive current official information about academic programs, services, procedures and policies. As such, it serves as the authoritative and complete catalog resource for current students and other members of our campus community.

We also provide an abridged print version of the CSUMB catalog.

For additional online academic information, please check the resources listed on the CSUMB.EDU/academics webpages including the CSUMB.EDU/schedule website and MyCSUMB.
