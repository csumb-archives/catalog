Science ULR ~ Approved Courses

Courses currently approved to fulfill the two parts of the Science ULR are listed below. To ensure continued alignment between courses and learning outcomes, the Science ULR learning community reevaluates course approvals regularly. Courses no longer satisfying part of the ULR can be viewed in the archive of previously approved courses.

The listing of a course as approved does not indicate that it will be offered in any given semester. Check current offerings on the CSUMB.EDU/schedule website or check the course descriptions to see how frequently a course is offered.

NOTE: Courses listed below with BIO, ENVS, GEOL and PHYS prefixes were formerly listed with an ESSP prefix. The course number has remained consistent throughout the approved years. Thus, the approval range for those courses includes the course number with the ESSP prefix.
