Library

Tanimura & Antle Family Memorial Library provides access to more than 12,000 journals, magazines, and newspapers in electronic format to take advantage of the web's enormous capacity for obtaining and sharing information. It also houses 153,000 print volumes, with the potential capacity for 573,000 volumes. The reference desk area offers dual-boot Mac/PC computers to access the library's online catalog and reference librarians assist with database searches, library catalogs and using the Internet.

The library also offers ample quiet study space with great views, group-study rooms equipped with the latest A/V technology and plasma monitors, classrooms and auditoria, a cafe, and student support programs.
