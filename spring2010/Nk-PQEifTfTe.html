Single Subject Credential ~ Careers and Graduate Study

Related Careers

Students earning their ELA/BCLAD Single Subject Credential at CSUMB have entered and achieved notable success as high school and middle school (grades 6-12) teachers. They have been particularly well prepared to work with linguistically and culturally diverse student populations.

Graduate Study

Many students earning their Single Subject Credential at CSUMB have attended a variety of graduate institutions, including CSUMB, for advanced study. Our Master of Arts in Education program engages practicing teachers in action research projects in their own schools and focused on issues surrounding social justice.
