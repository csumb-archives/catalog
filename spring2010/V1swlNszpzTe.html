Do you offer a program in water quality technology?

If you want to pursue a career in water quality technology, we offer the following pathways:

  • Earth Systems Science & Policy B.S.
    • Marine & Coastal Ecology Concentration
    • Watershed Systems Concentration
  • Earth Systems Science & Policy Minor

Some pathways may require additional coursework outside the major. Talk to your advisor to find out how best to meet your personal, academic and career goals.
