Do you offer a program in recreation management?

If you want to pursue a career in recreation management, we offer the following pathways:

  • Health and Wellness Minor
  • Kinesiology B.S.
    • Wellness Concentration
    • Interdisciplinary Concentration: Sport Focus
  • Liberal Studies B.A. Liberal Learning Pathway
  • Outdoor Recreation/Education Minor
  • Sports Management Minor

Some pathways may require additional coursework outside the major. Talk to your advisor to find out how best to meet your personal, academic and career goals.
