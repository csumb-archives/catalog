Student Affairs


Welcome to Student Affairs!

 

Welcome to CSU Monterey Bay!  As the Vice President for Student Affairs, I invite you to explore your campus community.  There are many opportunities to connect with faculty and staff, and a variety of student activities, clubs, and other events that will enrich your academic experience, both in and outside the classroom.

Student Affairs  involves many departments, programs, and services that provide a distinctive CSUMB experience.  Our focus is on academic success, health and wellness, student leadership, civic engagement, convenient and quality student support services, and personal growth and development.  We envision a student centered and learning focused environment.

The staff members in Student Affairs are here to support you and your success as a student, and are supportive in assisting you realize your goal of becoming a CSUMB graduate!

We hope that you will enjoy navigating this site; we look forward to your feedback and suggestions for improvement. Also, please feel free to contact us at (831) 582-3812, or drop by the Student Affairs Administration Building.

Sincerely,

Patricia Cardenas-Adame, Ph.D.
Vice President for Student Affairs
California State University, Monterey Bay
