Tuition & Fees ~ Home

As one of the 23 member campuses in the California State University system, CSUMB charges standardized registration fees and nonresident tuition along with a handful of campus-specific fees. These pages describe the fees and related procedures.

The CSU makes every effort to keep student costs to a minimum.  Fees listed in published schedules or student accounts may need to be increased when public funding is inadequate.  Therefore, CSU must reserve the right, even after initial fee payments are made, to increase or modify any listed fees, without notice, until the date when instruction for a particular semester or quarter has begun.  all CSU listed fees should be regarded as estimates that are subject to change upon approval by The Board of Trustees.

For information on the costs of housing on our residential campus, please visit the CSUMB.EDU/housing website.

For a summary of the overall costs of education and details on financial aid resources, please visit the Financial Aid section of this catalog.
