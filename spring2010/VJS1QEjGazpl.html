Computer Science and Information Technology, B.S. ~ Related Careers

Related Careers

Computer Software Engineer ~ Networks/Communication System Analyst ~ Information Security Specialist ~ Computer Systems Administrator/Analyst ~ Database Analyst/Programmer/Administrator ~ Game Developer

ITCD graduates excel in careers utilizing their knowledge and skills in technology, communication, collaboration, and problem-solving. Our graduates have entered careers and achieved notable success in web development, animation, programming, system administration, network management and security, teaching and more.

The organizations hiring them have included Apple, CISCO Systems, DoD, Defense Manpower Data Center, Symantec, SUN, Electronic Arts, EMC, Lockheed, NASA, USA Today, CSUMB, and school districts. Some graduates have started their own businesses or work as independent consultants.

Graduate Study

Graduates will be prepared to pursue graduate studies in computer science, information technology and many other related areas

ITCD graduates have attended a variety of Masters and Doctoral programs at graduate institutions including UC Santa Cruz, University of Utah, West Washington University, Naval Postgraduate School, and CSUMB.

They have entered and achieved notable success in the various fields of graduate study including management and information technology, computer science and information security.
