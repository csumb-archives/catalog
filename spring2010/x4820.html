The Undergraduate Advising Center (UAC) is your one-stop-advising location for all your advising needs.  We provide quality advising services to better guide you through your academic and personal goals, and assist you with questions you may have regarding the academic requirements and policies at CSUMB. 

The Undergraduate Advising Center is committed to providing you with the following services:

  • Provide an assigned advisor in the UAC (Freshmen, Sophomores and Undeclared students only)
  •  Provide excellent and quality advising services to enhance your success
  • Provide a friendly and knowledgeable advisor(s) staff
  • Make Real Time Appointments Online (available August 1, 2009)
  • Help you prepare 2-4 years educational plans for all degree requirements
  • Provide excellent referral services to other departments, majors, and services across campus
  • Prepare you to transition into your major successfully
  • Provide information to help you match your major to specific career goals
  • Provide detailed advising to help remove advising holds and roadblocks to your success


If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to stop by our office today!
