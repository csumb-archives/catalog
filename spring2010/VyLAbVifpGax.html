Directories & Resources

You can reach our A-Z Directory by clicking the link in the masthead of any page using the standard CSUMB template. The A-Z Directory provides a search mechanism to find employees, and an alphabetical listing of all CSUMB department, program, and service names, nicknames, and acronyms. It also offers listings by category.

The directories and guides listed in this section of the catalog will help you find career pathways, understand unique CSUMB terms , and guide you toward appropriate offices.

Finding Information

To find information in this section, use the navigation on the left or the matching links at the bottom of this page. You can also use the search box at the top of the page, selecting Search CSUMB Catalog from the drop-down list of search parameters.
