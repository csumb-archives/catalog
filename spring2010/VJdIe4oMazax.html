Do you offer a program in statistics?

If you want to pursue a career in statistics, we offer the following pathways:

  • Mathematics B.S.
    • Pure Mathematics Concentration
  • Mathematics Minor

Some pathways may require additional coursework outside the major. Talk to your advisor to find out how best to meet your personal, academic and career goals.
