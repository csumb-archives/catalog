Mathematics B.S. ~ Typical Pathways

Because we have built our academic programs on an outcomes-based education model, you have latitude to design an Individual Learning Plan to achieve your personal target outcomes while also satisfying University Learning Requirements, Graduation Learning Outcomes, and Major Learning Outcomes. Given that, you should consider the following pathways only as possibilities:

Freshman Pathway
Pure Mathematics Concentration

Transfer Pathway
Pure Mathematics Concentration

 Freshman Pathway
Teaching Concentration

 Transfer Pathway
Secondary Teaching Concentration

These pathways serve as tools, not prescribed programs of study. Before enrolling, you can use them to get a feel for what you can do in this program. After enrolling, you and your advisors can use them as a starting point for building your personalized outcomes-based ILP.
