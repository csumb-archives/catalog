Biology Minor ~ Degree Requirements

Semester Credits Required

Total credits in the minor: 20 (some of which may double-count for your major, as detailed below)

Prerequisites
  • Math 130/L
  • Chem 110/L
  • Chem 111/L
Outcomes, Courses and Assessment

You must achieve all of the Learning Outcomes listed below by successfully completing the listed courses or alternative assessment options.

Outcome 1 - Biological Foundations

  • Complete all of the following courses (12 credits). Up to 8 of these credits may double-count for the major; 4 credits should count only for the minor. Note that each of these biology courses has one or more chemistry prerequisites.

BIO 240/L; BIO 241/L; BIO 242/L

Outcome 2 - Advanced Knowledge in Biology

  • Complete three or more courses, totaling at least 8 credits and including at least one course with a lab, from the list below. At least 8 credits taken to fulfill this Advanced Biology Learning Outcome must be in addition to any upper-division biology courses counted toward one's major requirements. Note that each of these upper-division biology courses has one or more lower-division biology prerequisites

BIO 300; BIO 310/L; BIO 311; BIO 314; BIO 320/L; BIO 340/L; BIO 341; BIO 342/L; BIO 345/L; BIO 360/L*; BIO 410; BIO 420/L*; BIO 444L; BIO 448/L*, BIO 460L*; BIO 472/L*

* Note: Courses with an asterisk (*) are not yet available but are expected to be offered for the first time sometime in the next 2-3 years as part of the new biology major. They are included here for the benefit of student who wish to plan ahead. Interested students should contact the Division of Science & Environmental Policy for the latest information about when these courses will be offered.
