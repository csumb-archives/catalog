Computer Science and Information Technology B.S. ~ Overview

The Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and Information Technology is offered by the School of Information Technology and Communication Design.

The CSIT major is a degree program with a unique blend of theories of computer science and practice of information technology. Students in this degree program begin their education by taking a set of common core courses to establish a strong foundation in computer science and information technology. The students then build upon this foundation by selecting Software Engineering,  Network and Security or Information Systems,  as their concentration.  (Concentration in Game Development is under development).   Throughout the program students learn fundamental computing and information technology concepts while applying their knowledge to solve real-world problems. Collaboration and communication skills are infused into the learning experiences, and courses are heavily project-based, in a state-of-the-art technological environment.
