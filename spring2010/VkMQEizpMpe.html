Music, B.A. ~ Careers and Graduate Study

Related Careers

MPA graduates have the knowledge, skills, and abilities necessary to enter and achieve notable success in arts advocacy; audio engineering; band music; broadcast audio; choral music; music education (K-12 and college); music composition; ethnomusicology; film scoring; music criticism; music production; CD-ROM and DVD production; technical recording; songwriting; and sound design.

Graduate Study

The Music B.A. also well equips graduates to enter a variety of graduate programs in music.
