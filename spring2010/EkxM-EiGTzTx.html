2009-2010 Academic Year Calendar

Approved by CSUMB President's Cabinet 12/1/08

Summer 2009

  • Summer add/drop dates*
  • June 1 ~ Summer 2009 courses begin
  • June 26 ~ End of first summer 2009 four-week (June) session
  • June 29 ~ Second summer 2009 four-week (July) session begins
  • July 3 ~ Fourth of July observed ~ Campus closed
  • July 24 ~ Last day of summer 2009 courses
  • August 7 ~ Grades due ~ End of summer 2009 session

Fall 2009

  • August 17 ~ 2009-2010 Academic Year begins
  • August 17-19 ~ New faculty orientation
  • August 17-21 ~ Returning faculty planning days
  • August 21-23 ~ Fall 2009 Otter Days and move-in
  • August 24 ~ Fall 2009 courses begin
  • September 4 ~ Last day to add/drop courses without petition and a "W" grade
  • September 7 ~ Labor Day observed ~ Campus closed
  • September 21 ~ Fall enrollment census date
  • October 19 ~ Spring 2010 advising begins
  • November 9 ~ Spring 2010 registration begins
  • November 11 ~ Veteran's Day observed ~ Campus closed
  • November 23-25 ~ Fall break ~ Faculty planning days
  • November 26-27 ~ Thanksgiving holiday ~ Campus closed
  • December 14-20 ~ Assessment period
  • December 25-30 ~ Holidays observed ~ Campus closed
  • January 1 ~ New Year's Day observed ~ Campus closed
  • January 5 ~ Grades due ~ End of fall 2009 semester for faculty

Winter 2010

  • January 4 ~ Winter 2010 courses begin
  • January 6 ~ Last day to add/drop winter 2010 courses
  • January 18 ~ Martin Luther King Day observed ~ Campus closed
  • January 22 ~ Last day of winter 2010 courses
  • January 29 ~ Winter 2010 grades due

Spring 2010

  • January 19-22 ~ Spring 2010 faculty planning days ~ Pre-instructional activities
  • January 22-24 ~ Spring 2010 Student Orientation and move-in
  • January 25 ~ Spring 2010 courses begin
  • February 5 ~ Last day to add/drop courses without petition and a "W" grade
  • February 19 ~ Spring enrollment census date
  • March 22-26 ~ Spring break
  • March 29 ~ Fall 2010 advising begins
  • March 31 ~ Cesar Chavez Day observed ~ Campus closed
  • April 19 ~ Fall 2010 registration begins
  • May 17-23 ~ Assessment period
  • May 20-21 ~ Capstone Festival
  • May 22 ~ Commencement
  • May 24-27 ~ Spring 2010 faculty planning days
  • May 31 ~ Memorial Day observed ~ Campus closed
  • June 4 ~ Grades due ~ End of spring 2010 semester for faculty ~ End of 2009-2010 Academic Year

* Summer add/drop period: Four, five and eight week sessions add/drop during first week of classes. Session meeting less than four weeks add/drop prior to first day of class.
