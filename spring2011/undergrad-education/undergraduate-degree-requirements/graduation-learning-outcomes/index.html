Each CSU creates a set of graduation requirements unique to that campus. At CSUMB, this unique set of Graduation Learning Outcomes comprises three of the 13 University Learning Requirements and upper-division service learning. GLO courses that are 300 or 400 level taken by an upper division student (a student who has completed 60+ units) may count toward the 9 unit requirement of upper division GE. Students should consult an advisor.

  • Culture and Equity ULR
  • Language ULR
  • Technology and Information ULR
  • Upper-Division Service Learning
    CSUMB requires you to take an upper-division service learning course in your major, applying discipline-specific skills and knowledge to community issues and examining issues of service and social responsibility that relate to your career field. You satisfy the upper-division service learning requirement by passing a course in the major that carries an S suffix. You will find specifics regarding this requirement in each major's description in the Majors section of this catalog. You can also read descriptions of the upper-division courses that include a service learning component by looking for the S suffix (e.g., HCOM 432S: Social Action Writing) in the Course Descriptions section of this catalog.
