In conjunction with the highly committed faculty members in all of CSUMB's degree programs, the Service Learning Institute's dedicated staff and faculty members coordinate all service learning experiences. They welcome you and will be happy to address your questions and concerns. Feel free to visit, or call for more information.

Asya Guillory, Information Specialist
The information specialist can provide you with copies of forms, answer general questions about service learning, and direct you to the appropriate SLI staff person for additional assistance.

Belinda Hieb, Office Manager
Please call if you have questions about timesheets, travel, invoices, and payments.

Laura Lee Lienk, SMART Service Learning Coordinator
Please call with service learning questions or concerns related to CST, ESSP, TAT, and Math.

Dr. Deborah Burke, Coordinator of Student Leadership in Service Learning
Please call if you are interested in additional information about the Student Leadership in Service Learning (sl)2 Program.

Coordinator of Introduction to Service Learning Instruction
Please call if you have questions or concerns about the Community Participation University Learning Requirement.

Mele Paracuelles, Program Analyst
Please call if you have questions about service learning budgets, personnel, and course scheduling.

Dr. Seth Pollack, Director
Please call if you have questions about CSUMB's service learning program, or the major-based service learning courses.

Roberta Valdez, Associate Director
Please call if you have questions related to campuswide support for service learning, including the placement and partnership process.

Alicia Hernandez Sanchez, Coordinator of Community Partnerships
Please call if you want to find out more about partnership opportunities with service learning courses.
