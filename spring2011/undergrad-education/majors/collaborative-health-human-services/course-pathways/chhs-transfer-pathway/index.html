Requirement View

This sample pathway assumes that the student has opted for Dual Concentrations in Social Work and Community Health, has met all lower-division GE requirements, has passed an Introductory Statistics class, and has demonstrated competence at the equivalent of the Language 201 level upon entry to CSUMB.
