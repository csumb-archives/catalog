The Vibrancy ULR is a four-credit requirement consisting of a three-credit Theory course and a one-credit Activity course. A student must complete both portions to meet this ULR. It requires you to demonstrate knowledge of the concepts of health and wellness (with special emphasis on exercise science, nutrition science, and stress management), describe their dimensions, and then create a personal wellness plan or project, implementing it and reporting measured progress.

Courses currently approved to fulfill this ULR are listed below.  Links to the ULR's outcomes, assessement and an archived list of courses no longer satisfying the ULR can be found below or in the navigation column.
