An interdisciplinary program that integrates information technology, instructional design and educational technology, MIST responds to the increasing demand for professionals who understand learning theory, instructional design and technology, interactive multimedia and assessment. Whether a corporate trainer, classroom educator, or a person developing coursework for those professionals, the largely online MIST curriculum will empower you to make a difference for those you serve.

You will find all of the official information about the Master of Science in Instructional Science and Technology right here. Use the navigation or the matching links at the bottom of this page.

For more detailed information including a look at student work and faculty bios, please visit the MIST pages on the School of Information Technology and Communication Design website.
