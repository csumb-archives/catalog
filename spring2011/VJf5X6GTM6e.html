Spanish Emphasis Freshman Pathway

Pathway Type: 
Freshmen
Major: 
World Languages & Cultures

This pathway is intended for incoming Freshmen students, majoring in WLC with an Empasis in Spanish Language and Culture, who come with 2 years of high school Spanish language study. With such study, the student should be eligible to begin at the 102 level. Please consult with a WLC advisor for more information. If a student chooses to begin Spanish at the 101-level, the model below may need to be extended. WLC offers an online Spanish language placement test.
