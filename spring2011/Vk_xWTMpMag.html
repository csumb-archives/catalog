Payment Deadlines

July 29, 2011 ~ Last day to pay registration tuition and fees, have a financial aid payment extension, or have a third party authorization form covering registration tuition and fees on file with Student Accounting, otherwise your classes will be dropped for non-payment ~ To remain eligible for student housing, full payment of housing and meal plan charges must be received OR you must have a financial aid payment extension in place.  Housing and meal plan eligibility questions should be directed to Student Housing & Residential Life at 831-582-3378.

July 30, 2011 ~ Starting today, payment or a Financial Aid payment extension, or a third party authorization form covering registration tuition and fees on file with Student Accounting is required prior to registration.  This includes students whose enrollment was cancelled.

September 2, 2011 ~ Last day of fall 2011 payment extension for financial aid students ~ Last day to have registration tuition and fees and housing/meal plan balances paid without being assessed a $20 late administrative fee and/or a $20 late housing fee ~ Last day to pay all registration tuition and fees and housing/meal plan balances to avoid a Business & Finance (Student Financials) hold placed on your student account.

Refund Deadlines

August 19, 2011 ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses and receive a full refund of mandatory tuition and fees

September 2, 2011 ~ Last day to drop from full-time to part-time and receive a full refund of the tuition fee difference (nonresident students also receive full refund of non-resident tuition fee)

September 12, 2011 ~ Last day for Open University students to drop and receive a prorated refund

October 26, 2011 ~ Last day to officially withdraw or cancel all courses and receive a prorated refund of mandatory tuition and fees

Registration Deadlines

August 19, 2011 ~ Last day to register without being assessed a $25 Late Registration Fee

Installment Payment Plan

First Installment due with signed contract ~ Installments are due on or before the listed due date on contract ~ $20 Administrative Late Fee is assessed for each late payment made

September 7, 2011 ~ 2nd Installment Due

October 7, 2011 ~ 3rd Installment Due

Financial Holds

Business and Finance (Student Financials) holds (SF) are placed on student accounts with balances. For students with a financial aid payment extension, SF holds are placed following the payment extension deadline.
