Related Careers

Master of Arts in Education with Curriculum and Instruction Emphasis graduates have entered and achieved notable success in teaching and leadership careers in K-16 schools, community colleges, and nonprofit organizations in the Tri-County area.

Master of Arts in Education with Special Education Emphasis graduates have entered and achieved notable success in psychology; sociology; child development; communicative sciences and disorders; occupational and physical therapy; and human communication.

Doctoral Study

If you wish to continue for doctoral study, you will receive guidance that helps you focus your preparation in this area. Since all MAE students complete a culminating project/thesis, you will develop research and writing skills as a normal part of proceeding through the program.

Our graduates have attended a variety of graduate institutions including UC Berkeley; Chapman; University of San Francisco; Pepperdine; Brown; Maryland; and University of Washington.
