The College of Science, Media Arts and Technology's  mission is to empower the CSUMB learning community by providing:

  • A broad-based, integrated approach to the study of science, technology, and mathematics
  • Application of technology in the study of digital design and interactive media
  • A framework for making informed and ethical choices in the information age
