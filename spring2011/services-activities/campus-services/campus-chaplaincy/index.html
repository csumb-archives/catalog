Located within the Personal Growth & Counseling Center, the Campus Chaplaincy provides pastoral care for individuals and the entire campus community, interfaith and interdenominational resources, support to faith related clubs, and programs and events at CSUMB. Campus Chaplains serve all students, faculty, and staff regardless of religious tradition or denomination, and respect all faiths and spiritual practices. If you need a speaker for your club, group, or class, or are looking for someone with whom you can talk, please contact us.
