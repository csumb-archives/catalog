ARAB 101: Begin Arabic I: ASMT

Description
Alternative assessment for the first semester of Arabic. Freshmen and sophomore transfers attempting to satisfy the Language ULR through assessment must enroll in ARAB 101, 102, 201, and 202 concurrently, except for international students who may enroll only in ARAB 202. Junior and senior transfers attempting to satisfy the Language ULR through assessment, must enroll in ARAB 101, 102, and 201, except for international students who may enroll only in ARAB 201 or 202.

4
