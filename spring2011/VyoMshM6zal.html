Courses that once fulfilled the Vibrancy ULR but no longer do are listed below along with the timeframe during which each was approved. Successful completion of a course within the timeframe indicates ULR fulfillment.

NOTE: Courses labeled "KIN" may have previously existed under one or more discipline acronyms: HPWE from Fall 2004 through Spring 2008; HWI from Fall 2000 through Spring 2004,  WRSI from Spring 1997 through Spring 2000, and RLWS from Fall 1995 through Fall 1996. The course number consistently identifies the same course regardless of course prefix, e.g., course number 117  identifies the same course whether titled WRSI, HWI, HPWE and/or KIN.
