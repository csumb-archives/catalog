Major Requirements

Credits Required: 

Recording Technology: 126 Units

Contemporary Studies: 120 Units

Students will complete core courses, Major Learning Outcomes and courses specific to their chosen concentration in order to succesfully complete their degree in Music. Students are required to take a theory placement assessment and complete a diagnostic audition.

Core Courses

  • Complete all of the following
  • MPA 110: Keyboard I (1 units)
  • MPA 210: Keyboard II (1 units)
  • MPA 101: Musicianship & Theory I (4 units)
  • MPA 102: Musicianship & Theory 2 (4 units)
  • MPA 103: Musicianship & Theory III (4 units)
  • MPA 300: Major ProSeminar (2 units)
  • Take each semester of junior and senior year:
  • MPA 440: Master Classes/Lecture Demos (1 units)
  • Take two semesters of:
  • MPA 475: Senior Capstone (2 units)
