MLO 2 Media, narrative and ethnographic skills

Ability to gather responsibly, interpret critically, narrate and disseminate the voices and stories of people, communities or events by integrating ethnographic or journalistic field research methods, cultural or media analysis, narrative writing and media production, including new digital media

 

Required Courses

  • Complete one of the following:
  • HCOM 307: Social Impact of Mass Media (4 units)
  • OR
  • HCOM 307S: Soc Impct Of Mass Media SL (6 units)
  • HCOM 316: Media Ethics (4 units)
  • HCOM 328: Latina Life Stories (4 units)
  • HCOM 329: Auto/Biografias (4 units)
  • HCOM 350: Oral Hist/Comm Mem (4 units)
  • HCOM 350S: Oral Hist/Comm Mem SL (6 units)
  • HCOM 384: Newswriting (4 units)
  • HCOM 385: Reporting (4 units)
  • HCOM 387: Media Production Lab (4 units)

  • HCOM 487S: Community Media Project SL (6 units)

MLO 4 Philosophical analysis

Ability to understand why and how beliefs, values, assumptions and communication practices interact to shape ways of being and knowing

Required Courses

  • Complete one of the following
  • HCOM 301: Ways Of Knowing (4 units)
  • HCOM 404: Restorative Justice (4 units)
  • HCOM 405: Philosophy & Sexualities (4 units)
  • HCOM 406: Philosophy According to Movies (4 units)

Ability to interact ethically and effectively in interpersonal and group communication and decision-making processes

  • HCOM 306: Gender & Communication (4 units)
  • HCOM 309: Intercultural Communication (4 units)
  • HCOM 310: Free Speech & Resp (4 units)
  • HCOM 403: Ethical Issues (4 units)

  • HCOM 403S: Ethics in the Professions:SL (6 units)
  • HCOM 412: Multicultural Conflict Resolut (4 units)
