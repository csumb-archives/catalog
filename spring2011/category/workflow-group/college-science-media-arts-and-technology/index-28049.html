Science, Media Arts & Technology

The College of Science, Media Arts and Technology's  mission is to empower the CSUMB learning community by providing:

  • A broad-based, integrated approach to the study of science, technology, and mathematics
  • Application of technology in the study of digital design and interactive media
  • A framework for making informed and ethical choices in the information age

Communication Design

In this digital age, no matter your career path, your employment options will likely increase if you have web, instructional, animation, publication, or multimedia design skills. Even if you can't use the skills at work, they will serve you well in your personal pursuits. The Communication Design Minor lets you pursue your particular design interests as an added dimension of your academic preparation.

Computer Science

Whatever your major is, a minor in Computer Science and Information Systems will  open many doors for you and greatly expand your career options. Whether you want to learn to develop or adapt software, create new computer applications, understand  networks, or master information systems including databases, the Computer Science & Information Systems Minor provides the opportunity.

A bachelor's degree in mathematics will help you lay the groundwork for your future in business, education, engineering, government service, science, or virtually any career. As a mathematics major at CSUMB, the faculty will guide you on a journey into a deep understanding of mathematics. Your mathematical knowledge will allow you to take a problem, abstract its essential features, and provide a framework for finding the best possible solution.
