Earth Systems Science

Regardless of your major, the Earth Systems Science minor gives you the opportunity to develop a solid understanding of the natural world around you so that you can make scientifically informed decisions in your career or everyday life. If you enjoy science but prefer a less mathematical approach than required of a science major, you will appreciate this minor.
