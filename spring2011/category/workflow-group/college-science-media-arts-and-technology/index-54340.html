Teacher Preparation

The Teacher Preparation concentration is aligned with the California Commission on Teacher Credentialing's standards for middle school and high school biology teachers.

Premedical

The Premedical concentration is designed to fulfill admissions requirements for medical, pharmacy, or veterinary school, and is intended to serve students interested in going on to professional school in the health sciences.

General Biology

The General Biology concentration is designed for students with an interest any area of biology other than health sciences or preparing to be a secondary biology teacher. Students in the General Biology concentration can choose electives to focus on ecology, molecular biology, marine biology, or organismal biology, in preparation for either graduate school or working in a biology-related field after graduation

Related Careers

MSMIT graduates have entered and achieved notable success in industry in areas such as web development, software engineering, project management, and IT management.

Some work in small companies. Others work for large corporations including Apple, Cisco Systems, and Lockheed. Some work for government agencies such as the Department of Defense. Others are employed in universities, and in K-12 education. Several have started their own businesses.

Advising

MSMIT provides you with a complete analysis of your academic preparation that serves as the basis for the program.

Program faculty members conduct ongoing advising on an as-needed basis.. They maintain office hours that you can use to access appropriate course and academic advising either in person or by telephone. All faculty members also extensively use email for advising.

MSMIT also provides peer advising specific to the program.


Preparation

MSMIT students come from a variety of academic backgrounds. To ensure that each student is adequately prepared for the academic rigors of our graduate level curriculum, we require the following competencies as a foundation:

  • Management/Marketing
  • Finance/Accounting
  • Information Systems
  • Basic Computer Programming/Basic Web Design

You should prepare for the program by completing either the GRE or GMAT.
