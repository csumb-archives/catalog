Biology

Biology is one of the most versatile fields of study you can pursue. The biology minor enhances any major and opens the door to many opportunities for graduate study and potential careers. You'll gain a fundamental understanding of core biology concepts and apply that knowledge to electives that fit your academic and career goals.

Env. Health Policy

The Enviromental Health Policy minor is an asset to students interested in working with local and state environmental health agencies and community-based environmental health advocacy groups who work to improve environmental conditions and community health through program development, policy change and advocacy. This minor incorporates courses and learning outcomes from the Environmental Science, Technology & Policy and Collaborative Health and Human Services majors, but is open to all majors across campus.
