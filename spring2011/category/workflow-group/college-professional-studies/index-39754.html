Wellness Freshman Pathway

Pathway Type: 
Freshmen
Major: 
Kinesiology

These pathways serve as tools, not prescribed programs of study. Before enrolling, you can use them to get a feel for what you can do in this program. After enrolling, you and your advisors can use them as a starting point for building your personalized outcomes-based ILP.

This pathway, intended as a reference, shows a productive route to graduation in four years. Your actual pathway may not look exactly like the model due to several factors:

* Any required remedial courses, such as MATH 098 and WRT 095

* Actual availability of courses during a given semester

Marketing Transfer Pathway

Pathway Type: 
Transfer
Major: 
Business Admin

This pathway assumes that prior to entering CSUMB, the student has passed not only all the lower division General Education requirements but also the following classes that are pre-requisites for upper division Business classes:

  1. An Information Technology class with Library Research and extensive Excel content
  2. Macro and Micro Economics
  3. Financial and Managerial Accounting
  4. Business Law
  5. Pre-Calculus or Finite Math I
  6. Statistics
  7. 2nd level of a foreign language
