Overview

The CHHS major provides you with the essential knowledge, skills and abilities needed to become a future leader in health and social service organizations. It also prepares you for entry into graduate programs in public health, social work, public administration, nonprofit management, and related allied health and social service professions.

Professions in kinesiology are among the fastest growing in the world. Individuals, corporations and government entities have come to recognize that the health and wellbeing of individuals creates the foundation for the success of our society and economy. By providing you with broad knowledge, skills and abilities, our Kinesiology faculty and staff will help prepare you to pursue a number of highly demanded and enjoyable professions.

Outdoor Recreation

No place in the world can beat the Monterey Bay region when it comes to diverse outdoor recreation opportunities. Our Outdoor Education minor leverages our natural resources to allow you to hone your outdoor and recreation knowledge and skills in preparation for a variety of careers and a lifetime of enjoyment.

For additional information please visit the Kinesiology Department website.
