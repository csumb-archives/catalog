Executive M.B.A.

Are you a fully employed business professional ready to accelerate into an executive management position? Through online courses, the Executive M.B.A. program allows you to complete 48 units of required courses within 24 months. A fixed curriculum, graduate business cohort program, the EMBA offers courses through Extended Education in fall, winter, spring, and summer terms. When admitted you become a member of an approximately 25-student cohort that progresses through the program in a lock-step manner.
