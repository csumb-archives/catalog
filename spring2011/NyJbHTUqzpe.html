The Undergraduate Advising Center actively assists students during their freshmen and sophomore years with course selection based on their academic and career goals, university requirements and pathways from first semester's entrance to graduation. The center's staff also assists upper-division students when departmental advising is not available. Students using the Undergraduate Advising Center can become knowledgeable of the campus resources available to help them become self-reliant navigators of their academic and career objectives.
