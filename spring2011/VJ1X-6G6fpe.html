Applying for Financial Aid does not guarantee an extension of housing, meal plan, or registration payment.  To qualify for an extension of registration payment, students must accept the Federal Student Loan awarded on CMS prior to July 30, 2011 if the loan is needed to pay the tuition balance. This fee payment extension expires and all outstanding charges are due when all financial aid awards are disbursed to your student account, but no later than the add/drop date of each semester. If university charges are outstanding after these deadlines, late charges will be assessed and holds will be placed on your student record.
