FREN 101: Begin French I Assessment

Description
Alternative assessment for the first semester of French. Freshmen and sophomore transfers attempting to satisfy the Language ULR through assessment must enroll in FREN 101, 102, 201, and 202 concurrently, except for international students who may enroll only in FREN 202. Junior and senior transfers attempting to satisfy the Language ULR through assessment, must enroll in FREN 101, 102, and 201, except for international students who may enroll only in FREN 201 or 202.

4
