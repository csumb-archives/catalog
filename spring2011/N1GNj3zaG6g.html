Outcome 1: Students communicate in a designated language other than English with native speakers of the target language.

Outcome 2: Students describe and appreciate cultural customs, practices, products, and perspectives.

Outcome 3: Students compare their own culture with another culture with respect to customs, practices, products, and perspectives.
