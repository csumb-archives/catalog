Graduates who have completed the Business Administration minor in conjunction with a CSUMB major have entered and achieved notable success in the following careers: advertising, finance, management/management training, marketing, nonprofit management, retail management, sales and small business startup.

The required coursework for Business minors gives students the opportunity to gain valuable business skills which apply to many career paths. Students can expect to benefit from the following learning outcomes:

  • Leadership and Management including Human Resources or Hotel/ Resort Operations
  • Marketing including Marketing Research or Global Marketing
  • Finance including Accounting or Income Tax
  • Management Information Systems including Database Management and E-Commerce
  • Entrepreneurship including Product Management for Start-ups and Venture Formation
