Campus History

Though just over a decade old, like the Army base that preceded it, CSUMB has already compiled a rich history. As part of the university's tenth anniversary celebration, both the base and campus histories have been chronicled on the CSUMB History website, the former as a very brief sketch and the latter with some detail.
