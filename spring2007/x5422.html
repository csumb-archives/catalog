Fall 2006

Aug 25      

 Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses on-line and receive full refund of mandatory fees. Last day to register without a $25.00 late registration fee.

Fall courses begin.

ADD / DROP two-week period begins (Aug. 28- Sept. 11).

Aug. 28

to

Sept 1

Students will be able to ADD courses online through the first week of classes.

Campus Service Center will continue to accept Schedule Adjustments Forms (Add Forms) during this time.

Students can drop courses online.

Sept 5

to

Sept 11

Students must ADD classes at the Campus Service Center with approved Schedule Adjustment Forms.

Students can continue to drop courses online.

 

Sept 11

 

Last day to add/drop courses without petition and a “W” (withdraw) grade for Fall semester.

Last day to change grade mode to CR/NC.

Last day to officially take a Leave of Absence for the Fall  2006.

Last day to drop from full-time to part-time and receive a full refund of the state fee difference (nonresident students also receive full refund of tuition).

 
 

Sept 12

 

First day to withdraw from courses for serious and compelling reason (“W” grade assigned). Must submit supporting documentation.

Last day for Open University students to drop and receive a pro-rated refund.
