> Payroll

Payroll Services

Payroll Services ensures timely and accurate payroll for state employees. Payroll handles the following:

  • Pay for faculty, staff, and students.
  • Disability payments
  • Leave Accounting usage and accrual tracking
  • Clearance/Separation payments
  • Payroll reports
