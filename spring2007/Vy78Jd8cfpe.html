Adventure Connections

Adventure Connections offers outings and equipment rentals for sailing, kayaking, surfing, boogie boarding, rafting, scuba diving, snorkeling, cycling, hiking, rock climbing, inline skating, camping, fishing, and skiing. The program also offers sailing instruction and certification.
