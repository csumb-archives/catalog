MPP Orientation ProSeminar

MPP Orientaion ProSeminar is a blended classroom, case- study, and web based learning session to help ensure successful, consistent understanding of the 'hows' and 'whys' of  managing at CSUMB.

Outcomes

  • Awareness and understanding of the conditions placed on managers in the CSUMB collective bargaining environment.
  • Understanding of the relevant laws and routine practices at CSUMB regarding federal and state labor regulations.
  • An appreciation for and strategies to engage in effective performance management, including employee recognition, development, evaluation, coaching, counseling, record keeping, and/ or reward.
