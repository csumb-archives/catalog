Social and Behavioral Sciences, B.A. ~ Learning Environment

Our crosscultural faculty, staff, and student populations place you directly into a real-life laboratory that  mirrors the demographics of a globalized future.

You will find that the surrounding Monterey Bay and Salinas Valley present a broad geographical blend of cultures. The rapid changes experienced by the surrounding communities provide ample opportunities for cultural experiences. At  the same time, if you prefer a more rural environment, you will find that as well. We have the natural wonders of the Pacific Ocean,  Big Sur, and the valley  farmlands. Unlike a crowded intercity campus, our CSUMB campus is slightly out of the way with thousands of acres of open land surrounding our classrooms and housing. 

Our small interactive classes are conducted in spacious modern facilities featuring leading-edge multimedia, telecommunications, and computer technologies that allow instant access to social and behavioral information across the globe.
