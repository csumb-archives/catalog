Liberal Studies B.A. Liberal Learning Pathway ~ Requirements

Semester Credits Required

Total credits: 120

Upper-division credits: 60

Credits in residence at CSUMB: 30 (24 upper-division)

Credits in the major: 40 (28 upper-division)

Outcomes, Courses, and Assessment

You officially begin the major by enrolling in LL 300 Major Proseminar where you work with your Liberal Learning advising specialist to create or revise your Individual Learning Plan.  The ILP maps out how you will achieve each of the Major Learning Outcomes (MLOs) designated below by successfully completing the listed courses or alternative assessment options. You conclude the major by completing your Capstone, which displays your cumulative learning in the major.

Domain Area A: Orientation and Core Perspectives in the Liberal Learning Pathway ****

LL MLO A1 Completion of LL 300 Major ProSeminar

  • Complete LL300 
Domain Area B: Diversity and Multiculturalism

LL MLO B1 Cross-Cultural Competence

  • Complete LL 391 

LL  MLO B2 Single Ethnic/Cultural Group Awareness

  • Complete one of the following: HCOM 309, HCOM 322, HCOM 324, HCOM 328, HCOM 329, HCOM 337, HCOM 342, HCOM 344, HCOM 345, HCOM 346, HCOM 395, HCOM 443, JAPN 305, JAPN 306, LS 362, SBSC 284/384, SBSC 248/348, SBSC 269/369, SBSC 287/387, SBSC 288/388, SBSC 290/390, SBSC 291/391, SBSC 292/392, SPAN 308, SPAN 365, SPAN 370, SPAN 425, SPAN 426, SPAN 427, CHHS 360, LL 324

LL MLO B3 Global Competence

  • Complete one of the following: GS 314, GS 310, GS 320, GS 370, CHHS 360, SBSC 371, SBSC 327, SBSC 380, SBSC 356, GS 315, HCOM 356, SBSC 350 (May also meet Advanced Social Science; please see LS MLO C5)

LL MLO B4 Second Language Communication Competence

  • Complete the requirements of the Language ULR
Domain Area C: Liberal Learning Across the Disciplines

LL MLO C1 English Communication, Foundational

  • Complete the requirements of the English Communication A ULR at the lower-division level, or HCOM 211
  • Complete the requirements of the English Communication B ULR at the lower-division level, or one of the following: HCOM 213, HCOM 206, HCOM 212, ESSP 383, ESSP 384S
  • Complete the requirements of the Ethics ULR at the lower-division level, or one of the following: ESSP 380, ESSP 383, GS 195, GS 330, HCOM 213, HCOM 206, HCOM 260, JAPN 306, SBSC 284S/384S, TAT 370, VPA 308  

LL MLO C2 English Communication, Advanced

  • Complete one of the following: HCOM 304, HCOM 312, HCOM 317S, HCOM 330, HCOM 339S,  ESSP 383
  • Complete the Graduation Writing Assessment Requirement (GWAR)

LL  MLO C3 Literary Analysis and Criticism in Multicultural Literature

  • Complete one of the following: HCOM 325, HCOM 327, HCOM 328, HCOM 335, HCOM 336, HCOM 348,
    LS  323, LS 324, LS 394S, SPAN 310, SPAN 428

LL MLO C4 Technology Communication

  • Complete one of the following: CST 101, CST 102

LL MLO C4 Advanced Technology

  • Complete one of the following: CST 303, SBSC 318 (Other upper-division Advanced Technology courses are acceptable.)

LL MLO C5 Social Science

  • Complete the requirements of the U.S. Histories ULR at the lower-division level, or one of the following: GS 200S, HCOM 240, HCOM 267, HCOM 253, SBSC 212
  • Complete the requirements of the Democratic Participation ULR  at the lower-division level, or one of the following: GS 200S, HCOM 260, HCOM 267, SBSC 212
  • Complete an Advanced Social Science from the 300-level courses listed under LS MLO B3

LL MLO C6 Quantitative Literacy

  • Complete the requirements of the Mathematics Communication ULR  at the lower-division level, or one of the following: MATH 100, MATH 130, MATH 155, MATH 156, MATH 170, STAT 200, STAT 201 & STAT 202 (must complete both), STAT 204

LL MLO C7 Natural Sciences

  • Complete the requirements of the Science ULR  including one Methods course and one Content course

LL MLO C8 Visual and Performing Arts

  • Complete one Art course at the lower-division level or choose from the following: MPA 233, VPA 308, other approved VPA courses
  • Complete one Drama course at the lower-division level or choose from the following: TAT 330, other approved TAT courses
  • Complete one Dance course at the lower-division level or choose from the following: MPA 233, other approved MPA courses
  • Complete one Music course at the lower-division level or choose from the following: MPA 233, other approved MPA courses

LL  MLO C9 Human Growth and Development

  • Complete one of the following: LS 351, LS 360
  • Complete the requirements of the Vibrancy Theory ULR  at the lower-division level, or one of the following: HPWE 161, HPWE 162, HPWE 260, HPWE 262, HPWE 263, HPWE 264, HPWE 361S, HPWE 201
  • Complete the requirements of the Vibrancy Activity ULR  at the lower-division level, or any one of the numerous 1-credit (or more) HPWE activity labs or TAT 355

LL MLO C10 Liberal Learning Core

  • Complete 16 upper-division credits of LL core courses

LL MLO C11 Subject Area Emphasis

  • Complete 12 upper-division credits following an approved minor or concentration (Refer to specific department’s recommendations for concentrations or minors. May not be courses that were completed in the core LS MLOs. Grade in each course must be C or better. A grade of credit is not acceptable. See Liberal Studies Advising webpage for approved minors and concentrations.)
Domain Area D: Ethical Reflection and Social Responsibility

LL MLO D1 Field-based Service Learning

  • Complete HCOM 317S, HCOM 339S, or another upper-division service learning course (Must complete a minimum of 50 service hours.)
Domain Area E: Synthesis and Assessment in the Major

LL MLO E1 LL 400 Senior Capstone

  • Complete LL 400 (Maximum courseload in addition to LL 400 is 12 credits.)
