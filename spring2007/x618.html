Earth Systems Science & Policy B.S. ~ Home

If you have an interest in science or technology, care about people and the environment, and want to pursue a career or advanced graduate study in environmental science, resource management, habitat restoration, marine science, science education, scientific research, or related fields, then this is the CSUMB major for you!

Because of ESSP's emphasis on practical, real-world learning, ESSP graduates have been highly successful at getting great jobs and gaining admission to some of the nation's top graduate and professional schools.

You will find all of the official information about the Earth Systems Science & Policy major right here. Simply use the left-side navigation.

For more detailed information including a look at student work, faculty bios, research partnerships, and internship opportunities, please visit the Earth Systems Science & Policy major website.
