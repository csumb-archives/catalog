International Health Policy Minor ~ Learning Environment

The Monterey Bay region's diverse mix of communities and cultures, health and human service agencies, and public policy and advocacy organizations provide exciting and challenging opportunities for your field experiences in the "real-world" classroom. You will work with experts at a community-based public health agency during your field classes.

HHSPP campus classes are small, highly interactive, and utilize all aspects of our technologically advanced campus.

CHHS offers internships, courses, and a Master of Arts in Interdisciplinary Studies/Public Policy program in conjunction with the world-renown Panetta Institute. The Central Coast is home to several other, internationally known universities where our students have found additional educational, work and graduate study opportunities. Among these are Monterey Institute of International Studies and the University of California Santa Cruz.
