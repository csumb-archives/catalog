WORLD THEATER SPONSORS

2006/2007 SEASON

 

KAZU Radio 90.3 FM is the official radio sponsor for the World Theater 2006/2007 Season.

 

The Son de Madera concert is supported in part by Radio Bilingue of Salinas, CA.

 

          

SEASIDE POST & MARINA GAZETTE NEWSPAPERS

The Josephine Baker:  A Life of Le Jazz Hot concert is supported in part by the Seaside Post and Marina Gazette Newspapers.

 

The Compangie TchéTché performance is supported in part by the National Dance Proejct of the New England Foundation of the Arts, with lead funding from the Doris Duke Charitable Foundation.   Additional funding provided by The Ford Foundation and The Andrew W. Mellon Foundation.

 

The World Theater's Arts for Kids Series is supported in part by Integrity Printing in Marina, CA.  Integrity Printing -- "Affordable, quality and reliable service."

 

The World Theater's Arts for Kids Series is supported in part by The Pebble Beach Company Foundation.  The Foundation distributes annual grants to educational organizations and activities that benefit the youth of Monterey County. In addition to grants to local schools and cultural, environmantal and athletic organizations, the Foundation also funds scholarships for children of Pebble Beach Company employees for their post-secondary education.

 

The Nai-Ni Chen Dance Company residency is supported in part by the Cannery Row Company.

The Arts for Kids Series is supported in part by the UPJOHN California Fund.
