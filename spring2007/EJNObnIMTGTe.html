Undergraduate Majors and Concentrations

To give you an edge in the 21st-century workplace by arming you with broad and adaptable knowledge, skills, abilities, and attitudes, at CSUMB we have replaced traditional majors with dynamic multiple-focus degree programs. Each CSUMB major integrates several traditional fields of study to give you the breadth of knowledge and depth of skills so highly valued by employers. Many of the majors incorporate concentrations that also allow you to focus your studies within the broader interdisciplinary context.

In this section, you can use the left navigation to browse through our majors to find those matching your specific interests. Alternatively, you can use our Career Preparation Quickmatch Guide to look up career fields and see which CSUMB degree programs will prepare you for those fields.

To focus on outcomes, each of the CSUMB majors requires students to display achievement of a set of Major Learning Outcomes established by the program faculty. The description of each major includes a list of these MLOs and associated coursework on the Requirements page.
