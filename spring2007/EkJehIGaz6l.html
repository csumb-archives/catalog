> Ticket Order Forms

World Theater Performing Arts Series

Online Order Form Available Now!  Click here (pdf file format).

For your convenience, there are various ways to purchase tickets for the WORLD THEATER PERFORMING ARTS SERIES.

By Phone:  With Mastercard, VISA, American Express or Discover.  Call the World Theater box office at (831) 582-4580.  The box office is open Monday - Friday, 12 noon - 4:00pm.

By Mail:  Complete an order form.  Enclose a check or money order made payable to CSUMB World Theater or indicate credit card payment.  Mail to:  CSUMB, 100 Campus Center, Bldg. #28, Seaside, CA, 93955-8001.

By Fax:  Send a completed order form to (831) 582-4582.

In Person:  Stop by the World Theater box office located on the campus of CSU Monterey Bay.  The box office is open Monday – Friday, 12:00 P.M. – 4:00 P.M., while the University is in session.  Short-term parking is available in the lot adjacent to the theater near the north side of the building.

Online: Tickets may be purchased online at www.TicketGuys.com or by clicking on this logo whenever you see it throughout the World Theater website .

Online ticket availability may be limited. No online sales day of show. All tickets ordered online will be held at the World Theater box office.  Proper I.D. is required for ticket pick up.

All phone, mail, and fax orders will be charged an additional $2 processing fee for each order.  Online sales are subject to convenience fees as well.

Please note that tickets orders received by mail, phone, or fax less than three weeks prior to the event will not be mailed, but will be held at the box office.  Proper I.D. is required for ticket pick up.

Please check your tickets carefully!  Curtain times and days for performances vary.
