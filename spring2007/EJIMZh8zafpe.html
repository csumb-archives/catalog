Human Resources Series

This on-going series takes an closer look at the resources and information available to employees from University Human Resources.  Topics for 2006-2007:

~Performance Management and Evaluation - Goal setting; tracking your performance; the performance evaluation conversation; web-tour of relevant web resources

~De-mystifying Compensation & Classification - Classification standards; job evaluation process; in-range/in-class progression; compensation guidelines; Q & A

~Benefits Tune-up - Retirement plans; worker's comp & leaves; HCRA/DCRA; life insurance options; TSA (401k; 403b; 457 plans)

~Payroll from A-Z - Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA 'exempt' vs 'non-exempt'); absence and attendance reporting; compliance with state and federal laws

~How to Hire Like a Pro - Background checking; best practices in employee selection; behavioral interviewing; HR/legal/bargaining unit issues
