Foundation

The Foundation of California State University, Monterey Bay was established in 1994 to support and advance the vision and educational, research, and community service objectives of CSUMB. The Foundation is a 501(c)(3) public benefit corporation and a recognized auxiliary of the CSU system. An integral component of the university, the principal activities of the Foundation include operation of the housing program for students, faculty, and staff; commercial services, conferencing, and other projects with revenue-producing potential; administration of research grants and contracts; and fund development activities.

EEO/AA

The Foundation supports and is committed to the principle of equal employment opportunity. In addition, the Foundation provides an environment that is free of unlawful harassment. For additional information, refer to the non-discrimination policy in the Employee Handbook.

Payroll

Foundation employees are paid semi-monthly according to the published payroll calendar. Each paycheck will include earnings for all work performed through the end of the previous pay period. Refer to the Employee Handbook for additional information.
