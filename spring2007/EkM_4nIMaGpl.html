To use the chart above you need to identify your current academic semester. An "X" identifies 1/6 of the semester.

Actual paydays will normally be the regular University payday. As a general rule, all faculty appointed for only semester will receive their final paycheck on the normal payday for the month in which the semester ends.

In addition, all final payment schedules are dependent on a timely submission of documentation from the department.

Questions should be directed to your Payroll Technician.
