Imani Winds
Wednesday, October 25, 2006, 7:30 P.M.

Featuring five outstanding musicians, Imani Winds joined forces to create an ensemble that would push the boundaries of a traditional classical music wind quintet. “Imani,” literally “faith” in Swahili, is the embodiment of the ensemble’s mission:  bridging the European, American and African musical traditions, exploring repertoire from diverse cultural backgrounds and reflecting upon their rich experience as classical musicians.

 


Salome Jens, actress
Tuesday, November 7, 2006, time TBD

Join actress Salome Jens and Gavilan College students during an interview session in which Ms. Jens discusses her career as a television, stage and screen artist.

 

 


ANDES MANTA
Wednesday, May 2, 2007, 7:30 P.M.


Rooted in the cultural heritage of the Incas, the musicians of Andes Manta perform the vibrant music of the Andean mountains of South America. From the lyrical sounds of the quena, or Andean flute, to the haunting tones of six-foot long pan pipes, the music of Andes Manta is an experience that never fails to bring audiences to their feet.
