Screenshots

 

On the PERSONAL tab, you are able to keep up your contact information. Advancing to the next tab saves your data. These would be available for any additional job you apply for at CSUMB. Note that if this section is completed, a check appears on the tab.


 

On the GENERAL tab, you are able to specify information concerning your employment and availability. These data are all saved for any future applications.

 

 

The EDUCATION tab allows you to enter your education history. Additionally, you can selectively add employment histories from any previous CSUMB applications.


 On the EMPLOYMENT/RESUME tab, you are able to enter data regarding your employment history. Once again, you can selectively add your histories from previous applications.


At the bottom the EMPLOYMENT/RESUME page, you are able to upload a file of your resume using Word or other formats.


 The SKILLS tab allows you to provide data concerning languages and computing skills. These data are all saved for any future applications.


 On the REVIEW tab, our system checks that you have completed the required fields prior to your submission. In the event something was missing, you would still be able to make those changes before your final submission.
