Staff and Management

University Human Resources provides a variety of services to staff and management

Recruitment & Compensation - Recruits a diverse and highly qualified staff and assists managers in meeting their staffing needs. Administers CSUMB's compensation program.

The recruitment process for staff and administrative employees at CSUMB incorporates all federal and state regulations, CSU collective bargaining agreements, CSU system policies, and university policies. Additional information can be found in the Staff Recruitment Protocol.

  • Job Description Template (Word)

Salary schedules and classifications structures for CSUMB employees are set by the Chancellor's office. For more information refer to the following sites:

  • CSU Salary Schedules

Benefits

Medical benefits for state and foundation employees are similar and administered by CalPERS. Dental, vision, life, retirement and other benefits are different for CSUMB and Foundation. For additional information refer to the State Benefits  or Foundation web pages.

Payroll

Payroll Services for State and Foundation employees are handled separately and there are different cut-off and pay dates. Links to the respective payroll calendars are shown below; for more information, refer to the Payroll web page.
