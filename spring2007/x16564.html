Panel Discussion on Healthy Living & Longevity
11am - 12pm       Bld. 29 Living Room  

Learn various tips on living a long and healthy life from our distinguished panelists, presenting on various topics listed below.  A time of Q&A will wrap up the discussion, allowing attendees to ask their specific questions to the panelists.

"Die Young, as Old as Possible"  
Dr. Kent Adams, Professor of Human Performance and Wellness at CSUMB

The benefits of strength training for Aging and Quality of Life

"Why I run…because I'd have to get up too early to walk"
bobbi bonace, Director of Human Performance and Wellness Education at CSUMB

The mental, spiritual, and emotional sides to working out

"Preventing Diabetes"
Chesney Hoagland-Fuchs, BSN, RN, CDE 
Diabetes Educator at Natividad Medical Center

Diabetes, Weight Loss and Healthy Eating

"The Power to Choose Daily: Easy Ways to Move Towards Vibrant Health Today"
Ana Poirier, Certified Nutritional Health Counselor, Vegetarian Chef, Yoga Instructor, and Body Worker at Energia Body Centre & Day Spa

Baby steps are the only way to make  long lasting changes in one's diet.  The all or nothing approach sets one up for failure.  Ana will offer some simple and general suggestions for people to incorporate into their daily lives.

Introduction to the Savings Plus Program 
12 - 1pm     Bld. 29, Rms. 115 & 116

Mark Hinte, Nationwide Retirement Specialist for the Savings Plus Program of California

For the first time ever, a representative from the Savings Plus Program will be onsite to give an introduction to the Savings Plus Program.  Learn how tax deferred savings will help you reach your retirement goals, why you should save for retirement, the advantage of tax-deferred savings, and how to enroll in the Savings Plus Program.   Additionally, this seminar will illustrate the differences between 401k, 457 and 403b, and will help you choose the right plan for your retirement.

Be informed about this CSU voluntary retirement option.  Plan on attending!

 

Life, Voluntary Life, and Long Term Disability Insurance
1 - 1:30pm     Bld. 29 Living Room

Darin Plotnick, CSU Account Manager at Standard Insurance

This seminar will focus on the CSU-paid Basic Term Life Insurance benefit, discussing the various amounts available by bargaining unit to benefit -eligible employees as well as employee- paid Voluntary Life Insurance, going over the options available for both employees and dependents.  They will also go over the options if someone were to leave employment.
