Helpful Information

CSU Monterey World Theater
On Sixth Avenue

Box Office 831-582-4580

Information and Box Office Hours
The World Theater box office is located in the front of the building facing Sixth Avenue on the campus of CSU Monterey Bay.  Box office hours are Monday – Friday, 12:00 PM. – 4:00 P.M.  For performances, the box office is open one hour before curtain through intermission.

  • Emergency calls during performance events can be placed to the numbers listed above.
  • Regular box office hours may vary during academic breaks.

Ticket Orders and Reservations
All phone, mail, and fax orders will be charged an additional $2 processing fee for each order.

Please note that tickets orders received by mail, phone, or fax less than three weeks prior to the event will not be mailed, but will be held at the box office.  Proper I.D. is required for ticket pick up.

By Phone:  With Mastercard, VISA, American Express or Discover.  Call the World Theater box office at (831) 582-4580.

By Mail:  Complete the attached form.  Enclose a check or money order made payable to CSUMB World Theater or indicate credit card payment.  Mail to:  CSUMB, 100 Campus Center, Bldg. #28, Seaside, CA, 93955-8001.

By Fax:  Send a completed form to (831) 582-4582.

In Person:  Stop by the World Theater box office located on the campus of CSU Monterey Bay.  The box office is open Monday – Friday, 12:00 P.M. – 4:00 P.M., while the University is in session.  Short-term parking is available in the lot adjacent to the theater near the north side of the building.

Online: Tickets may be purchased online at www.TicketGuys.com. Online sales are subject to an additional convenience fee. Online ticket availability may be limited. No online sales day of show. All tickets ordered online will be held at the World Theater box office.  Proper I.D. is required for ticket pick up.

Please check your tickets carefully!  Curtain times and days for performances vary.


Refunds/Exchanges

  • All sales are final. 
  • Ticket refunds are issued only in case of an event cancellation. 
  • Tickets cannot be exchanged. 
  • Processing fees are non-refundable, even in the event of an event cancellation.

House Opening/Seating and Restrictions

  • The house will be open one-half hour before curtain with the approval of the performers.
  • All seats are reserved for events in this brochure.
  • Special seating needs will be accommodated five minutes before house opening.
  • Latecomers will be seated at the discretion of the management.
  • Cameras, recording devices, pagers, cell phones, smoking, food and beverages are strictly prohibited in all performance areas.

Patron Categories

General Public:  all patrons who do not qualify for discount or full-time CSUMB student tickets.

Discount:   available for seniors (age 55 and older), CSUMB faculty and staff, Fort Ord Alumni Association, CSUMB Alumni Association, and military personnel. Identification required for all discount tickets and some limits and restrictions may apply.

Student:  current students at CSUMB receive a maximum of two tickets per event.  These tickets are not transferable and are to be used by the student who purchases them.  Identification required and some limits and restrictions may apply.

Group Sales:  We love groups!  Come with family, friends, business associates or club members.  A discount is offered for groups of 20 or more and tickets are based on availability. Call the box office manager at (831) 582-4580 to make arrangements.

Oops! I Bought The Whole Thing:  Treat yourself to a ticket for each show in this brochure for $170 – a savings of $45 off the General Public ticket price. Tickets to all nine shows must be purchased at the same time. No substitutions or partial-season orders accepted. No refunds will be given for unused tickets and tickets are not transferable. Deadline for purchasing the Oops! package is September 30, 2004.


Disability Services:

Wheelchair accessible:  The World Theater is equipped with ramps and wheelchair and companion seating on the right and left sides of the house.  All wheelchair spaces can be accessed from the lobby of the theater.  Accessible restrooms are located in the lobby.  Handicapped parking is located in the parking lot adjacent to the World Theater on the north side of the building.    Please indicate the number of wheelchair seats needed on your ticket order form so that we may properly seat you in the facility.

Phonic Ear Sound Enhancement:  The World Theater is equipped with an Assisted Listening System to aid with sound amplification and clarity.  Headphones are available in the lobby free of charge.  A credit card, driver’s license or valid student ID will be held as security while headphones are in use.

Sign interpreters:  The World Theater will provide sign interpreters for patrons who request services.  All requests for sign interpreters must be made ten (10) business days prior to performance.  Please advise the box office at the time of your purchase for appropriate seating.

TTY/TDD:  The World Theater is equipped with a TTY/TDD machine.  You can contact us at (831) 582-5066 to access this device.

 

Parking and Travel Directions
Parking in designated areas is free for all patrons attending Performing arts Series events at the World Theater.  During normal business hours, parking passes are required.  We are located 20- minutes from Carmel and 20-minutes from Salinas.
