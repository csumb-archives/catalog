Visual and Public Art B.A. ~ Home

Our Visual and Public Art major develops your individual skills and ability to collaborate through hands-on projects  where you solve problems, use technology, and apply your art. Come join us as we create public art that manifests the dreams and hopes of communities in magical, intangible ways; makes invisible members of society visible; and bridges cultural, racial, and social divisions by providing meaningful collective experiences.

You will find all of the official information about the Visual and Public Art major right here. Simply use the left-side navigation.

For more detailed information including galleries of student work, please visit the Visual and Public Art Department website.
