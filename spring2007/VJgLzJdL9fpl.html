Frequently Asked Questions

When will this new catalog site have complete information about CSUMB academic programs?

This site will provide complete current information about academic programs, services, procedures, and policies by the end of the Spring 2005 semester.

Where should I go to find what's missing from the catalog site?

Information on registration processes and fees can be found on the CSUMB.EDU/schedule website. Though potentially dated, you will find academic program information in the 2003-2005 CSUMB Catalog, available in print from the CSUMB Bookstore, or online in our Catalog Archive. A new 2005-2007 CSUMB Catalog will be available in print during summer 2005.
