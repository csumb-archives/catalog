Human Performance and Wellness, B.S. ~ Learning Environment

Along with intensive, energizing work in campus classrooms and in the lab, Human Performance and Wellness students learn by actively providing our community with the resources and knowledge necessary to make healthy lifestyle and behavioral choices.

Our learning environment includes strong partnerships with local schools, businesses, and nonprofit and governmental organizations. Our courses feature experience-based learning opportunities throughout the region.

We utilize the coastal mountains, Pacific Ocean, trails, and other prolific natural resources on the Monterey Peninsula and in the Tri-County Region as amazing outdoor classrooms.
