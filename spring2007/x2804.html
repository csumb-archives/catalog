CONSTRUCTION ALERT!!!

As of 6/14/06

Dear World Theater Patron,

As you may know, CSUMB has embarked on a project to upgrade its infrastructure.  As such, the campus will undergo construction in the next 18 months.  Please note the revised written travel directions to the World Theater below in red.  Should you have any questions or need additional information, please contact the World Theater box office at 831-582-4580 or at worldtheater@csumb.edu.  Click on image to download the map and travel directions.

 

From HIGHWAY 1:
• Take the CSU Monterey Bay exit (Fort Ord Main Entrance).
• The exit will put you on LIGHT FIGHTER DRIVE.
• Proceed down LIGHT FIGHTER DRIVE through two traffic signals.
• Turn LEFT at the third traffic signal onto GEN JIM MOORE BOULEVARD.
• Proceed on GEN JIM MOORE BOULEVARD to the first stop sign.  Turn right onto FIRST STREET.
• Proceed on FIRST STREET through two stop signs.  At the third stop sign and the end of the street, take a right on to A STREET.
• Proceed around the bend to the stop sign which is 6TH AVENUE.  Turn right onto 6TH AVENUE, the WORLD THEATER is the second building on the right.

 

From SALINAS:
• Take RESERVATION ROAD to IMJIN ROAD.  Take a left on IMJIM ROAD.
• Proceed on IMJIM ROAD through one traffic signal.  At the second signal, turn left on ABRAMS ROAD.
• Proceed on ABRAMS ROAD through one stop sign.  At the second stop sign, turn right on INTERGARRISSON ROAD.
• Proceed on INTERGARRISSON ROAD to the second 4-way stop: 6TH AVENUE. Turn left onto 6th Avenue.
• Proceed on 6TH AVENUE, go through one stop sign. The WORLD THEATER is the second building on the right.

For a map of campus showing the buildings included in the construction project as well as the streets to be impacted go to CSUMB.EDU/news.
