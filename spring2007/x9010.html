Online Recruitment System

Overview

Applicants are now able to:

  • Create and maintain their user profile
  • Re-use application data, including education and employment history, for additional jobs
  • Check what jobs an applicant has applied for
  • Attach a resume and cover letter
  • Partially complete their application, log-off and log-in the next day to complete and submit their application
  • Catch mistakes through built-in error checking prior to application submission
  • Receive timely emails regarding the status of their application

 Other useful features include:

  • Ability for selection committee to view applications online
  • Ability for committee chair to select applicants to be interviewed online
  • More user-friendly design
  • Page tabs for more efficient navigation
