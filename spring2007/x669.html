Teledramatic Arts and Technology, B.A.~ Learning Environment

TAT facilities and resources provide the infrastructure and support to realize creative and technologically innovative ideas. These include streaming media facilities; TAT-TV cable channel; a production studio with green screen; a digital media computer lab, and uncompressed edit suites on a high speed network supported by the latest software applications including editing, DVD, motion graphics, web, and 2D/3D animation production; film and digital video cameras; lighting and sound equipment; wireless and GPS-enabled PDAs and computer tablets; and projectors.

TAT presents media and technology supported  productions and screenings in various CSUMB venues. Instruction is held in classrooms and labs with media, Internet, wireless, and computer support.
