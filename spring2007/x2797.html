> Donor Opportunities

Share your love of the arts as a contributor to the

You will join a loyal group of individuals who provide annual and planned gifts to the World Theater. Your contribution enables the World Theater to support learning and rich cultural arts experiences for students and the community at large. Your gift also helps bring some of the world’s most accomplished professional artists to our community at affordable prices.

Friends’ contributions are tax-deductible as allowed by law.

FRIEND
$50 - $99

  • Priority ticketing for reserved seating

SUPPORTER
$100 - $249

  • Friend benefits plus:
  • Recognition in performance programs

SPOTLIGHT
$250 - $499

  • Support benefits plus:
  • Advance notice of World Theater events
  • Invitation to backstage tours and special
  • Meet-and-Greets with artists

Twice is nice!  Double your gift – and double your benefits – with matching gifts from your employer.

The most taxwise gifts to CSUMB’s World Theater are planned gifts. These include bequests, trusts and gift annuities, which can provide significant tax benefits and often an income to you for life. Planned gifts can be used with virtually any type of asset - securties, real estate, insurance, etc. - to make a gift now at much less net cost than you thought possible.

For more information about the Circle of Friends, planned or matching gifts, please contact Steve Weldon, Director of Planned Giving, at 831-582-3070.

Member benefits extend from July 1, 2005 through June 30, 2006.
