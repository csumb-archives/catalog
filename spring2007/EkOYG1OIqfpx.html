Telecommunications, Multimedia, and Applied Computing, B.S.~ Careers and Graduate Study

Related Careers

TMAC graduates excel in technology, design, communication, collaboration, and problem-solving. Our graduates have entered careers and achieved notable success in web design, web development, print design, animation, programming, system administration, network management and security, instructional design, instructional technology, teaching, and more.

The organizations hiring them have included Apple, Cisco Systems, Defense Manpower Data Center, CTB/McGraw Hill, The Salinas Californian, USA Today, CSUMB, and school districts. Some graduates have started their own businesses or work as independent consultants.

Graduate Study

TMAC graduates have attended a variety of graduate institutions including Naval Postgraduate School, UC Santa Cruz, and CSUMB.

They have entered and achieved notable success in the various fields of graduate study including management and information technology, computer science, information security, and instructional design.
