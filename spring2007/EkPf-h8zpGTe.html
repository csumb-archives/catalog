OPIS - Organizational Performance Improvement System

The Professional Development program invites you to be a strong advocate for learning communities by taking advantage of department-wide learning events. The professional development staff will help you focus your outcomes, clarify expectations, arrange seminar materials, and facilitate the events.  The process can take the form of a workshop, retreat, or full semester process. Here are some examples of content areas:

~Organizational Assessment - Practices, people, performance

~Client Relations Management - Explore new ways to serve our students and succeed in our mission

~Change and Opportunity - Tools for managing and succeeding during times of change

~Department Retreats & Teambuilding - Refresh, renew, re-engage
