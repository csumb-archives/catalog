Theater Tech Specifications

WORLD THEATER GRID:  For a downloadable picture, click here (pdf format - this file is somewhat large, please be patient while it downloads).

SEATING:    

  • 457 total fixed seats
  • 6 wheel chair access spaces (3 house left, 3 house right)

STAGE:   

  • Proscenium opening:   48ft. (14.63m) wide by 19ft. 6in. (5.94 m) high
  • Stage width:   70ft. (21.34m)
  • Depth to cyc:   34ft. (10.36m)
  • Grid Height:   19ft. 6in. (5.94m)
  • Wing space stage left and right:   9ft. (2.74m), legs hung at 20ft. from centerline, 4ft. (1.22m) wide backstage on stage left and stage right. 
  • Stage is a double sprung floor (Rosco dance floor available).
  • Two access ramps leading from seating area to stage:   6ft.wide (1.83m)
  • Loading dock up stage left:   8ft. 9in. wide x 10ft. high (2.67m x 3.05m) - door opening
  • Crossover is a hallway upstage of the stage.

NO FLY SYSTEM AVAILABLE

LIGHTING:

  • Strand Lighting Obsession lighting console. 125 channels, 500 cues
  • 93 - 2.4kw dimmers, dimmer per circuit.
  • House lights controlled by LBX console or not.
  • Company box stage left wing:   200 amps 3-phase

INSTRUMENTS:              

  • 108- Source 4 ellipsoidal (15 @ 50º, 60 @ 36º, 24 @ 26º and 9 @ 19º)        
  • 40 - Par 64
  • 30 - 8 in. Fresnel 
  • 12 - L & E three cell cyc lights
  • 2- Lycan SuperArc 400 spots (left and right of control booth)

Accessories:

  • 5 - Drop in irises
  • 18 - Pattern holders for Source 4 ellipsoidal
  • 30 - 10in. Barn Doors

PIPE:

  • 4 - 14ft.(4.27m) pipes/trees
  • 6 - 12ft.(3.66m) pipes/trees
  • 8 - Boom bases:  2 round bases (50 lbs.), 6 square bases (40 lbs.)

SOUND SYSTEM:
Sound Control is a Yamaha GF24/12 mixing console Yamaha 03D backup).  House Speaker Cluster is split far through/ near through not stereo.

Microphones:

  • 2 AKG SE300B - CK91,CK94 & CK98
  • 4 AKG C420 (headset)
  • 1 Audio Technica AT 4050/CM5
  • 6 Shure Beta 57A,  6 Shure Beta 58A
  • 4 Crown PCC-160
  • 6 Audio Technica AT 933 Rx/C  (hanging microphone)
  • 2 EV R-Series (wireless hand held)
  • 2 Telex FMR-450-C (wireless lavaliere)
  • 2 Shure UCI-UB (wireless lavaliere)

Processing:

  • 1 Lexicon Multi-effect processor
  • 2 Klark-Teknik Dual 1/3 Oct EQ
  • 1 Furman RR 321 Duel 1/3 Oct EQ
  • 2 DBX 1046 Quad Comperessor limiter
  • 1 DBX Quad Gate
  • 1 Symetrix 528 de-esser

Monitors:

  • 4 JBL MR-804 (2-way stage monitor 12 in.)
  • 4 JBL SR-4704 (2-way stage monitor 15 in.)

Playback Equipment:

  • 1 Denon CD/Cassette Tape Combination unit
  • 1 Sony MDS-JE630 Mini Disk Player
  • 1 Tescam DAT Recorder

Intercom:

  • Clearcom intercom system is run throughout the building.
  • 8 wired belt packs with head- sets and 6 wireless belt packs and headsets.

PROJECTION:

  • Two 35mm motion picture Projectors
  • Two 16mm motion picture projectors
  • One projection screen -15ft. x 17ft. 6in. (4.57 m X 5.33 m)
  • One Dolby CP500 Digital Cinema Processor

VIDEO:

  • One JVC DLA-M400LU, D-ILA Super Projector
  • One Sony DVD Player
  • One JVC BR-S800U Video Cassette Recorder
  • One DPS 475 Analog/Digital AV Synchronizer

SOFT GOODS:

  • Main curtain and valance are red (main curtain is motorized).
  • One white seamless cyc 51ft. 6in. x 19 ft. (15.7 m x 16.76m)
  • One mid-stage black velour traveler 69ft. x 19ft. (21.03m x5.79m).  Makes up the #2 set of legs.
  • Two pair black velour legs (makes up the #1 and #2 set of legs)
  • One upstage black velour traveler 55ft. x 19ft. (16.76m x 5.79m)
  • Four black velour borders/teasers1 ft. high

OTHER:

  • One JLG personal lift and one 8ft. A-frame ladder

DRESSING ROOMS:

  • There are two (2) chorus dressing rooms and two (2) restrooms. All rooms are connected together with the greenroom being in the middle.  All dressing rooms and the greenroom have monitors. The dressing rooms are located backstage.

WE HAVE NO SHOWER FACILTIES.
