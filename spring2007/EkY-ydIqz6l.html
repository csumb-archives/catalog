Tuition & Fees ~ Fall 2006 Payment/Refund Deadlines

 April 1, 2006 ~ First day Installment Payment Plan (IPP) contracts available for Fall 2006 

July 15, 2006 ~ Second payment due for Fall 2006 Installment Payment Plan  ~ Last day to apply for the Fall 2006 Installment Payment Plan (first and second payments due)

August 15, 2006 ~ Third and final payment due for Fall 2006 Installment Payment Plan

August 25, 2006 ~ Last day to register without a $25 late registration fee ~ Last day for students without a financial aid fee deferral to pay registration fees without a $20 late administrative fee ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses and receive a full refund of mandatory fees ~ Last day to apply for financial aid and have fees deferred for Fall 2006 semester

September 11, 2006 ~ Last day to drop from fulltime to part-time and receive a full refund of the state fee difference (nonresident students also receive full refund of tuition)

September 21, 2006 ~ Last day for Open University students to drop and receive a prorated refund

November 1, 2006 ~ Last day of Fall 2006 fee deferment for financial aid students ~Last day to have fees paid without a $20 Late Administrative Fee or $20 Late Housing Fee

November 2, 2006 ~ Last day to officially withdraw or cancel all courses and receive a prorated refund of mandatory fees
