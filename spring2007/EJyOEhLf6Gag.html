Payroll News

Summer Employment 2006

May 31, 2006

All students who are working and not enrolled in 6 or more units, will be appointed to an 1874 position. This requires that they pay 7.5% into Retirement and 1.45% into Medicare. Students in this classification are also allowed to work up to a maximum of 40 hours per week. There are no exceptions to this and they will not be paid overtime.

Students enrolled in 6 or more units will continue to be paid as regular 1870 student assistants. These students continue to work up to an average of 20 hours per week.

Students who are paid as an 1874 classification, may withdraw the amount of the retirement deducted. Students should read and complete page 3 of the PST Benefit Payment Booklet. The form must be mailed to address at the bottom of page 3. Payments are made 90-120 days following the last date of contribution.

This amount may be withdrawn following the beginning of the fall semester.
