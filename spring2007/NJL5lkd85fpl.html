2006-2007 Professional Development Calendar

Professional Develpment believes in fostering a learning organiztion  based on the following principles.

Professional Development  -

~contributes to a deeper understanding and practice of the core values of multiculturalism, mutual respect, collaboration, institutional excellence and civic engagement

~builds a culture of learning for all members of the institution

~responds to real issues and challenges of institutional and social change

~promotes lifelong learning

The monthly listings on the left will link to course and program descriptions. 

To register click here and follow the instructions below:

  1. Log into PeopleSoft CMS-HR using your network login. (no User ID? email CMSquestions and request an HR User ID)
  2. From the menu, select MB Self Service > Personal Portfolio> Home>Personal Portfolio>Training Summary> You can then search for course sessions and enroll
  3. Click on search by date, enter a date range, and click search.
  4. Click on view sessions available for the course you want.
  5. Select a session number
  6. Click the Enroll button.  You will see a confirmation page when you click OK
