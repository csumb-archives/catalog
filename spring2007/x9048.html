Wednesday, September 20th

University Center

 

 

  • 2006 Open Enrollment: September 1st - October 6th
  • Blood Drive
  • Vendors and Raffle Prizes

  • The Year of Longevity and the Paper Crane

University Human Resources invites all Staff, Faculty, and their families to CSUMB's Benefits & Wellness Fair, Wednesday, September 20th from 11am - 2pm in the University Center. The purpose of this fair is to:

1. Showcase CSUMB's benefit programs and providers,

2. Provide Open Enrollment information and assist employees in making plan changes including HCRA/ DCRA enrollments, as well as

3. Highlight the different programs and companies in the Monterey County area that promote healthy lifestyles, this year totaling 45+ vendor participants!

This year, we will be having an Asian New Year's festival, celebrating "The Year of Longevity." As in many New Year's celebrations, the focus of this year's Fair is renewal where our goal is to encourage employees and their families to make, renew, and/or maintain healthy lifestyle habits. To assist in accomplishing this goal, we have FREE massages, seminars, food, and raffle prizes! We'll even have an origami paper crane-making station where employees can make a paper crane, symbolizing the new and/or existing healthy habits in their lives.

New this year, we will have a blood drive courtesy of the CHOMP Blood Center and the First Annual Workplace Walk-off Challenge, where teams of five compete to collectively walk the most steps, earning the title of "Workplace Walk-off Challenge Champions."

Blood Drive

The Community Hospital of the Monterey Peninsula (CHOMP) mobile donor unit will be on campus during the Benefits & Wellness Fair to draw whole blood.  Blood donation is an important way for healthy people to help their community by becoming volunteer blood donors.  Please stop by the "blood mobile" anytime between 11am and 2pm, parked in front of the University Center and become a much needed volunteer blood donor!

FREE Seminars

Back by popular demand, TIAA-CREF will be conducting FREE one-on-one financial counseling by appointment only, Monday, 9/18/06 and Tuesday, 9/19/06.  Likewise, there will be several FREE seminars never conducted at CSUMB before which are open to all Benefits & Wellness Fair attendees!  At the conclusion of each seminar, each seminar attendee will receive a voucher for an additional raffle ticket to give you extra chances to win a prize during the Benefits & Wellness Fair!  Seating is on a first come, first serve basis so plan to arrive early!

   The Year of Longevity and the Paper Crane
