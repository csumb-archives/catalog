Management Leadership Certificate Program

This program is designed for the seasoned manager who is interested in expanding knowledge and awareness in areas relevant to leadership development.

Recommended prerequisite:  2 year's experience as manager or Department Chair and completion of MPP Orientation ProSeminar (formerly MPP Success Seminar)

Sessions for 2006-2007:

~Leadership & Management Roles for a Changing Workforce - Understand the differences between management and leadership; 360 management profile; Best Practices in change management

~Successful Employment Practices & Legal HR Issues - Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO); Diversity; Sexual harassment prevention; State/Federal laws-ADA; FLSA; et.al.

~Managing in the CSU Union Environment - Recognizing the bargaining units; 23-campus impact of management decisions; coaching/counseling; corrective actions and their implications

~Teambuilding, Coaching, Mentoring - Defining roles, goals, expectations; establishing a communication plan; managing different generations during change and uncertainty

~Performance Management - The "Performance Management Conversation"; Behaviorally anchored rating systems (BARS); reward & recognition
