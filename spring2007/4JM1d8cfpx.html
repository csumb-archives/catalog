Japanese Language and Culture Minor ~ Distinguishing Features

This minor is outcomes-based. It entails a functional level of language proficiency (Intermediate-Mid according to ACTFL guidelines) and an extensive understanding of the Japanese people and their culture.

All classes use multimedia and Internet technologies to provide rich contemporary content.
