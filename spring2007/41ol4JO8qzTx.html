Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

CSUMB recognizes that life at work and at home can be full of difficult challenges. It's important to know that you are not alone. As a CSUMB employee, you and any member of your household can turn to the Employee Assistance Program for help. Your EAP provider is only a phone call away and is available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. This benefit is available through Magellan Health Services to offer employees and their families information on hundreds of health and wellness topics, including:

  • Stress
  • Family or parenting issues
  • Alcohol or drug dependencies
  • Marital or relationship issues
  • Adjusting to change
  • Self –improvement
  • Child and elder care
  • Pre and postnatal concerns
  • Grief
  • Work/ life balance

 All services are kept strictly confidential. Face-to-face counseling with qualified local counselors is also available and provided at no charge. Brochures are available at the Campus Service Center or through University Human Resources. To reach the EAP provider, call 1-800-523-5668. (TDD 1-800-882-7610) or visit them online at http://www.magellanassist.com/.

CalPERS Long Term Care Insurance - State Employees Only

This program is one of the most comprehensive and affordable coverage plans available today. It is the nation's first self-funded long-term care program offered on a not-for-profit basis. It offers vital protection against the devastating cost of long-term care. All public employees and retirees should consider long-term care coverage as an integral part of their health and retirement planning. All California public employees, retirees, their spouses, parents, parents-in-law, and adult siblings (age 18 - 79) are eligible for this CalPERS Program.

The CalPERS Long Term Care open enrollment begins July 1 through September 30 2006.  You can request to have information materials mailed to you , including an information guide, plan options, an application, and more by calling the Kit Request phone line at (800) 266-1050. For more information go to: http://www.beginswithaplan.com/.

CALPERS Member Home Loan Program - State Employees Only

Active members of CalPERS are eligible to apply for a CalPERS home loan through various financial institutions. Employees must qualify for the loan through a lender. Special features include competitive interest rates, loan discounts, and fixed rates. For more information, please  visit the CalPERS Home Loan website or call them at (800) 874-7377.

Mortgage Assistance

CSU offers a special mortgage program through CitiMortgage that is primarily targeted to assist moderate to low income households and employees making investments in moderate to low income census tracts as defined by the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD).

Employees that purchase or refinance their homes through CitiMortgage will be eligible to receive either a credit at closing of the application fee paid at the time of origination, or a credit of the appraisal and credit report fees if an application fee is not collected.

In addition, CitiMortgage will provide opportunities for 100% loans at 80% loan-to value rates, avoiding private mortgage insurance rate premiums, when leased land from campuses and/or affiliates is involved.

For further information, please contact our area CitiMortgage representative at (408) 298-1311.

Presidio of Monterey Outdoor Recreation Program

CSUMB employees enjoy special benefits made available through the partnership between CSUMB and the Army Recreation Program. A mini-equipment center has been set up at the Stillwell Community Center. This benefit allows CSUMB employees to:

  • Rent various outdoor recreation equipment (e.g. camping equipment, bikes,)
  • Purchase discount tickets for California attractions including - local special events, sporting events, movies, cultural events, and specialty programs
  • Rent RV storage
  • Rent properties in South Lake Tahoe
  • Rent a charter bus

For further information, they may be reached at (831) 242-5506/6133 or via their web site at http://www.pom-odr.com/.

Otter Sports Center and Otter Aquatics Center

The Otter Sports Center houses a 10,000-square-foot gymnasium available for basketball, volleyball, badminton, indoor soccer, aerobics, dance, sports games and special events. There are also three racquetball courts, a 2,500-square-foot fitness room with cardiovascular and weight training equipment, and locker/shower/ sauna facilities. Vending machines with snacks and beverages are on site. Please contact the Otter Sports Center at 831.582.3031 for the latest information.

The Otter Aquatics Center  is an outdoor heated 25 meter by 25 yard eight lane swimming pool that is NCAA short course swimming and diving compliant.  The pool's depth ranges from  3 1/2 feet  to 13 feet deep and and includes two diving boards, a 1 meter and a 3 meter.  Lap swim, open swim, and swim lessons are a few of the programs offered.  Please contact the Aquatics Center at 831-582-4552 for more information.

Special discounted membership rates for staff and faculty are available.  For more information regarding class schedules, times and fees, please give them a call or visit their website at: http://sports.csumb.edu/recreation/ottersportscenter/osc.htm.

Monterey Sports Center Corporate Discount available to CSUMB employees

CSUMB has partnered with the Monterey Sports Center to offer CSUMB employees a 10% discount on a 1-year Adult Extended Use Pass (EUP). To take part in this benefit, simply present your CSUMB Staff/ Faculty Otter Card to a Monterey Sports Center staff member when applying for membership.  For information on hours of operation and the services provided, visit the Monterey Sports Center at www.monterey.org/sportscenter/.

Campus Health Center

The mission of the CSUMB Campus Health Center is to provide the highest standard of medical service while encouraging patients to be active participants in their health care. The Campus Health Center is primarily student focused. The center's staff includes a broad range of health professionals who have expertise in primary care and women's health services. All clinical staff are licensed or registered in California.

The Campus Health Center is available to:

  • All full-time or part-time CSUMB students who have registered and paid on-campus health fees
  • CSUMB faculty and staff
  • The dependents of CSUMB students, faculty, and staff
  • Local residents and visitors to the university

Some of the services provided at the clinic are:

  • Medical services
  • Communicable disease control
  • Women's health services

The Campus Health Center may be reached at 831/582-3965.

For further information, please contact our area's Field Marketing Manager at 1-877-776-7048 or visit A+ Auto & Home Insurance Plus via the web at: http://aplus.com/corporate/.
