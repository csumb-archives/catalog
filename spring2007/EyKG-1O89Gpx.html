Administrative Professionals Certificate Program

This program focuses on the skills and knowledge vital for success in the administrative support function. Participants will work on recognizing and improving areas that will allow them to provide effective support to their organizations or departments.

Sessions for 2006-2007

~Succeeding in Times of Change and Transition - Identifying opportunities; managing transitions - the uncertain place between old and new; creating new systems

~Delivering Great Customer Service - Learning to actively listen; identifying customer needs; understanding your customer; recognizing what you have to offer the customer

~Effective Business Communication - Clear business writing; effective telephone skills; effective presentations and public speaking

~Project Management Fundamentals - Goal setting; building your project team; time and task management

~Working Effectively Across Generations - Generational profiles; supervising the older/younger worker; gaining perspective

~Culminating project - participants are encouraged to complete a project or process improvement, or assemble a portfolio demonstrating mastery of the skills and knowledge encompassed by the certificate.

Certificates will be granted upon completion of the Culminating Project and awarded during a ceremony on campus
