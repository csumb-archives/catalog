In the Major ProSeminar, in consultation with the HCOM peer advisor and a faculty advisor, you will prepare your Individual Learning Plan (ILP). You will continue to work with a faculty advisor every semester to track and maintain progress toward the degree. For career advising, graduate school advising, internship advising and related mentoring, you will consult with the concentration coordinator for your specific HCOM concentration.

In addition, each Major ProSeminar class selects student representatives who serve as liaisons representing student interests and concerns.
