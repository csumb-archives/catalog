The Academic Skills Achievement Program is a free tutoring service open to all students.  It provides the CSUMB community with high-quality learning assistance in the areas of writing, critical reading, mathematics, statistics, science, languages (ASL, Japanese, and Spanish), computer technology and English for multilingual speakers. Peer and professional tutors employ active and collaborative learning strategies aimed at enabling students at all ability levels to expand their knowledge and abilities by empowering them to become independent learners.  We provide tutors training and experience to develop teaching, leadership and communication skills.  ASAP is Level II certified by the College Reading and Learning Association.
