CSUMB's Alternative Transportation Program provides a number of ways for the CSUMB campus community to save money on transportation while helping to keep the environment clean at the same time.

All students, staff and faculty will have free, unlimited access to Monterey-Salinas Transit.

To learn more about alternative transportation options and find additional resouces, visit CSUMB.EDU/trip.
