University Police Department

    100 Campus Center

A fully certified, state law enforcement agency, the CSUMB Police Department oversees all aspects of public safety. Officers respond to reports of criminal activity, suspicious circumstances, traffic collisions, safety hazards, and medical and fire emergencies.
