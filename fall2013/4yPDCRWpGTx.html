A department within Health & Wellness Services, the PGCC is committed to the development of the whole person - academically, personally, socially, spiritually, and physically - and provides high quality crisis intervention, counseling, consultation, educational outreach programs, support groups and referrals.  Our mission is to promote the mental health of CSUMB students in order to improve the quality of their lives and aid them in achieving academic success. We value the self-worth of each individual, respect and embrace human diversity, and provide services that aid in the development of healthy behaviors.
