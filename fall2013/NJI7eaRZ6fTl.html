If you transferred into CSUMB from a California community college as an AA-T-certified student in Studio Art, you must complete the following courses for your bachelor's degree in Visual and Public Art at CSUMB. If you are unsure about your transfer status, please talk to an advisor. 

If you transferred into CSUMB from a California community college as an AA-T-certified student in Studio Art, you must complete the following courses for your bachelor's degree in Visual and Public Art at CSUMB. If you are unsure about your transfer status, please talk to an advisor. 

Core Curriculum (Total of 34 units)

VPA 300: VPA Major ProSeminar (fall, Junior year)

VPA 302: VPA Visiting Artist Series (twice)

VPA 307: Diverse Hist Contemp Art OR VPA 308: Ways Of Seeing (C1 Upper Division GE)

VPA 310: Mixed Media

VPA 309: Materials & Methods (spring, Senior year)

VPA 320S: Museum Studies OR VPA 306S (UD SL)

VPA 313: Regional Art History (A4, GWAR)

VPA 405: Senior Capstone (fall and spring semesters, Senior year)

 

Select from these Suggested Courses  (at least 14 units)

VPA 301: Beginning Painting

VPA 312: Vis Arts Ed For Teachers

VPA 324: Museum Conservation & Display

VPA 304: Painting & Mural

University Requirements (up to 12 units)

Language Proficiency Requirement through 201 level (unless you have already met the language requirement through 201, in which case you can take VPA electives).

For American Institutions requirement, select SBS 350: Domination & Resistance: US or SBS 385: Environmental History of CA (4 units) unless you have already met the requirement, in which case you can take VPA electives).
