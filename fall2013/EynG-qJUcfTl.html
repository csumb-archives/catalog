The Center for Student Success is designed to enhance student achievement and to support the retention goals of the university. In its nascent stage, the center seeks to serve as a resource for incoming and continuing students, facilitate student-to-student interaction and student involvement, and increase overall academic engagement. The center also exists as a collaborative partner with other campus units that address issues that impact student development and success.
