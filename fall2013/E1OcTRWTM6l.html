Credits Required

A miminum of 12 credits.
Credits at CSUMB: Consult with a Kinesiology Department advisor.

Outcomes, Courses and Assessment

You must achieve all of the Learning Outcomes (LO) designated by successfully completing courses or alternative assessment.

LO  1: Knowledge and Perspectives in Sport Management

Select one of the following. Each course may be used only once.

  • KIN 320: Sports Nutrition
  • KIN 335: Fundamentals of Coaching
  • KIN 380: Sports Administration
  • KIN 381: Legal Issues in Sport
  • KIN 484: Facility Management

LO  2: Diversity and Multiculturalism

  • KIN 345: Sports Ethics

LO 3: Collaboration

Select one of the following. Each course may be used only once.

  • KIN 310: Athletic Strength & Condition
  • KIN 410: Care/Prevent of Athlet Injury
  • KIN 468: Sp Top Intern: Spt Man & Coach
  • KIN 486: Sports Finance

LO 4: Leadership and Social Responsibility:

Select one of the following. Each course may be used only once.

  • KIN 310: Athletic Strength & Condition
  • KIN 335: Fundamentals of Coaching
  • KIN 381: Legal Issues in Sport
  • KIN 410: Care/Prevent of Athlet Injury
  • KIN 486: Sports Finance 

Please contact the Kinesiology Department regarding courses for this minor.
