Academic advisors actively assist students during their freshmen and sophomore years with course selection based on their academic and career goals, university requirements and pathways from first semester's entrance to graduation. The center's staff also assists upper-division students when departmental advising is not available. Students using the Advising Center can become knowledgeable of the campus resources available to help them become self-reliant navigators of their academic and career objectives.

Career advisors prepare students to make informed decisions about their futures by providing resources, programs and counseling on career development, internships, employment and graduate school.
