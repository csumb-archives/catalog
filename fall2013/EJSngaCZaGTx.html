High School
Recommended
  • Information processing/computer operations (at least one year)
  • Second language (three or more years)
Community College
Recommended
  • Complete lower division transfer work approved for the Liberal Studies Multiple Subjects Integrated Program
  • Reading
  • Writing
  • Critical thinking
  • Oral communication
  • Music, art, dance and drama
  • Mathematics, particularly college algebra
  • Introduction to teaching (if offered at your community college)
  • Information processing (computer operations and applications)
  • Three or more semesters of a second language
  • Natural sciences (life, physical and earth)
  • Ancient world history
  • World regional geography
  • U.S. history
  • Government (U.S. and California)
  • Literature (multicultural focus, preferred)
  • Child development (K-8 focus)
CSUMB Lower-Division
Recommended
  • Complete all lower-division University Requirements (including First Year Seminar and Language courses at the 102 and 201 levels) and Liberal Studies Lower Division MLOs before advancing to upper division MLOs, minor or emphasis.
