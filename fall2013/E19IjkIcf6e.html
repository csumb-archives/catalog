Smoking is prohibited in state-owned buildings and leased space including atriums, balconies, stairwells, and other similar building features. Smoking is also prohibited in state-owned vehicles.

Smoking is not permitted anywhere in the residence halls. This includes individual rooms.

Smoking is permitted outdoors in areas that are at least 25 feet away from doorways and ground-level air intake structures.

For more information, see the Smoke-Free Environment Policy on the CSUMB Policy website.
