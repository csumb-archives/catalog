English Subject Matter Preparation Program

For those intending to teach English at the secondary level, the English Subject Matter Preparation Program  Concentration offers a California-state-approved curriculum that prepares you to enter a fifth-year teaching credential program and attain a credential to teach English in secondary schools. The concentration features a multicultural study of poetry, drama, essays, fiction, nonfiction, memoirs and film drawing from American and British literatures. It includes the study and practice of expository and creative writing; the study of argument; and the history, politics, global significance and social dynamics related to the English language.

  • Please see English Subject Matter Preparaton Program Coordinator Jennifer Fletcher for a full list of required courses.
