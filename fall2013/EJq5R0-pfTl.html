The Special Education faculty advise students throughout their tenure in the program. Students are highly encouraged to meet with their advisor at least once a semester. The advisement is individualized, timely and supportive. A credential analyst is available on site to answer technical questions regarding state certification. An administrative support assistant responds to student requests and concerns, maintains up-to-date records and informs students regarding all aspects of the program.
