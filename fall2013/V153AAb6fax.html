The objectives of the MBA program are to:

  • Provide the knowledge required for career advancement opportunities to current as well as future managers and executives;
  • Help corporations, government agencies and nonprofit organizations achieve organizational effectiveness through professional development for their managers, and;
  • Drive economic development in the tri-county region by developing a stronger School of Business with more advanced business offerings.
