Leaders must possess both business acumen and technological expertise in the high-tech marketplace fueled by rapid technological advances and globalization. Those who become savvy in both management and technology will empower themselves to succeed. The M.S. in Management and Information Technology tightly integrates knowledge of core management disciplines and information technology to prepare graduates for high-tech management positions or to launch a new business.  Additionally, MSMIT students gain skills to effectively lead an ethnic, cultural, and gender diverse workforce.
 
MSMIT currently offers fully online courses via CalStateOnline (www.CalStateOnline.com). Designed for full-time working professionals, the program provides a flexible learning experience and leverages their current job and past experience.  MSMIT fully online courses have different fee structure and academic calendar, please contact school of ITCD for details and see Extended-Ed page for more information.
 

For more detailed information including student work and faculty bios, please visit the
 MSMIT pages on the School of Information Technology and Communications Design website.
