Fulltime: Traditional Student Teaching Pathway

In the traditional multiple subjects credential program, you can earn your credential in two consecutive fulltime stages if all academic and experiential prerequisites have been completed. The following would be a typical pathway that you might follow to complete the requirements as a fulltime student. Please note, individuals in iTEP complete a minimum of 12 units of Stage I credential courses in the last semester of their senior year (excluding ED 538: Intro to Classrm Tech Level 1 and ED 611: Teaching & Learning), and Stage II courses in the post baccaluaurate semester.

Stage I

ED 538: Intro to Classrm Tech Level 1

ED 601: Stage I Supv Elem Teaching

ED 601A: Stage1 Elem Teaching Seminar

ED 609: Math Methods

ED 611: Teaching & Learning

ED 612: Pedagogy for Ling Diversity

ED 616: Lang/Literacy Curriculum I

ED 675: Health for Elem School Child

ED 676: PE for Elem School Child

Stage II

ED 603: Stage 2 Supv Elem Teaching

ED 603A: Stage2 Elem Teaching Seminar

ED 608: Science Meth K-8 Classroom

ED 615: Social Studies C & I K8

ED 617: Lang/Literacy Curriculum II

ED 619: Visual & PerfArts C & I K8

SPED 560: Inclusionary Practices

Internship Pathway

The Internship pathway takes four continuous school terms, beginning in June of one summer and ending the end of July the following summer if all academic and experiential prerequisites have been completed.

The following would be a typical pathway that you might follow to complete the requirements through the Internship pathway in that time frame. It is possible to ease the load on a given semester by extending your time in the program by one or two semesters. Please note: The intern pathway is based on availability pending further review of school districts demand for multiple subject interns. Therefore, it is offered on an "as needed" basis with a high class enrollment.

ED 575: Teachers Academy

ED 611: Teaching & Learning

ED 612: Pedagogy for Ling Diversity
