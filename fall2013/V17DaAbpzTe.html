The Office of International Programs provides opportunities for CSUMB students and faculty in all majors and fields to join the global community, and for international students to take part in our high quality learning experiences. We believe that individual crosscultural encounters are the best way for those in the CSUMB community to gain the skills, knowledge, and empathy required for success in our increasingly interdependent world.

~ Dr. Chris Hasegawa, dean, extended education and international programs
