The bachelor of science degree in Computer Science and Information Technology is offered by the School of Information Technology and Communication Design.

The CSIT major is a unique degree program which blends computer science theory and information technology practice.

Students in this degree program begin by taking a set of common core courses to establish a strong foundation in computer science and information technology.  Following the foundational courses students select a concentration in either Software Engineering, Network and Security, Information Systems or Game Development. In the program students learn computing and information technology concepts while applying their knowledge to solve real-world problems. Collaboration and communication skills are infused into the learning experiences with heavily project-based courses in a state-of-the-art technological environment.
