If you transferred into CSUMB from a California community college as an AA-T-certified student in Psycholgy, you must complete the following courses for your bachelor's degree in Psychology at CSUMB. If you are unsure about your transfer status, please talk to an advisor. 

Psychology Major Curriculum: 31

PSY 300: Expermntl/Psy & Human/Assesmnt

PSY 301/L: Adv/Psychological Statistics or PSY 305/L: PSY Testing & Measurement

PSY 302/L: PSY/Resear/Methods/Data/Analys

PSY 310: Biological Psychology series course (choose 1 from 310-319)

PSY 320: Psychopathology series course (choose 1 from 320-329)

PSY 330: Cognitive Psychology series course (choose 1 from 330-339)

PSY 340: Developmental Psychology series course (choose 1 from 340-349)

PSY 350: Social Psychology series course (choose 1 from 350-359)

PSY 400: Psychology Capstone

University Requirements: 22 units

Any approved upper division D2 course (such as SBS 350: Domination & Resistance: US or SBS 385: Environmental History of CA)

Any approved D4 course

Any approved A4 course (GWAR)

Language Proficiency Requirement through the 201 level. 

Electives (additional units cannot bring total units to more than 60)

Any PSY electives
