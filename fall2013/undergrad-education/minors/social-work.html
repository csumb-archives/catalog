Planning to pursue a professional career in social work, counseling, or case management or considering a graduate Master of Social Work program? The Social Work Minor provides the practical knowledge and skills you will need to begin. It covers professional ethics and values, human behavior and development, and counseling practice skills.

You will find all of the official information about the minor below. For more detailed information including faculty bios, please visit the Health, Human Services, and Public Policy Department website.
