The Service Learning Leadership Minor provides students with the opportunity to develop advanced knowledge and skills in community and social change processes, perspectives in service and social justice, and service learning pedagogy.

CSUMB is recognized as a national leader in the service learning field.  Graduating with the Minor in Service Learning Leadership will prepare students to work in schools, nonprofits, public agencies, and community organizations as community educators and social-change agents.  Be a leader in this rapidly growing field, with a degree from one of the most respected programs in the country.

Want to make a difference in your community?
Want to be a part of the solution, and not a part of the problem?
Want to change lives and inspire action to improve our communities?
Then, add this minor to your academic plans!

You will find all of the official information about the minor below. For more detailed information including a look at service learning principles, faculty, and community partners, please visit the Service Learning Institute website.
