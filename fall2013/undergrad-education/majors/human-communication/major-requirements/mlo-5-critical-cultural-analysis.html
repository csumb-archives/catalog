--- layout: archive title: MLO 5: Critical cultural analysis CSUMB Catalog year: fall2013 ---

Ability to investigate and explain relationships among cultural ideologies and sociohistorical experiences, interests, identities and actions of specific cultural groups.

Required Courses

  • Complete one of the following:
  • HCOM 338: Multicultural Adolescent Lit (4 units)
  • HCOM 342: Feminist Theories & Methods (4 units) *
  • HCOM 343: Race & Gender Justice (4 units)
  • HCOM 344: Chicana/Latina Experiences (4 units) *
  • HCOM 345: Chicano Life & Culture (4 units)
  • HCOM 348: Race, Colonialism & Film (4 units) *
  • HCOM 422: SelTop Multicultural Rhetorics (4 units)
  • HCOM 426: Travel Narratives (4 units)
  • HCOM 440: Leadership & Community (4 units)
  • HCOM 443: Black Feminist Theory & Praxis (4 units)
  • HCOM 455: Paradigms Of Chicano Comm (4 units)
  • HCOM 555: Paradigms Of Chicano Comm (4 units)
  • *Fulfills the research intensive/theory requirement
