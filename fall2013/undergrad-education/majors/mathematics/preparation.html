High School
Recommended
  • Mathematics (all four years)
  • Algebra II
  • Geometry
  • Pre-Calculus
  • Advanced mathematics courses that are available at your school
Community College
Recommended
  • Three semesters of calculus
  • One course in discrete mathematics
  • One course in linear algebra
  • One course in differential equations
  • One course in computer programming

CSUMB Lower-Division

Recommended
  • Completion of following courses
    • MATH 150: Calculus I
    • MATH 151: Calculus II
    • MATH 170: Discrete Mathematics
    • MATH 250: Multivariate Calculus
    • MATH 265: Diff Equs & Linear Algebra
    • CST 231/L: Problm-Solving/Programng
  • Completion of all lower-division General Education Requirements
