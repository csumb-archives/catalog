Degree Summary View

This pathway is one example of how you might complete all the requirements for your degree, assuming you have completed all of the courses listed in the Preparation section for the major prior to enrolling at CSUMB.

Your own unique situation and a number of other factors may mean your actual pathway is different. You'll want to work closely with an advisor and use the academic advisement report to take all that into account and develop a pathway that's customized for you.
 
In the meantime, refer to the major requirements page and this sample pathway as a starting point for choosing classes or discussing your plans with an advisor. Your advisor is your best resource when it comes to figuring out how to fit all the courses you need, in the right sequence, into your personal academic plan.
