Students shall take no more than four lower-division courses with the same subject prefix as their major to fulfill their general education requirements.

Approved Courses

  • BIO 240L: Ecol, Evolutn, & Biodiv Lab (1 unit)
    Approved from Fall 2012

  • CHEM 110L: Chemistry I Lab (2 units)
    Approved from Fall 2012

  • ENVS 101: Energy and Sustainability (4 units)
    Approved from Summer 2014

  • ENVS 201: Intro to Environmental Science (4 units)
    Approved from Fall 2012

  • KIN 370L: Anatomy & Physiology I Lab (1 unit)
    Approved from Fall 2012

  • KIN 371L: Anatomy & Physiology II Lab (1 unit)
    Approved from Fall 2012

  • PHYS 121L: Integrated Phy Science Lab (1 unit)
    Approved from Fall 2012

CSU General Education Alignment

  • Laboratory Practices: CSU GE Area B3

Outcomes

  1. Students demonstrate satisfactory laboratory and/or field methods to collect and evaluate data used in scientific inquiry.
  2. Students apply standard scientific methods to critically evaluate evidence to address questions about the natural world and communicate their findings.
