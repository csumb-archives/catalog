We will assign a faculty advisor to you. When you are placed in schools, a university supervisor and a teacher colleague will guide the development of your teaching skills. Also, if needed, the Academic Skills Achievement Program will help you with additional out-of-class assistance in the development of effective communications skills. A credential analyst is available on site to answer technical questions regarding state certification.
