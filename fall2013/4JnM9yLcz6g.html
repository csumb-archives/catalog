If you want to pursue a career or advanced graduate study in environmental science, resource management, habitat restoration, applied ecology, natural resources or related fields, then this is the CSUMB major for you!

Because of our major's emphasis on practical, real-world learning and quantititative and geospatial skills, our graduates have been highly successful at getting great jobs and gaining admission to some of the nation's top graduate and professional schools.

You will find the official information about the Environmental Science, Technology & Policy major in this Catalog. 

For more detailed information including a look at student work, faculty bios, research partnerships and internship opportunities, please visit the Environmental Science, Technology & Policy major website.
