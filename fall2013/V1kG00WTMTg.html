The Cinematic Arts & Technology faculty and advising staff provide consultation, mentoring and guidance to help you develop your learning plan.

Advising support is available to majors and prospective majors though the fall and spring semesters and part of summer.

Cinematic Arts & Technology students also take advantage of universitywide services including:

  • Academic Skills Achievement Program 
  • Migrant Student Support Services 
  • Student Disability Resources 
  • Personal Growth and Counseling Center 
  • Office of International Programs
