Service Learning ~ Learning Environment & Service Placement

Faculty members work with community organizations to find the appropriate fit between course learning outcomes and the needs and resources of each organization. Instructors provide you with a list of sites from which to choose, and they may even arrange class visits or fieldtrips to the sites to guide you toward finding an organization that will appreciate your talents and skills. SLI houses the Service Opportunities Database which contains information on over 500 organizations interested in working with YOU!

In service learning courses, the community IS your classroom!
