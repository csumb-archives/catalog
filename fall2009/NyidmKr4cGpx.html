Special Education Credentials ~ Academic Advising and Support

The Special Education faculty advises students throughout their tenure in the program. The advisement is individualized, timely, and supportive. A credential analyst is available on site to answer technical questions regarding state certification. An administrative support assistant responds to student requests and concerns, maintains up-to-date records, and informs students regarding all aspects of the program.
