We Need Your Help!

Although California has recognized and validated the critical need for this facility, the state's austere budget limits funding to a minimally functional facility, essentially a concrete box. Gift funds will allow creation of an attractive, multi-functional facility with an enhanced design that can keep pace with improvements in cutting-edge technologies. These enhancements will also enable our broader community access to this facility as a "learning commons" for the entire tri-county area. Gifts of all sizes are needed to realize this dream. Please click here to make a tax-deductable gift appropriate to your own means benefitting students and citizens of today and tomorrow.
