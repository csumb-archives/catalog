Financial Aid ~ Fee Deferment

Financial Aid applicants are granted a fee deferment to allow additional time for award processing. The deferment expires on November 1 for the fall semester and April 1 for the spring semester. If you have outstanding university charges after these deadlines, CSUMB will assess late charges and place registration holds on your student record.
