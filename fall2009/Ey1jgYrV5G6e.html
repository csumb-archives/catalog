Do you offer study in cultural history?

If you want to pursue a career in cultural history, we offer the following pathways:

  • Global Studies B.A.
  • Hispanic Cultures and History Minor
  • Human Communication B.A.
    • Africana Studies Concentration
    • Chicana/o-Latina/o Studies
    • History, Oral History, and New Media
  • Japanese Culture and History Minor
  • Social and Behavioral Sciences B.A.
    • Anthropology Concentration
    • Archaeology Concentration
    • Social History Concentration

Some pathways may require additional coursework outside the major. Talk to your advisor to find out how best to meet your personal, academic and career goals.
