World Languages and Cultures B.A. ~ Careers and Graduate Study

 

Emphases in Japanese and Spanish

Related Careers
WLC graduates have entered and achieved notable success in the fields of:
  • Education:
    • College-level and K-12 language teaching
    • Educational material development
    • Japan Exchange and Teaching Program (JET Program)
  • Travel
    • Airlines and air transport services
    • Interpretation
  • Technology
    • International web design
    • Web localization
  • Business
    • Translation and interpretation
    • International marketing
    • International and domestic sales
    • Television

Graduate Study

Students graduating from CSUMB with a major in World Languages and Cultures have entered and achieved notable success in the following fields of graduate study: Asian studies; international business; Japanese language and linguistics; Latin American studies; Spanish language and Hispanic cultures; and Spanish language and linguistics.
