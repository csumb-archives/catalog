Welcome to the Campus Service Center!

Hours

Monday: 8:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m.

Tuesday-Friday: 8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.

We are located in the Student Services Building 47, First Floor

The Campus Service Center ("The CSC") provides forms, information, and front counter support for Admissions & Recruitment , Records & Registration, Financial Aid, and Cashiering. The CSC distributes parking permits (Prices), Otter ID Cards and paychecks.
