Visual and Public Art B.A. ~ Transfer Pathway

This pathway, intended as a reference, models graduation after earning 60 credits at CSUMB. This pathway assumes that the student has met all lower-division GE requirements,  and has demonstrated competence at the equivalent of the Language 201 level upon entry to CSUMB.  Your actual pathway may not look exactly like the model.
