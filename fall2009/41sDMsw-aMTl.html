Mathematics B.S.

To learn mathematics is to learn a language - a "pure language," "the language of science," and "the only universal language." By learning this powerful language at the highest levels, you will build a foundation for your future in business, education, engineering, government service, science, or virtually any career. Our Bachelor of Science in Mathematics program will help you do exactly that!

You will find the official information about the major right here. Simply use the left-side navigation or the matching links at the bottom of this page.

For more detailed information including course webpages and faculty profiles, please visit the Mathematics and Statistics Department website.
