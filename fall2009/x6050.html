Student Disability Resources (SDR) promotes the empowerment, wellness, and full integration of students with disabilities into campus life and the campus community.

Further, we seek to provide academic and personal support services to students with disabilities in order for them to attain success at California State University, Monterey Bay.

Attention New Students:

Student Disability Resources offers an orientation each semester for students who might be interested in learning what services CSUMB's Student Disability Resources Office has to offer. Procedures for requesting accommodations and learning about on- and off-campus resources are discussed.

If you missed the Fall Orientation, please click here to review the agenda with links to the handouts.
