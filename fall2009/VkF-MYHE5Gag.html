Educational Opportunity Program

A state funded program that supports historically disadvantaged, first-generation college students, EOP provides admissions and academic support to qualified students. EOP has a specific program for freshmen as well as specific transition programs and workshops that meet the needs of the transfer student. EOP provides academic and personal support to assist the students in the program to strive for excellence.
