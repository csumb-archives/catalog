University Bookstore

Located in the University Center, the University Bookstore has an extensive selection of textbooks, apparel, software and computer accessories. Additionally, it stocks a variety of magazines, snacks and gifts. You can also shop the bookstore online and have your books held in the store or delivered directly to you.
