Integrated Studies Special Major, B.A.~ Learning Environment

Wired for the 21st century since opening in 1994, CSUMB sits on the leading edge of technology. At the same time, we are multicultural and diverse both in backgrounds and interests. We have committed ourselves to pluralistic faculty, staff, and student populations that interact in small classes conducted in spacious, modern facilities.

Regardless of the fields that you choose to integrate, our campus and region will help you put it all together. Our campus location is a major asset. Monterey Bay is a scientific wonderland. Silicon Valley&#8212;center of the high-tech world&#8212;lies one hour to the north. San Francisco&#8212;a mecca for the arts and humanities&#8212;is just two hours up the coast. We have established direct working relationships with surrounding communities, organizations, and companies to open doors to rich internship and employment opportunities for our students in a number of fields.
