Do you offer programs in advertising?

If you want to pursue a career in advertising, we offer the following pathways:

  • Business Administration B.S.
    • Management and International Business Concentration
    • Marketing Concentration
  • Business Minor
  • Human Communication B.A.
    • Practical and Professional Ethics Concentration
    • Writing and Rhetoric Concentration
  • Human Communication Minor

Some pathways may require additional coursework outside the major. Talk to your advisor to find out how best to meet your personal, academic and career goals.
