Do you offer a program in marine science?

If you want to pursue a career in marine science, including specialization in marine biology, marine botany, marine ecology, marine geochemistry, marine habitat mapping, marine animal husbandry, marine policy, marine research, and marine technology, we offer the following pathways:

  • Biology B.S.
    • General Biology Concentration
  • Coastal and Watershed Science & Policy M.S.
  • Environmental Science, Technology & Policy B.S.
    • Marine & Coastal Ecology Concentration
    • Watershed Systems Concentration
    • Environmental Policy Concentration
  • Environmental Science, Technology & Policy Minor
  • Marine Science M.S.

Some pathways may require additional coursework outside the major. Talk to your advisor to find out how best to meet your personal, academic and career goals.
