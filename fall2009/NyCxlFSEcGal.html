Do you offer programs in humanities?

If you want to pursue a career in the humanities, we offer the following pathways:

  • Human Communication B.A.
    • Africana Studies Concentration
    • Chicana/o-Latina/o Studies Concentration
    • Comparative American Studies Concentration
    • Creative Writing and Social Action Concentration
    • History, Oral History, and New Media Concentration
    • Journalism and Media Studies Concentration
    • Literary and Film Studies Concentration
    • Peace Studies Concentration
    • Practical and Professional Ethics Concentration
    • Pre-Law Concentration
    • Single Subject English Waiver Concentration
    • Single Subject History and Social Sciences Waiver Concentration
    • Women's Studies Concentration
    • Writing and Rhetoric Concentration

Some pathways may require additional coursework outside the major. Talk to your advisor to find out how best to meet your personal, academic and career goals.
