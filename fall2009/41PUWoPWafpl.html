Business Administration B.S. ~ Entrepreneurship/Intrapreneurship Concentration ~ Freshman Pathway

Because we have built our academic programs on an outcomes-based education model, you have latitude in designing an Individual Learning Plan to achieve your personal target outcomes while also satisfying University Learning Requirements, Graduation Learning Outcomes, and Major Learning Outcomes.

This pathway, intended as a reference, shows a productive route to graduation in four years.  Your actual pathway may not look exactly like the model due to several factors:

  • Any required remedial courses, such as MATH 098 and WRT 095
  • Actual availability of courses during a given semester
  • Personal choices you make in your Individual Learning Plan – in consultation with your First Year Seminar professor, and the business student peer advisors – including the decision to pursue more than the minimal learning requirements
