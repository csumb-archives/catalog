Psychology B.A. ~ Home

In the Psychology major you'll develop a solid understanding of human behavior and mental processes using a scientific approach. You'll explore the ways in which human biology, psychological experience and learning, and cultural context combine to influence our actions and thoughts. Because the major meets all ten recommendations of the American Psychological Association Board of Educational Affairs, you will be highly qualified to continue your study of psychology at the graduate level, or apply your learning to a wide variety of career fields.

You will find the official information about the Psychology major right here. Simply use the left-side navigation or the matching links at the bottom of this page.

Please visit the CSUMB.EDU/psychology website for more detailed information including a look at faculty bios and department news.
