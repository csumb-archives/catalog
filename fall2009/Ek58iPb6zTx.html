Otter Card Services

The Otter Card serves as student identification card, library card, campus shuttle and Otter Sports Center pass, discount card at participant local retailers, and electronic access card to certain campus buildings. With the purchase of Otter Bucks, it may also be used as a debit card at all dining facilities, Residence Hall laundry rooms, the campus bookstore, and selected vending and copy machines.
