Liberal Studies B.A. ~ Academic Advising and Support Services

Faculty, staff and peer advisors work collaboratively to support and guide you through the curriculum so you reach your academic goals. Faculty members serve as mentors to help you develop your academic plans and position yourself to achieve your career objectives. The Liberal Studies advising coordinator, a specialist trained to advise and coordinate with you during the advising process, works one-on-one with you to develop an Individual Learning Plan that outlines how you will achieve the Major Learning Outcomes. Student peer advisors provide experienced, expert advice on completing graduation and degree requirements.

The Liberal Studies Advising Center serves as a one-stop-shop to answer questions about the major and how to prepare for entrance into a multiple subjects teaching credential program.

Advising is available on an appointment or drop-in basis. Email queries can be directed to liberal_studies@csumb.edu.

Advising Information

The Liberal Studies Department website also contains advising information for Liberal Studies majors. The student handbook includes current course requirements, policies and recent changes to the program. The department website can aid you in planning future coursework and in gaining better insight into the major. For more  information visit CSUMB.EDU/liberalstudies.
