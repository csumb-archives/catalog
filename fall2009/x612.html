Mathematics B.S. ~ Preparation

High School
Recommended
  • Mathematics (all four years)
  • Algebra II
  • Geometry
  • Pre-Calculus
  • Advanced math courses that are available at your school
Community College
Recommended
  • Three semesters of calculus
  • One course in discrete mathematics
  • Study of a foreign language as part of General Education requirements- 3 semesters

CSUMB Lower-Division

Recommended
  • Completion of MATH 150, MATH 151, MATH 250 and MATH 170
  • Completion of all lower-division University Learning Requirements/General Education
