Interdisciplinary Master of Arts with Instructional Science and Technology Emphasis ~ Careers and Doctoral Study

Related Careers

Interdisciplinary Master of Arts with Instructional Science and Technology (IST) graduates are uniquely qualified to enter fields such as e-learning development, corporate training, educational technology, instructional design, and related fields. A valuable instructional technology skillset prepares program graduates to teach at all levels.

Doctoral Study

The IST program provides a strong background for those graduates who wish to continue their study at the doctorate level.  IST graduates will find abundant opportunities to enter notable Ed.D. and Ph.D. programs.
