Music B.A.

Whether you're headed for a career as a performing artist, recording technician, or music teacher, our comprehensive Music B.A. program will equip you for the workplace in our rapidly changing technological world. It will increase your skills and appreciation for music and related arts.

You will find the official information about the Music major right here. Simply use the left-side navigation or the matching links at the bottom of this page.

Please visit the Music and Performing Arts Department website for more detailed information including a look at student work, faculty bios, and MPA events.
