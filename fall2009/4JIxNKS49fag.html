Environmental Health Policy Minor ~ Distinguishing Features

The EHP curriculum addresses local, state, and national policy issues within the disciplines of environmental science, community-based public health assessment, and policy analysis.

Real-world internship experiences allow you to learn about public health from professionals in community-based organizations.

Innovative and interactive classroom experiences include team-based assignments, student policy debates, case-study discussions, and guest lectures by environmental health professionals and program experts.

We focus on public health as a means of creating social change as informed, responsible, compassionate, and civically engaged global citizens.

We engage you in a dialogue of conscience with our diverse community of students, faculty, and professionals as we discuss a broad range of social, political, and cultural perspectives on current environmental health issues.
