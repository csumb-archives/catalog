Coastal and Watershed Science and Policy, M.S. ~ Related Careers

Coastal and Watershed Science & Policy prepares you for a wide array of careers in science, resource management, and environmental policy. According to a recent survey of regional employers, this master's degree could launch you into a career with federal, state, and local resource management agencies, nonprofit environmental organizations, county and local planning agencies, scientific and environmental consulting firms, and other businesses.
