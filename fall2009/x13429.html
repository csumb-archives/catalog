Do you offer a program in rhetoric?

If you want to pursue a career in rhetoric, we offer the following pathways:

  • Human Communication B.A.
    • Writing and Rhetoric Concentration
  • Writing and Rhetoric Minor

Some pathways may require additional coursework outside the major. Talk to your advisor to find out how best to meet your personal, academic and career goals.
