Tuition & Fees ~ Summer 2009 Payment/Refund Deadlines

May 1, 2009 ~ Summer registration opens (Payment is due prior to registering)

May 29, 2009 ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses from the first four-week session and receive any refund ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses from the eight-week summer session and receive a full refund of mandatory fees ~ Last day to register in the eight-week and first four-week summer sessions without a $25 late registration fee

June 10, 2009 ~ 2nd payment due for Summer 2009 installment payment plan

June 26, 2009 ~ Last day to register in the second four-week summer session without a $25 late registration fee ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses from the second four-week summer session and receive any refund

July 2, 2009 ~ Last day to officially withdraw or cancel all courses from the eight-week summer session and receive a prorated refund of mandatory fees

July 10, 2009 ~ Third and final payment due for summer 2009 installment payment plan
