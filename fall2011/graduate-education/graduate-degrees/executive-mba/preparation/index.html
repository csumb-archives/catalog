  • EMBA program students may come from a variety of undergraduate majors.
  • The EMBA program requires a Bachelor degree from an accredited college or university.
  • The EMBA program requires submission of prior academic records (GPA) from each degree-granting institution so that one's academic history can be assessed.
  • The EMBA program requires a completed CSUMB application form.
  • The EMBA program requires a student's statement of purpose for embarking on this degree program.
  • The EMBA program requires a minimum of three (3) letters of reference that directly relate to the student's potential performance in the EMBA program.
  • The EMBA program requires significant work experience. Approximately seven years of work experience, with approximately three of those years in a managerial/supervisory capacity.

For more information, visit CSUMB.EDU/emba.
