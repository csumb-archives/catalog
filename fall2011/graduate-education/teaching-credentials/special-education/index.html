Recognizing the shortage of teachers prepared to serve students with disabilities throughout the state of California and nationwide, CSUMB provides a relevant and thoughtful course of study with well-respected faculty members and field practitioners.

The Special Education Program at CSUMB offers the Level I  Mild Moderate Education Specialist Credential, the Level II Professional Clear Credential, and the Level I and Level II Moderate Severe Education Specialist Credentials. All Special Education Credentials will be revised for Fall 2011. Stay tuned for additional information. The Special Education Program also offers the Applied Behavior Analysis Certificate.

For more detailed information on the application process, field placement and more, please visit the Special Education website.
