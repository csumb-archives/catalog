Whether you want to improve teaching and learning in your classroom, advocate effectively for student growth and learning, assume leadership roles in schools and agencies serving students and families with disabilities, deepen your understanding of multicultural education, become a strong educational leader, earn your CLAD certificate, qualify to earn your doctorate, or become an outstanding teacher of culturally and linguistically diverse students, CSUMB's Master of Arts in Education program will deliver what you need.

You will find all of the official information about the program right here. Simply use the navigation or the matching links at the bottom of this page.
