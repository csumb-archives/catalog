The School of ITCD maintains a Student Services program to help you resolve issues and to direct you to available resources, including a Peer Advising program that will be help you to develop an Individualized Learning Plan so that you can be working towards fulfilling your degree requirements.

You will also be assigned a Faculty Advisor to provide specific advice relative to your specialization in the major.

More information is available on the ITCD Website.
