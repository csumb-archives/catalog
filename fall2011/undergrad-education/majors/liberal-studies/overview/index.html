With a Liberal Studies B.A., you will have a solid foundation for entry into a credential program or other graduate study. Our outcomes-based, multidisciplinary major integrates the arts and humanities, natural and social sciences, and service learning. We emphasize breadth across a variety of disciplines with a subject area emphasis in at least one academic discipline.

The Liberal Studies Multiple Subjects Integrated Program allows you to complete the Multiple Subjects Teaching Credential and begin working in the field sooner. Integrated means that you can complete your undergraduate degree and your multiple subjects credential in 135 units (roughly 4.5 years for freshman or 2.5 years for transfer students). Essentially, students complete the first semester of their multiple subject credential coursework in the last semester of their senior year as undergraduates.
