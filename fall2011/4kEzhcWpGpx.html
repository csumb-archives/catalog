The Science ULR provides you with the scientific literacy you will need to make effective and ethical decisions in your personal, professional, and community service life as a global citizens in the 21st century. It consists of two parts. The first focuses on how science is done, and the second provides important integrative science content.

Courses currently approved to fulfill this ULR are listed below.  Links to the ULR's outcomes, assessement and an archived list of courses no longer satisfying the ULR can be found below or in the navigation column.
