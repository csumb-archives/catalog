CSUMB offers nearly three dozen undergraduate minors that allow you to augment your major through targeted study in another area. To view their descriptions, select from the list below.

Like the majors, to focus on outcomes, each of the CSUMB minors requires you to display achievement of a set of Learning Outcomes (LOs) established by the program faculty. The description of each minor includes a list of the LOs and associated coursework.
