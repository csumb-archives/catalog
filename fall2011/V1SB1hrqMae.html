Ability to interact ethically and effectively in interpersonal and group communication and decision-making processes

Required Courses

  • Complete one of the following:
  • HCOM 304: Relational Ethics (4 units)
  • HCOM 306: Gender & Communication (4 units)
  • HCOM 309: Intercultural Communication (4 units)
  • HCOM 310: Free Speech & Resp (4 units)
  • HCOM 403: Ethical Issues (4 units)
  • OR
  • HCOM 403S: Ethics in the Professions:SL (6 units)
  • HCOM 412: Multicultural Conflict Resolut (4 units)
