Related Careers

Master of Science in Instructional Science and Technology (MIST) graduates are able to research, plan, design, develop, implement and evaluate instructional and non-instructional interventions in various work sectors. Graduates are uniquely qualified to enter fields such as e-learning development, corporate training, educational technology, instructional design and other related fields.

Doctoral Study

The MIST program provides a strong background for those graduates who would like to continue their study at the doctoral level.  MIST graduates are well-prepared to enter notable Ed.D. and Ph.D. programs.
