Payment Deadlines

January 6, 2012 ~ Last day to pay registration tuition and fees, have a financial aid payment extension, or have a third party authorization form covering registration tuition and fees on file with Student Accounting, otherwise your classes will be dropped for non-payment ~ To remain eligible for student housing, full payment of housing and meal plan charges must be received OR you must have a financial aid payment extension in place. Housing and meal plan eligibility questions should be directed to Student Housing & Residential Life at 831-582-3378.

January 7, 2012 ~ Starting today, payment, a Financial Aid payment extension or a third party authorization form covering registration tuition and fees must be on file with Student Accounting prior to registration. This includes students whose enrollment was cancelled.

February 3, 2012 ~ Last day of spring 2012 payment extension for financial aid students ~ Last day to have registration tuition and fees and housing/meal plan balances paid without being assessed a $20 late administrative fee and/or a $20 late housing fee ~ Last day to pay all registration tuition and fees and housing/meal plan balances to avoid a Business & Finance (Student Financials) hold placed on your student account.

Refund Deadlines

January 20, 2012 ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses and receive a full refund of mandatory tuition and fees

February 3, 2012 ~ Last day to drop from full-time to part-time and receive a full refund of the tuition fee difference (nonresident students also receive full refund of non-resident tuition fee)

February 20, 2012 ~ Last day for Open University students to withdrawal and receive a prorated refund of tuition only

April 6, 2012 ~ Last day to officially withdrawal or cancel all courses and receive a prorated refund of mandatory tuition and fees

Registration Deadlines

January 19, 2012 ~ Last day to register without being assessed a $25 Late Registration Fee

Installment Payment Plan

First Installment due with signed contract ~ Installments are due on or before the listed due date on contract ~ $20 Administrative Late Fee is assessed for each late payment made

February 7, 2012 ~ 2nd Installment Due

March 7, 2012~ 3rd Installment Due
