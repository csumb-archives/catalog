The EMBA program advising function is minimal since it is a cohort program and many decisions pertinent to other graduate programs do not apply. You must commit to completing your courses of study with your cohorts. However, in extraordinary circumstances, you may drop out of a cohort. In order to complete your program of study, you must wait until the courses required for degree completion are offered again and take the remainder of the required courses with a new cohort.

For more information, visit CSUMB.EDU/emba.
