You can reach our A-Z Directory by clicking the "Contact Info (A-Z)" link in the footer of any page. The A-Z Directory provides a search mechanism to find employees, and an alphabetical listing of all CSUMB department, program, and service names, nicknames and acronyms. It also offers listings by category.

The directories and guides listed in this section of the catalog will help you find career pathways, understand unique CSUMB terms , and guide you toward appropriate offices.
