The Marine Science B.S. is an applied marine sciences program emphasizing:

  • A rigorous, interdisciplinary science foundation integrating biology, chemistry and physics;
  • Strong preparation for careers and graduate study in applied marine ecology, marine science, marine resource assessment and management, and geospatial analysis;
  • Hands-on experience with advanced technologiesfor collecting, analyzing, visualizing and communicating scientific data;
  • Active, field-oriented learning through frequent field and laboratory experiments and community-based service projects and internships;
  • Real-world application of science and technology for the benefit of society;
  • Strong collaboration and communication skills, including written, oral and multimedia communication formats.
