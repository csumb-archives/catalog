GERM 101: Begin German I Assessment

Description
Alternative assessment for the first semester of German. Freshmen and sophomore transfers attempting to satisfy the Language ULR through assessment must enroll in GERM 101, 102, 201, and 202 concurrently, except for international students who may enroll only in GERM 202. Junior and senior transfers attempting to satisfy the Language ULR through assessment, must enroll in GERM 101, 102, and 201, except for international students who may enroll only in GERM 201 or 202.
Units:
4
