Credits Required

Total credits in the minor: 16 credits of upper-division coursework (grade "C" or above)

Students must complete

Two outcomes and their corresponding core courses (2 courses; 4 units each)
One elective course in one of your selected outcomes (4 units)
One more elective course from any of the outcomes (4 units)

Leadership and Management (Outcome)

Core course: BUS 305: Principles of Management Principles of Management
Electives: BUS 331: Organizational Behavior Oganizational Behavior, BUS 330: Human Resources Management Human Resources Management, BUS 372: Hotel/Resort Operations Hotel/Resprt Operations Management, BUS 493: Int'l Comparative Mgmt International Comparative Management


Marketing (Outcome)

Core course: BUS 306: Fundamentals of Marketing Fundamentals of Marketing
Electives: BUS 322: Prod Managemt/Marketing Product Management and Marketing for Startups, BUS 323: Service Marketing Service Marketing, BUS 421: Marketing Research Marketing Research, BUS 425: Global Marketing Global Marketing


Finance (Outcome)

Prerequisite: BUS 203: Financial Accounting Financial Accounting
Core course: BUS 307: Finance Finance
Electives: BUS 383: Intermediate Accounting I Intermediate Accounting I, BUS 384: Intermediate Accounting II Intermediate Accounting II, BUS 385: Income Tax I Income Tax I


Information Technology (Outcome)

Prerequisite: BUS 299: Excel Spreadsheet Excel Spreadsheet (If BUS 200: Intro to Business Computing not taken for tech/info ULR)
Core Course: BUS 308: Computer Information Systems Computer Information Systems
Electives: BUS 469 Database Management, BUS 460: E-Com: Emerg Bus Models-Iss E-Com: Emerging Business Models-Issues


Entrepreneurship (Outcome)

Prerequisite: BUS 203: Financial Accounting Financial Accounting (required)
Prerequisite: BUS 306: Fundamentals of Marketing Fundamentals of Marketing (required)
Prerequisite: BUS 307: Finance Principles of Finance (required)
Core Course: BUS 310: Entrepreneurship Entrepreneurship
Electives: BUS 322: Prod Managemt/Marketing Product Management and Marketing for Startups, BUS 451: Venture Formation/Finance Venture Formation and Finance (prereq BUS 310: Entrepreneurship)
