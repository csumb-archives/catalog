Application Fee

Application fee (non-refundable), payable by check or money order at time application is made: $55

Tuition Fee

The CSU makes every effort to keep student costs to a minimum.  Tuition and fees listed in published schedules or student accounts may need to be increased when public funding is inadequate. Therefore, CSU must reserve the right, even after initial tuition and fee payments are made, to increase or modify any listed tuition and fees, without notice, until the date when instruction for a particular semester or quarter has begun. All CSU listed tuition and fees should be regarded as estimates that are subject to change upon approval by the Board of Trustees.

PLEASE NOTE:  The following tuition fee schedules reflect the 12% increase approved by the CSU Board of Trustees on July 12, 2011.

Campus-Specific Fees

All matriculated students pay the following fees each semester regardless of students status or semester credits taken.

Materials, Services, and Facilities Fee $145.50
Associated Student Body Fee $48.00
Sports, Recreation, and Leisure - IRA Fee $30.00
Student Union Fee $22.00

Nonresident Students (U.S. and Foreign)

Nonresident tuition fee (in addition to other tuition and fees charged all students): $372 per semester credit.

The total nonresident tuition fee paid per term will be determined by the number of credits taken.  There is no cap on the amount charged for nonresident tuition fee per academic year

For more information about how nonresident status is determined, please refer to the Admissions section of the catalog.
