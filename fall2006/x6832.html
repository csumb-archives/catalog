Social Work Minor ~ Home

Planning to pursue a professional career in social work, counseling, case management, client advocacy, or corrections? The  Social Work Minor provides the practical knowledge and skills you will need to begin. It covers professional ethics and values, human behavior and development, and practice skills.

You will find all of the official information about the minor right here. Simply use the left-side navigation. For more detailed information including a look at internship opportunities and faculty bios, please visit the Health, Human Services, and Public Policy Department website.
