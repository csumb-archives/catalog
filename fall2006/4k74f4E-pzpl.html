SDR Mission Statement

"Student Disability Resources (SDR) promotes the empowerment, wellness and full integration of students with disabilities into campus life and the campus community. Further, we seek to provide academic and personal support services to students with disabilities in order for them to attain success at California State University, Monterey Bay.

Our staff and student assistants may facilitate accommodations for students with learning, mobility, hearing, speech, vision, or psychological disabilities, and/or chronic medical conditions. We may also assist students with temporary disabilities.

In addition, we provide CSUMB faculty, staff, and students with presentations and trainings, information and referral, policy issues, and collaboration for courses, events, and activities."
