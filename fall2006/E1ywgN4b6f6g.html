> Support Services

Support Services

The CSUMB Strategic Plan  makes a strong commitment to Student Learning and Support for Learning by identifying them as two of four themes guiding university operations. Moreover, CSUMB prides itself in being a student-centered university, and was lauded by the Western Association of Schools and Colleges accreditation team for standing out in that regard. As a result, CSUMB provides a full range of support services to help you academically and personally. So that you can connect with the support services that you need, these pages describe them briefly, link to their websites, and provide contact information.
