Policies & Regulations ~ Housing Policies

CSUMB enforces specific housing policies for all on-campus residents. These policies assure safety, legal compliance, and effective community living. A complete listing of these policies can be found in the housing license agreement and handbook. You will find the handbook and additional information on the CSUMB.EDU/housing website.
