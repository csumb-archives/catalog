Visual and Public Art, B.A.~ Careers and Graduate Study

Related Careers

VPA graduates have entered and achieved notable success as public artists, museum registrars, community arts administrators, exhibition coordinators, community artists, website designers, book illustrators, and art educators.

The organizations hiring them have included Boys and Girls Clubs, The Maritime Museum, YWCA, Berkeley Adult School, and Seniors Council.

Graduate Study

VPA graduates have attended a variety of graduate institutions including John F. Kennedy, California College of Art, New College, Art Center College of Design, St. Mary's, and CSU Monterey Bay.

They have entered and achieved notable success in the graduate fields of fine art and arts education.
