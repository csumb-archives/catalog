Multiple Subject Credential ~ Home

Want to give something back to your community by educating young people? CSUMB has everything you need to prepare for a rewarding elementary school teaching career, including two on-campus alternatives and the nontraditional, self-study CalStateTEACH alternative for earning your Multiple Subjects Teaching Credential. One of these options is sure to fit your schedule and circumstances.

You will find all of the official information about the on-campus options right here. Simply use the left-side navigation. You will find detailed CalStateTEACH information on the CalStateTEACH.net website.
