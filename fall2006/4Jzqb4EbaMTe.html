Instructional Science and Technology, M.A. ~ Academic Advising and Support Services

MIST provides you with a complete analysis of your academic preparation that serves as the basis for the program.

MIST faculty members conduct ongoing advising on an as-needed basis. They maintain office hours that you can use to access appropriate course and academic advising either in person or by telephone. All faculty members also extensively use email for advising.

MIST also provides peer advising specific to the program.

MIST Student Services  can be reached at 831-582-4791or by email at mist@csumb.edu
