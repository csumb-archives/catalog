Faculty Responsibilities

When contacted by a student who has been authorized by Student Disability Resources to receive a specified accommodation faculty need to:

  • Meet with that student (in person, on-line or by telephone) to review the request.

  • Sign appropriate forms in a timely manner and return a copy to SDR.

  • Provide recommended accommodations for the specified time and manner denoted in the student’s request.

  • Address any disability verification issues or concerns about requested accommodations directly to the office for Student Disability Resources.
