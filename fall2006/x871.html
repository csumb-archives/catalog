Human Development Minor ~ Home

If you make a list of academic fields that have universal value, this has to be included. Anyone who works with others,  raises children, deals with a partner, or wants a better understanding of self—in other words, everyone—will benefit from gaining insight into human development. Through our Human Development Minor, you will develop understanding of psychological, cognitive, emotional, biological, and familial changes across the lifespan. It will mark you as someone with scholarly insight into how individuals change and grow over time.

You will find all of the official information about the minor right here. Simply use the navigation to the left.
