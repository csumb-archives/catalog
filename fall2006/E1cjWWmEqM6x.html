Public Policy, Interdisciplinary M.A.~ Learning Environment

You will gain experience through internships and classroom coursework with our diverse team of faculty and professionals, who are affiliated with institutions throughout the Central Coast, and who offer opportunities for you to participate in challenging academic and applied policy analysis research through community-based, policy-development projects. You will use these resources and networks to complete a required, professional internship.

You may also take advantage of the educational, work, and study opportunities available through other internationally known institutions in our area, including the Monterey Institute of International Studies; University of California, Santa Cruz; and the Panetta Institute.

HHSPP campus classes are small, highly interactive, and utilize all aspects of our technologically advanced campus.
