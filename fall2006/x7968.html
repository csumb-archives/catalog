Department of Rehabilitation & Workability IV

Student Disability Resources assists students with application to and use of the Department of Rehabilitation/Vocational Rehabilitation.  The Department of Rehabilitation provides specialized career counseling to eligible students and may provide financial support for course books and supplies, etc. 

In partnership with the Department of Rehabilitation and the CSUMB office for Career Development, Student Disability Resources also collaborates with the WorkAbility IV Program.  For a referral to either program, schedule an appointment with an SDR advisor.
