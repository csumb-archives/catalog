Alternate Formats

Student Disability Resources Provides Alternate Formats of texts to students eligible for this service.  This section outlines the types of Alternate Formats available (also called Alt Formats or Alt Media) and Procedures for requesting Alternate Formats.  SDR works to provide Alternate Formats in conjunction with Assistive Technology coordinator Jeanie Wells.
