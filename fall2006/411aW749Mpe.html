Financial Aid ~ Scholarships

Scholarships are usually, but not always, financial aid that does not have to be repaid.

General Scholarship Form

If you wish to be considered as an applicant for all of the scholarships offered through CSUMB, you can obtain a General Scholarship Form from the Campus Service Center or the CSUMB.EDU/financialaid website. As funds become available the Financial Aid Office will notify you if supplemental information is needed, and will then forward your application for consideration and selection to the appropriate scholarship committee.
