Deaf & Hard-of-Hearing Services

To request services, we recommend that Students who are Deaf or Hard of Hearing make use of Priority Registration and meet with the SDR Deaf Services advisor by appointment immediately after registration.

Services include and are not limited to:

  • Sign Language Interpreting (Provided for classroom and required class-related activities.)

  • Assistive Listening Devices

  • Visual door alarms, emergency strobe lights, telephones, etc. in CSUMB Housing through the Residential Life Petition process.

  • Real time captioning (Provided for classroom and required class-related activities.)

  • Video transcription or captioning for videos and online audio instruction.


NOTE: SDR also assists students with learning to request communication access for other campus activities.  CSUMB is located in rural Monterey County where it is often challenging to secure qualified interpreters and captioners.  To obtain equal access, students need to plan ahead.
