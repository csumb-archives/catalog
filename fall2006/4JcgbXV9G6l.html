Outdoor Education/Recreation Minor ~ Home

No place in the world can beat the Monterey Bay region when it comes to diverse outdoor recreation opportunities. Our Outdoor/Recreation Minor leverages our natural resources to allow you to hone your outdoor and recreation knowledge and skills in preparation for a variety of careers and a lifetime of enjoyment.

You will find all of the official information about the minor right here. Simply use the left-side navigation.

For additional information including a look at faculty bios, please visit the Human Performance and Wellness Education Department website.
