Prospective Clients

SDR welcomes new clients throughout the year.

This section addresses the most frequently asked questions new students have about Student Disability Resources.  If your questions are not addressed below, consult the SDR Student Handbook, or feel free to contact us.

FAQ's:

  • What Services does SDR provide?
  • Who is eligible for services?
  • How do I request services?
  • I think I might have a Learning Disability, where can I get tested?

Student Disability Resources does not currently offer Disability Assessment as a service.  However, we can provide you with referrals to local institutions currently offering this service.

Monterey Peninsula College, Hartnell College, and most other Community Colleges provide Learning Disability Assessment services.  For more information, download "Learning Disability Assessment Options" or access it from the Forms & Publications section of the SDR Website.

For referrals to local practitioners regarding psychological conditions, please contact the CSUMB Personal Growth and Counseling Center (PGCC), located in Building 80. You can reach the PGCC by telephone at: (831) 582-3969.

 

NOTE: All students new to CSU Monterey Bay are urged to attend New Student Orientations.
