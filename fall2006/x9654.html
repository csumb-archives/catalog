Single Subject Credential ~ Faculty and Student Quotes

Teacher quality counts. It is one of the most critical elements influencing successful student learning. Good teaching is not obvious or simplistic. It takes a thorough knowledge of student and subject, the ability to make complex ideas understandable, and professional judgment. The CSUMB single subject program prepares talented individuals to become teaching professionals equipped to serve youth in linguistically and culturally diverse classrooms such as those in the Monterey Bay area.
~ Beverly Carter, Program Coordinator
