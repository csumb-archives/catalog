Print Catalog

We publish a two-year print version of the CSUMB Catalog in April of odd-numbered years. Through the 2003-2005 edition, the print catalog contained all official catalog information. As of the current 2005-2007 edition, the print version does not contain the depth of information provided on the comprehensive CSUMB.EDU/catalog website. Because the university provides high-speed wired and wireless online access to all current students and employees, we publish information needed only by those members of our campus community online but not in print. In the abridged print version, we provide just the information necessary for prospective students, and those who advise them, to make informed decisions regarding enrollment at CSUMB.
