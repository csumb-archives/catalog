First Year Seminar ~ Learning Environment

Many of the First Year Seminar sections are taught in technology-enhanced classrooms to support your learning.

Professors encourage active and collaborative learning. In First Year Seminar classes, you use critical thinking to analyze textual, visual, and verbal resources. You work collaboratively. This class encourages you to explore your values and think about your identity as a learner, citizen, and community member.
