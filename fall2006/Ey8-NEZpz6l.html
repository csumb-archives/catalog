2004-2005 Academic Year Calendar

Fall 2004
  • August 16-18 ~ New faculty orientation

  • August 19-22 ~ Fall 2004 Otter Welcome Days/Orientation
  • August 23 ~ Fall 2004 courses begin

  • January 21 ~ Last day of winter 2005 courses
  • January 28 ~ Winter 2005 grades due

  • January 24 ~ Spring 2005 courses begin
  • February 7 ~ Last day to add/drop courses

  • March 21-25 ~ Spring break
  • March 31 ~ César Chávez Day observed ~ Campus closed

  • May 19-20 ~ Capstone Festival

  • May 30 ~ Memorial Day observed ~ Campus closed
  • June 3 ~ Grades due ~ End of spring 2005 semester for faculty

Summer 2005

  • July 1 ~ End of first summer 2005 four-week (June) session
