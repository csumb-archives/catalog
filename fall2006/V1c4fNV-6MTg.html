Alternate Course Pathways/Course Substitutions

Some students with disabilities are eligible for Course Substitutions on the basis of disability. Petition procedures are available for:

  • Foreign Language University Learning Requirement (ULR)
  • Mathematics (Mathcom ULR)

To petition for course substitution in other subjects, please schedule an appointment with a SDR advisor.
