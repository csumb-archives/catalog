Special Education Credentials ~ Home

Recognizing the shortage of teachers prepared to serve students with disabilities throughout the state of California and nationwide, CSUMB provides a relevant and thoughtful course of study with well-respected faculty members and field practitioners.

The Special Education Program at CSUMB offers the Level I Mild Moderate Education Specialist Credential, the Level II Professional Clear Credential, and the Level I Moderate Severe Credential. The Level II Moderate Severe Credential will be offered in 2005-06 pending state approval. The Special Education Program also offers the Applied Behavior Analyst Certificate. You will find all of the official information about all of these credential and certificate options right here. Simply use the left-side navigation.
