Special Education Credentials ~ Overview

There continues to be a shortage of teachers prepared to serve students with disabilities throughout the state of California and nationwide. When teachers participate in a relevant and thoughtful course of study with well-respected faculty members and field practitioners, it increases the likelihood that they will remain teaching in the field.

At CSUMB, you will be well prepared to meet the instructional and social needs of students with disabilities. Small class size and individual attention characterize the program. The Special Education Program at CSUMB offers the Level I Mild Moderate Education Specialist Credential, the Level II Professional Clear Credential, and the Level I Moderate Severe Credential. The Level II Moderate Severe Credential will be offered in 2005-06 pending state approval. The Special Education Program also offers the Applied Behavior Analyst Certificate.
