> Catalog Home

CSUMB Catalog

This CSUMB.EDU/catalog website provides comprehensive current official information about academic programs, services, procedures, and policies. As such, it serves as the authoritative and complete catalog resource for current students and other members of our campus community.

We also provide an abridged print version of the CSUMB catalog geared toward prospective students who may not have easy access to this website.

For additional online academic information, please check the CSUMB.EDU/schedule website. In addition to the current listing of class offerings, that site provides extensive information related to registering for and taking classes.
