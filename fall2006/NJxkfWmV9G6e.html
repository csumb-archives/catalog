Prospective Clients

SDR welcomes new clients throughout the year.

This section addresses the most frequently asked questions new students have about Student Disability Resources.  If your questions are not addressed below, consult the SDR Student Handbook, or feel free to contact us.

FAQ's:

  • What Services does SDR provide?
  • Who is eligible for services?
  • How do I request services?

    The following are steps for students to request disability services or accommodations at CSU Monterey Bay:

    1. Contact the office for Student Disability Resources (SDR) and request a Prospective Student Packet, or download its contents from the SDR Forms page.

    2. Complete and submit the SDR Needs Assessment Form.

    3. Submit to SDR Disability Documentation/Verification from a licensed physician or qualified specialist.  Two forms are available for Documentation/Verification of Disability: The Disability Documentation form is intended for students with Psychological and/or Learning Disabilities (Includes ADHD/ADD). The Disability Verification form is intended for all other students with disabilities. (Examples: Mobility, Vision, Hearing, Chronic Illness, etc.)

    4. Schedule an appointment with a Disability Resources Advisor to determine how SDR can best serve you. Appointments can be scheduled in-office or by telephone at (831) 582-3672 (Voice), or 582-4024 (TTY).

  • I think I might have a Learning Disability, where can I get tested?
 

NOTE: All students new to CSU Monterey Bay are urged to attend New Student Orientations.
