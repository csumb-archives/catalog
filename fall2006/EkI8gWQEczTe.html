Telecommunications, Multimedia, and Applied Computing, B.S. ~ Faculty and Student Quotes

Our faculty has designed an integrated and collaborative curriculum, which prepares students to be future leaders in the fields of telecommunications, multimedia, and computing. Students work with faculty to assemble a portfolio of learning experiences that bridge traditional technology disciplines. Students combine this technological knowledge with design, communication, and management skills in the development of customized university experiences pertinent to their career interests and ambitions and to the needs of a global, multicultural society.
~ Dr. Eric Y. Tao, School Chair

I am amazed with all the technology CSUMB has on campus! I enjoy having all of the labs and equipment available at all times. The main reason I chose CSUMB was because, when I compared universities, it had the most impressive computer technology program. I plan to earn my degree in TMAC with an emphasis on multimedia design. My goal is to one day create my own computer program that will help millions of people all around the world.
~ Marco Alicea, Napa, California
