Human Communication Minor ~ Distinguishing Features

The minor prepares you to be an ethical, creative critical thinker and doer in a multicultural and increasingly interconnected global society.

As new humanists you will study literature and develop your own creative expression.

You will hear untold stories and help create the memory of the future.

You will be encouraged to learn history and to make history in the field, in the archives, and through multimedia.

You will study communication through ethical inquiry and inclusive, invitational dialogue, prioritizing care, compassion, equality, respect, and mutual understanding.

You will apply your knowledge to concrete, socially-engaged projects.
