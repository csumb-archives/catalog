Collaborative Health and Human Services, B.A. ~ Recommended Preparation

High School
Recommended
  • Community service
  • Four years of English with an emphasis on writing
  • Four years of second language
  • Social and behavioral sciences classes
Community College
Required
  • Statistics (This prerequisite may be taken concurrently in the program.)
Recommended
  • Social and behavioral sciences classes
  • Three semesters of second language
  • Human development
  • Human anatomy and physiology
  • English communication, especially writing
CSUMB Lower-Division
Required
  • STAT 200 (This prerequisite may be taken concurrently in the program.)
Recommended
  • Complete all lower-division University Learning Requirements including the following coursework:
    ~  Social and behavioral sciences classes
    ~  Second language (four semesters)
    ~  Human development
    ~  Human anatomy and physiology
    ~  English communication, especially writing
  • CHHS 485 or SBS 366  Research Methods
