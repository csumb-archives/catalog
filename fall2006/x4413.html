PROS 100 became FYS 100 in Fall 2003.  Only the Science/Technology  PROS 100  section and Technology PROS 100 section fulfilled the ULR. No other PROS 100 section fulfilled this ULR.  Approved from Fall 1997 through Spring 2003.
