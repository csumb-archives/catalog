Prospective Clients

SDR welcomes new clients throughout the year.

This section addresses the most frequently asked questions new students have about Student Disability Resources.  If your questions are not addressed below, consult the SDR Student Handbook, or feel free to contact us.

FAQ's:

  • What Services does SDR provide?
  • Who is eligible for services?

CSUMB and SDR may provide accommodations to: 

  • Students with learning, mobility, vision, physical, or psychological disabilities,
  • Students with chronic medical conditions
  • Students who are deaf or hard of hearing

To be eligible for accommodations/services CSU Monterey Bay Students must provide SDR with detailed current documentation of a verified disability from a qualified medical doctor or disability specialist.

Accommodations and services are provided in accordance with:

  • Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act

  • California State University Policy for the Provision of Accommodations and Support Services to Students with Disabilities.

Students who lack disability documentation should consult their physician or specialist.  For Prospective Clients requiring Disability Assessment/Testing, please see our section on Disability Assessment.
