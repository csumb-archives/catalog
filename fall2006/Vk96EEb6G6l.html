Admissions ~ Freshman Requirements

Generally, you will qualify for regular admission as a first-time freshman applicant if you meet the following requirements: 

  • Have graduated from high school, have earned a Certificate of General Education Development (GED), or have passed the California High School Proficiency Examination; and
  • Have a qualifiable eligibility index (see section on Eligibility Index); and
  • Have completed with grades of C or better each of the courses in the comprehensive pattern of college preparatory subject requirements (see Subject Requirements).

Contact a high school or community college counselor or CSUMB Admissions & Records if you have questions.

 

Note: Adopted at the California State University system level and mandated for inclusion in all CSU campus catalogs , gray-colored text applies to  all 23 campuses in the system. Black text pertains uniquely to CSUMB.
