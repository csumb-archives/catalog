Japanese Language and Culture Minor ~ Learning Environment

The community involved in Japanese language and area studies offers a small but active and welcoming academic environment. It includes exchange students from CSUMB's Japanese sister university, Obirin Diagaku, other CSUMB students, language facilitators, and faculty members. Each year, several Japanese major and minor students visit and study in Japan; they return with firsthand experiences that further enrich the community.
