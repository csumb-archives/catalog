Financial Aid ~ Work Study

Work-study is financial aid that is earned through part-time employment.

Federal Work Study

Federal Work Study is a federal employment program that places students in campus jobs. Students may work up to 20 hours per week. Priority for FWS is given to students demonstrating the highest financial need. Employment opportunities include community service. Due to limited funding, you must apply as soon after January 1 as possible.

Student Assistant Employment

The Student Assistant Employment program funded by CSUMB places students in part-time campus employment. For details and an employment application, you should call the CSUMB Career Development Office (831-582-3845) or visit the CSUMB.EDU/career website.
