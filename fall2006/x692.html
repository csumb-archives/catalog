Education, M.A. ~ Distinguishing Features

Our personalized curriculum fits your interests and needs. You have flexibility in selecting courses, thesis topics, readings in your courses, and projects to work on.

The seminar-like courses allow you to know your colleagues well.

Students describe the simultaneously supportive and challenging environment as transformational.

This program honors the working professional by providing a high quality MAE program that can be completed with 30-32 credits of coursework.

The Special Education Emphasis provides you with a solid background in legal and policy issues related to advocacy in the field.

We develop your technological sophistication, including your skills in applying technology to educational environments and your abilities to critique its uses.
