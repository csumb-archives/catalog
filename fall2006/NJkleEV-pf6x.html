Welcome to the Campus Service Center!

Regular Hours
  Mon-Thursday:        8:00am - 6:00 pm            Located in Bldg 47, First Floor 1
  Friday:                    9:00am - 5:00 pm            In Student Services Bldg.

The Campus Service Center ("The CSC") provides forms, information, and front counter support for Admissions, Records, Transcripts, and Financial Aid, and Cashiering. The CSC distributes parking permits (Prices),  Otter ID Cards and paychecks. The CSC is able to provide information and services with its access to student records in the Banner system and a fully functional cashiering system.
