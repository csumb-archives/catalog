Human Movement: Adapted Physical Activity Minor ~ Faculty and Student Quotes

"The Human Movement: Adapted Physical Activity Minor allows you to study the breadth and depth of various dimensions of human movement, specifically adapted physical activity. You will be prepared to provide programming within the framework of movement performance that includes the cognitive, affective, and psychomotor domains. This minor particularly enhances classroom teachers' skills to provide an active curriculum resulting in higher academic and social success for all of their students."
-bobbi bonacé, Ph.D., Chair, Human Performance and Wellness Education Department

"It [Human Movement] would be a wonderful background to have with a teaching credential."
-Laura Caldwell, Principal, Del Rey Woods Elementary, Monterey Peninsula Unified School District
