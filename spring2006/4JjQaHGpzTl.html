Computer Science & Information Systems Minor ~ Overview

The Computer Science & Information Systems Minor provides you with an opportunity to explore your particular interests in the development of software applications, computer networking, and information systems.

Integration with Majors

CSIS allows you to tailor your minor curriculum so that it can be integrated with many other major programs at CSUMB.

We recognize that we have an obligation to provide high quality educational opportunities to all CSUMB students who want a deeper understanding of information technology and communications design in order to enhance their learning experience and career opportunities.
