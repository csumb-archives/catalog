Financial Aid ~ Prepayment Policy

CSUMB has a prepayment policy requiring you to pay registration fees before registering for courses. Financial Aid applicants are granted a fee payment extension to allow additional time for award processing. The fee payment extension expires and all outstanding charges are due when all financial aid awards are paid out but no later than November 1 for fall semester and April 1 for spring semester. If university charges are outstanding after these deadlines, late charges will be assessed and holds will be placed. The Campus Service Center has a list of applicable fees.
