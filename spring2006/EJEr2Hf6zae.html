The social and behavioral sciences all share a common body of theory and method. This is so obvious that it is highly controversial!
~ Dr. George Baldwin, Division Director

The overall campus community has impressed me immensely. Everyone has always been so friendly and supportive. Because SBS is one of the smaller programs, students really get the benefit of small classroom atmosphere. And, with an interdisciplinary program like SBS, students have more freedom to choose and excel in their field of choice with a solid foundation from several different disciplines.
~ Melissa M. Padilla, Fremont, California
