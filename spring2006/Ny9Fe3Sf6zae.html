Business Administration, B.S. ~ Overview

Business Administration prepares you for careers in business, government, and the nonprofit sector where management, technology, critical thinking, and communication skills are essential. Our graduates' entrepreneurial and leadership traits, combined with their business skills and knowledge, have enabled them to launch their own businesses or move into high-potential jobs. Our goal is for you to graduate with not just a solid foundation in business knowledge and skills, but also behavioral attributes that give you a competitive advantage in either your chosen career path or graduate school.
