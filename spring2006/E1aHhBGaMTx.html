Athletics

A Division II member of the National Collegiate Athletic Association (NCAA) competing in the California Collegiate Athletic Association, we field intercollegiate athletic teams in baseball, basketball, cross country, golf, and soccer for men; basketball, cross country, golf, soccer, softball, volleyball, and water polo for women; and coed sailing.
