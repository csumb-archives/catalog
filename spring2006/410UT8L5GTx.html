> Campus Life

Campus Life

CSUMB believes that everyone in our community is a partner in the learning process and that the activities that happen outside of the classroom are a valuable part of the entire educational experience.  From the residence halls to the beach, CSUMB offers plenty of options to keep you engaged in campus life.  Whether you’re boogie boarding through Adventure Connections, playing on a boisterous intramural sports team, or discussing a film with the Chat the Movies club, there is always something to do.  So that you can connect with the groups and activities that fit your interests, these pages describe them briefly, link to their websites, and provide contact information.
