 4-Year Pathways To Graduation

Freshmen interested in the TMAC degree should plan on focusing primarily on completing their University Learning Requirements (ULRs) during their first two years of study.  During their sophomore or junior year they should register for CST 300. Only when the student has declared the TMAC major with Admissions and Records and has enrolled in CST 300, will he or she be officially considered a TMAC major.

There are many pathways a student may take to fulfill the ULRs and MLOs required to attain a TMAC degree. A suggested 4 year pathway is listed below to serve as a guideline for incoming freshmen going into the TMAC major.
