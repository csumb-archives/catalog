Admissions ~ Provisional Transfer Admission

CSUMB may provisionally admit you as a transfer applicant based on your academic preparation and courses planned for completion. If so admitted, the campus will monitor your final terms to ensure that you complete all required courses satisfactorily. All accepted applicants are required to submit an official transcript of all college-level work completed. The campus will rescind admission if you are found not to be eligible after the final transcript has been evaluated.

 

Note: Adopted at the California State University system level and mandated for inclusion in all CSU campus catalogs , gray-colored text applies to  all 23 campuses in the system. Black text pertains uniquely to CSUMB.
