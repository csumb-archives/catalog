Health and Wellness Minor ~ Home

The Health and Wellness Minor offers courses that focus upon both personal well-being and the skills to implement the content into chosen majors regardless of career or life path.  Anyone wanting to live optimally and assist family, community, and personal well-being will benefit.

Once a minor is declared, three courses are required covering both theory and practical implications.  These can come from the following content offerings:

  • Effectively Dealing with Modern Day Stress
  • Fundamentals and Issues involved in Human Sexuality
  • Life Long Nutrition/ Prevention Foundations 
  • Patterns and Dynamics of Relationships
  • Behavior Change and Support Strategies
  • Multicultural Ancestral Views of Well-Being and Healing

You will find all the official information about the minor using the left-side navigation on this page.

"My experience of seeing students develop throuogh this minor is that it enables a depth of maturity and skills in personal well-being and self-development which is directly applicable to their chosen careers.  People who want to work with others in a variety of settings such as schools, public organizations, social work, and business gain not only rich content, but also  can apply  their work and facilitate behavoir change .  All of this leads to higher levels of well-being and community responsibility."
- JoAnn Cannon, Dr. P.H., D. Min., Wellness Education Faculty
