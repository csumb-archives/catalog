Education, M.A. ~ Academic Advising and Support

You should select or be assigned an advisor upon being admitted to the program. Ideally, this should be a faculty member who has knowledge in your area of interest. Your advisor helps you plan your MAE program of study, and supports you intellectually as you conceptualize and complete your Action Thesis Project.
