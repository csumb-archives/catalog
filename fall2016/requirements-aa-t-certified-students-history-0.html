Requirements for AA-T certified students in History

If you transferred into CSUMB as an AA-T-certified student in History, you must complete the following courses for your bachelor's degree in Social and Behavioral Sciences with a concentration in Social History at CSUMB. If you are unsure about your transfer status, please talk to an advisor.

Download the CCC TMC for History

Complete ALL of the following courses:

  • SBS 300
  • SBS 300L
  • SBS 315
  • SBS 328
  • SBS 350 (also fulfills American Institutions requirement)
  • SBS 363
  • SBS 400
  • SBS 402 OR SBS 404S
  • SBS 405

Complete ONE of the following course combinations:

  • SBS 360S AND SBS 360L
  • SBS 361 AND SBS 361L
  • SBS 362 AND SBS 362L
  • SBS 366 AND SBS 366L

Complete THREE of the following courses:

  • SBS 304
  • SBS 306
  • SBS 325
  • SBS 332
  • SBS 348
  • SBS 355
  • SBS 378
  • SBS 381
  • SBS 382
  • SBS 383
  • SBS 385
  • SBS 386
  • SBS 388
  • SBS 475
  • GS 316
  • GS 320
  • GS 326
  • GS 339
  • GS 339S
  • GS 342
  • HCOM 324
  • HCOM 325
  • HCOM 345
  • HCOM 346
  • HCOM 350
  • HCOM 352
  • HCOM 353
  • HCOM 354
  • HCOM 356
  • HCOM 357
  • HCOM 358
  • HCOM 359
  • HCOM 365
  • HCOM 426
  • HCOM 445
  • JAPN 302
  • JAPN 307
  • SPAN 307
  • SPAN 308
  • SPAN 309
  • SPAN 311

Students must also demonstrate second language proficiency through the 201 level. Read more about the Language Proficiency Requirement.

In addition students must also meet the University's Upper Division Service Learning (D4) and the Graduate Writing Assessment Requirement (A4).

Students may select the elective coursework if necessary to complete 60 units.
