Admissions

As one of the 23 member campuses in the California State University system, CSUMB follows standardized admissions policies, procedures, and requirements adopted at the CSU system level.

Requirements for admission to CSUMB are in accordance with Title 5, Chapter 1, Subchapter 3, of the California Code of Regulations. Complete information is available on the CSUmentor.edu/planning.
