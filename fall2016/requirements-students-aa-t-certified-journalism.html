Requirements for students AA-T certified in Journalism

If you transferred into CSUMB as an AA-T-certified student in Journalism, you must complete the following courses for your bachelor's degree in Human Communication at CSUMB. If you are unsure about your transfer status, please talk to an advisor.

Download the CCC TMC for Journalism

Complete ALL of the following courses:

  • HCOM 300
  • HCOM 475
  • HCOM 312
  • HCOM 307S
  • HCOM 330
  • SBS 350 (Fulfills American Institutions Requirement, GE Area D2)

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 306
  • HCOM 309
  • HCOM 310

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 301
  • HCOM 406

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 338
  • HCOM 342
  • HCOM 343
  • HCOM 344 (RTI)
  • HCOM 345
  • HCOM 348 (RTI)

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 322
  • HCOM 323
  • HCOM 324
  • HCOM 325 (RTI)
  • HCOM 326
  • HCOM 327 (RTI)
  • HCOM 335
  • HCOM 336
  • HCOM 337

Complete THREE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 307
  • HCOM 307S
  • HCOM 310
  • HCOM 315
  • HCOM 316
  • HCOM 319
  • HCOM 334
  • HCOM 352
  • HCOM 370
  • HCOM 371
  • HCOM 385
  • HCOM 387
  • HCOM 388
  • HCOM 389

Upon prior consent of instructor:

  • HCOM 390
  • HCOM 406
  • HCOM 486
  • HCOM 487
  • HCOM 487S

Students must also demonstrate second language proficiency through the 201 level.Read more about the Language Proficiency Requirement. If you need to begin language study at the 101 level, consult your department chair to determine your 60 unit pathway.
