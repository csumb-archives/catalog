Requirements for students AA-T certified in Psychology

If you transferred into CSUMB from a California community college as an AA-T-certified student in Psychology, you must complete the following courses for your bachelor's degree in Psychology at CSUMB. If you are unsure about your transfer status, please talk to an advisor.

Download the CCC TMC for Psychology

Complete ALL of the following Psychology courses:

  • PSY 300
  • PSY 301/L OR PSY 305/L
  • PSY 302/L
  • PSY 310 series course (choose 1 from 310-319)
  • PSY 320 series course (choose 1 from 320-329)
  • PSY 330 series course (choose 1 from 330-339)
  • PSY 340 series course (choose 1 from 340-349)
  • PSY 350 series course (choose 1 from 350-359)
  • PSY 400

Complete ALL of the following courses:

  • Any approved upper division D2 course (such as SBS 350: Domination & Resistance: US or SBS 385)
  • Any approved D4 course
  • Any approved A4 course (GWAR)

Students are also required to demonstrate second language proficiency at the 201 level. Read more about the Language Proficiency Requirement.

Electives (additional units cannot bring total units to more than 60). Any PSY electives.
