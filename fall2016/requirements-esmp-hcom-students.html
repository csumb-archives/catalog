Requirements for ESMP-HCOM Students

The following courses are required for HCOM majors who have selected the English Subject Matter Preparation Concentration. These courses fulfill the requirements of the major and the concentration.

HCOM Students are required to complete one course approved as research or theory intensive; these courses are indicated as (RTI) in the course lists below.

Complete ALL of the following courses:

  • HCOM 300
  • HCOM 320 (RTI)
  • HCOM 327 (RTI)
  • HCOM 338
  • HCOM 429 (RTI)
  • HCOM 437
  • HCOM 475

Complete ONE of the following course combinations (not necessarily in the same semester):

  • HCOM 312 AND HCOM 339S
  • HCOM 317S AND HCOM 330

Complete EITHER Option A or Option B below:

Option A: Complete HCOM 356 AND ONE of the following courses (not necessarily in the same semester):

  • HCOM 307
  • HCOM 307S
  • HCOM 315
  • HCOM 316
  • HCOM 328
  • HCOM 329
  • HCOM 350
  • HCOM 350S (RTI)
  • HCOM 384 (RTI)
  • HCOM 385
  • HCOM 390
  • HCOM 446
  • HCOM 487S
  • HCOM 389 (Only with prior consent of the instructor)

Option B: Complete HCOM 328 OR HCOM 329 OR HCOM 350 AND one of the following courses (not necessarily in the same semester):

  • HCOM 346
  • HCOM 351
  • HCOM 352
  • HCOM 353
  • HCOM 354
  • HCOM 356
  • HCOM 359
  • HCOM 365 (RTI)
  • HCOM 445
  • HCOM 451
  • HCOM 454

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 304
  • HCOM 306
  • HCOM 309
  • HCOM 310
  • HCOM 403 (RTI)
  • HCOM 403S
  • HCOM 412 (RTI)

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 301
  • HCOM 404
  • HCOM 405
  • HCOM 406 (RTI)
