Requirements for AA-T certified students in History

If you transferred into CSUMB as an AA-T-certified student in History, you must complete the following courses for your bachelor's degree in Human Communication at CSUMB. If you are unsure about your transfer status, please talk to an advisor.

To complete the requirements of the HCOM degree students must complete at least one course which is designated as RTI -Research/Theory Intensive.

Download the CCC TMC for History

Complete ALL of the following courses:

  • HCOM 300
  • HCOM 312
  • HCOM 350S (RTI)
  • HCOM 475
  • SBS 350 (Fulfills American Institutions, GE Area D 2)

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 306
  • HCOM 309
  • HCOM 310

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 301
  • HCOM 406

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 338
  • HCOM 342
  • HCOM 343
  • HCOM 344
  • HCOM 345
  • HCOM 348

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 322
  • HCOM 323
  • HCOM 324
  • HCOM 325 (RTI)
  • HCOM 326
  • HCOM 327 (RTI)
  • HCOM 335
  • HCOM 336
  • HCOM 337

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 330
  • only with prior consent of the instructor: HCOM 332, HCOM 333, HCOM 334 or HCOM 432

Complete ONE course from each of the following groups:

Group A. History

  • HCOM 346
  • HCOM 352
  • HCOM 353
  • HCOM 359
  • HCOM 363 (RTI)
  • HCOM 365 (RTI)
  • HCOM 366
  • HCOM 367
  • HCOM 445 (RTI)
  • HCOM 451 (RTI)

Group B. Historical Narrative

  • HCOM 324
  • HCOM 325
  • HCOM 336
  • HCOM 344 (RTI)
  • HCOM 348 (RTI)
  • HCOM 426
  • HCOM 432 (RTI)
  • HCOM 432S (RTI)

Group C. Multimedia

  • HCOM 328
  • HCOM 329
  • HCOM 356

Students must also demonstrate second language proficiency through the 201-level

Read more about the Language Proficiency Requirement through the 201 level. If you need to begin language study at the 101 level, consult your department chair to determine your 60 unit pathway.
