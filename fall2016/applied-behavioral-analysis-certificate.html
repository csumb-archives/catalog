Applied Behavioral Analysis Certificate

CSU Monterey Bay offers a Certificate Program in Behavior Analysis. The program consists of the following Special Education Program university courses:

  • SPED 566
  • SPED 571
  • SPED 681
  • SPED 578

These courses are only offered on campus. Students who are not already enrolled in one of our special education credential programs may enroll in these courses through CSUMB's Open University. Please note: certificates may only be awarded to matriculated students.

For additional information on the program please contact Dr. Josh Harrower.
