Master of Business Administration

Through online courses, the executive or traditional track of the M.B.A. program allows you to complete 48 units of required courses within 24 months. A fixed curriculum, graduate business cohort program, the MBA offers courses through Extended Education in Fall, Spring A, Spring B, and Summer terms. When admitted you become a member of an approximately 25-student cohort that progresses through the program in a lock-step manner.

For additional information visit mba.csumb.edu.

Required Courses

Complete all of the following courses:

  • BUS 601
  • BUS 603

  • BUS 604

  • BUS 605

  • BUS 626

  • BUS 631

  • BUS 632

  • BUS 652

  • BUS 661

  • BUS 681

  • BUS 691

Learning Outcomes

  1. Understand, analyze, and evaluate business conditions by applying business knowledge in the core functional business areas.
  2. Evaluate business situations and create written business documents to achieve results.
  3. Evaluate business situations and orally present the synthesized findings.
  4. Evaluate and create business opportunities by applying critical thinking processes.
  5. Analyze and evaluate business problems using quantitative data and methods.
