Requirements for Students AA-T Certified in Art History

If you transferred into CSUMB as an AA-T-certified student in Art History, you must complete the following courses for your bachelor's degree in Visual and Public Art at CSUMB. If you are unsure about your transfer status, please talk to an advisor.

Download the CCC TMC for Art History

Complete ALL of the following courses:

  • VPA 300
  • VPA 302 (taken twice)
  • VPA 307 or VPA 308
  • VPA 310
  • VPA 313
  • VPA 320S or VPA 306S
  • VPA 309
  • VPA 405 (to be taken twice in the senior year)

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • SBS 350
  • SBS 385

Students must demonstrate second language proficiency through the 201 level.Read more about the Language Proficiency Requirement.

If additional coursework is necessary to complete the required 60 units, students may select from the following recommended courses:

  • VPA 301
  • VPA 312
  • VPA 324
  • VPA 304
