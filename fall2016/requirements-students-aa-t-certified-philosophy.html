Requirements for students AA-T certified in Philosophy

If you transferred into CSUMB as an AA-T-certified student in Philosophy, you must complete the following courses for your bachelor's degree in Human Communication at CSUMB. If you are unsure about your transfer status, please talk to an advisor.

To complete the requirements of the HCOM degree students must complete at least one course which is designated as RTI -Research/Theory Intensive.

Download the CCC TMC for Philosophy

Complete ALL of the following courses:

  • HCOM 300
  • HCOM 317S
  • HCOM 330
  • HCOM 475
  • SBS 350 (Fulfills American Institutions, GE Area D 2)

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 315
  • HCOM 328
  • HCOM 329
  • HCOM 385
  • HCOM 389

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 306
  • HCOM 309
  • HCOM 310

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 301
  • HCOM 404
  • HCOM 405
  • HCOM 406

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 338
  • HCOM 342
  • HCOM 343
  • HCOM 344 (RTI)
  • HCOM 345
  • HCOM 348 (RTI)

Complete ONE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 322
  • HCOM 323
  • HCOM 324
  • HCOM 325 (RTI)
  • HCOM 326
  • HCOM 327 (RTI)
  • HCOM 335
  • HCOM 336
  • HCOM 337

Complete THREE of the following courses:

  • HCOM 301
  • HCOM 304
  • HCOM 306
  • HCOM 307
  • HCOM 307S
  • HCOM 309

  • HCOM 312
