In order to graduate, you will also need to complete your general education and university requirements.

Lower Division Major Requirements
Complete the following COMMUNITY COLLEGE courses (in the ADN Curriculum): 
  •  Nursing Theory I
  •  Nursing Theory II
  • Nursing Theory III
  •  Nursing Theory IV
  • Clinical Practicum I
  • Clinical Practicum II
  • Clinical Practicum III 
  • Clinical Practicum IV
Upper Division Major Requirements
Complete the following CSUMB courses:
  • CHHS 302: Professional Communication
  • NURS 300: Intro to Prof Nursing
  • NURS 306: Chronic Care Mgmt
  • NURS 311: QSEN: Pt. Centered Care
  • NURS 312: QSEN:Teamwork & Collaborat
  • NURS 313: QSEN:Evidence Based Practice
  • NURS 314: QSEN:Safety & Qual. Improve
  • NURS 315: QSEN: Informatics
  • NURS 321: Info Mgmt in Nursing
  • NURS 330: Research Methods
  • NURS 340: Mgmt Public & Nonprofit Orgs
  • NURS 360: Nurs Sociopolitical Envmt
  • NURS 395: Special Topics
  • NURS 410: Health & Soc. Policy Analys
  • NURS 421: Genomics in Aging
  • NURS 490: Community Health Nursing
  • NURS 499S: Community Health Practicum
