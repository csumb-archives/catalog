Lecturers

The part-time faculty is comprised of mathematics and statistics educators. Their diverse professional backgrounds, coupled with experience and compassion, translates into a learning atmosphere that is positive and engaging. In the University environment, Lecturer is the official title of Instructors who are not on the track for Tenure.
