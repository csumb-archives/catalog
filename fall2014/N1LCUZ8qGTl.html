The CHHS faculty and student peer advisers are strongly committed to providing you with direct one-on-one advising and referral to additional campus resources.

Freshmen and sophomores work with Center for Advising, Career, and Student Success for guidance on General Education requirements. 

As juniors entering the CHHS major, students can meet with a CHHS peer advisor or faculty advisor, who will guide them in planning their coursework and pathway through the major.

The CHHS field program coordinator advises and assists majors with meeting their field-based course requirements for graduation.

For more information, contact our CHHS peer advisor at 831-582-4163, visit our website, email us at chhsadvising@csumb.edu, or stop by our office in Ocean Hall, Suite D to sign up for an advising appointment.

CHHS has a strong collaborative relationship with the Academic Skills Achievement Program for students who need assistance with writing skills, and with the Personal Growth and Counseling Center for students who need to deal with personal issues and to optimize decision-making, problem-solving, communication, stress management and conflict resolution skills.
