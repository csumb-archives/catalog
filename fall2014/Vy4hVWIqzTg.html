Credits Required

A minimum of 12 credits in the minor plus prerequisite.

Credits at CSUMB: Consult with advisor.

Prerequisites

KIN 374KIN 374: Phys Ed for Elem Sch Child

KIN 374LKIN 374L: Phys Ed for Elem Sch Child Lab

Outcomes, Courses and Assessment

You must achieve each of the Learning Outcomes (LO) designated below by successfully completing the listed courses or alternative assessment options.

LO 1 Knowledge and Perspectives in Adapted Physical Activity ~ Students understand the fundamental principles and issues common to the field of adapted physical education, including the neurosensory developmental approach, etiology (the cause and origin) of most commonly occurring disabilities, indicators and contraindicators related to body awareness and academic success.

Complete the following course:

KIN 376KIN 376: Prin of Mov for Ind w/ Disab

LO 2 Diversity and Multiculturalism ~ Students understand complex social issues for individuals with disabilities.

Complete one of the following courses:

KIN 372KIN 372: Socio Persp Spor w/ Dis Emph

KIN 375KIN 375: Motor Behavior

LO 3 Collaboration & LO 4 Leadership and Social Responsibility ~ Students work in multicultural team settings to contribute to the development of multicultural classrooms and develop skills in working with diverse children. Students display a social conscience at the individual, community, school or agency level regarding individuals with disabilities. 

Complete the following course:

KIN 377KIN 377: Neurological Impairment
