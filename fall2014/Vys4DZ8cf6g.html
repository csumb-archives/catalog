Your Otters are NCAA Division II members competing in the prestigious California Collegiate Athletic Association (CCAA).  CCAA members have won 152 NCAA Championships (102 men, 50 women); all time, far and away the most of any Division II conference.

The 12-member league is home to some of the nation's finest athletic programs in Cal Poly Pomona, Cal State Dominguez Hills, Cal State Easy Bay, Cal State L.A., Cal State San Bernardino, Cal State Stanislaus, Chico State, Humboldt State, UC San Diego, San Francisco State, and Sonoma State.

The Department of Athletics serves 250+ student-athletes competing in baseball, men & women's basketball, men & women's cross country, men & women's golf, men and women's soccer, softball, women's volleyball, and women's water polo.
