Upon entering the program, each student is assigned a faculty advisor. Your advisor will work closely with you and provide most of your academic support as you progress through the program. In addition, a university supervisor will help guide the development of your teaching skills in the field, along with your school-based cooperating master teacher (for student teachers) or mentor support provider teacher (for interns). The program coordinator, other program faculty, the field coordinator and our credential analyst are also available to assist you with specific questions pertinent to their roles in the program.
