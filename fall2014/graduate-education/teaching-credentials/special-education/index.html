Recognizing the shortage of teachers prepared to serve students with disabilities throughout the state of California and nationwide, CSUMB provides a relevant and thoughtful course of study with well-respected faculty members and field practitioners.

The Special Education Program at CSUMB offers the Preliminary Mild Moderate Education Specialist Credential, the Level II Professional Clear Credential, and the Preliminary and Level II Moderate Severe Education Specialist Credentials. The Special Education Program also offers the Behavior Analysis Certificate.

For more detailed information on the application process, field placement and more, please visit the Special Education website.
