Leaders must possess both business acumen and technological expertise in the high-tech marketplace fueled by rapid technological advances and globalization. Those who become savvy in both management and technology will empower themselves to succeed. The M.S. in Management and Information Technology tightly integrates knowledge of core management disciplines and information technology to prepare graduates for high-tech management positions or to launch a new business.  Additionally, MSMIT students gain skills to effectively lead an ethnic, cultural, and gender diverse workforce. Designed for full-time working professionals, the program provides a flexible learning experience and leverages their current job and past experience.

For more detailed information including student work and faculty bios, please visit the MSMIT pages on the School of Information Technology and Communications Design website.
