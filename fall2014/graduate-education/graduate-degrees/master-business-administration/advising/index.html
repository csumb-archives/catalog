Advising for which classes to take is not needed because as a cohort program, you start with one group of students and follow a consecutive set of classes together. Because the classes are in order, and students are not able to choose the order, the process is simple for the students. In extraordinary circumstances, you may take a break and pick up with the next cohort of students.

Administrative advising is available to assist students' understanding and follow the campus rules and regulations. Success advising is available to assist students in the electronic classroom environment.

Our goal is to be there for you while you complete your MBA program successfully and efficiently.

For more information, visit mba.csumb.edu.
