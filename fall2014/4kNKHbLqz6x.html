If you transferred into CSUMB as an AA-T-certified student in Geography, you must complete the following courses for your bachelor's degree in Social and Behavioral Sciences with a concentration in Geographic Information Systems (GIS) at CSUMB. If you are unsure about your transfer status, please talk to an advisor.

Complete ALL of the following courses:
  • SBS 300/L: Major/ProSem:Theo/Meth/SBS
  • SBS 328: Social Science Theory Module
  • SBS 361/L: Introduction to GIS
  • SBS 366/L: Rsrch Mthd for SBS:Quant Emph
  • SBS 356: Introduction to Geography
  • SBS 371: Applications/GIS/Soc/Scie/Busi
  • ENVS 436: Rmt Sns/Image Process
  • SBS 400: Senior Capstone Seminar I
  • SBS 405: Assessment Lab/Grad/Srs
Complete ONE of the following courses:
  • SBS 402: Senior Capstone Seminar II (For students who have already taken a non-capstone upper-division service learning course)
  • SBS 404S: Service Learning Capstone (For students who have NOT taken an upper-divsion service learning course)
Complete TWO of the following courses:
  • SBS 351: Global Econ:Theory, Ethics
  • ENVS 332: Intro to GIS/GPS
  • CST 305: 3D Modeling & Animation
  • CST 363: Intro to Database Systems
  • BUS 469: Database Management
    If the American Insitutions requirement has not been met before transfer, students may substitute SBS 385: Environmental History of CA for one of the courses in the list above.
Students must also demonstrate second language proficiency at the 201 level. 

Read more about the Language Proficiency Requirement.

Students may select additional elective coursework if necessary to complete 60 units.
