In order to graduate, you will also need to complete your general education and university requirements.

Major Requirements and Learning Outcomes are identified below. In order to graduate, students must also meet all general education and university requirements. 

WLC Major requirements are determined by emphasis. Please see the appropriate emphasis below.

All WLC Majors will complete the following courses:
  • WLC 300: WLC Major ProSeminar
  • WLC 400: WLC Major Capstone
Select one of the two following emphases:
Emphasis in Japanese
Complete the following course:
  • JAPN 320S: Advanced Japanese SL
Complete TWO of the following courses:
  • JAPN 301: Adv Japanese Lang Cult & Com
  • JAPN 302: History of Japan
  • JAPN 303: Business Japanese
  • JAPN 304: Intro Translate & Interpret
  • JAPN 312: Japanese for the Professions
  • JAPN 380: Japan: Land and People
  • JAPN 401: Structure of Japanese Lang
  • JAPN 402: Japanese Literature

  • JAPN 407: Japan in Asia
  • JAPN 408: Business In Japan

  • SPAN 325: Hispanic Cinema

  • SPAN 370: The Chicano Community

Students are strongly encouraged to complete:
  • JAPN 400: Adv Interpersonal Com OR JAPN 403: Pre-Capstone OR JAPN 404: Advanced Japanese Research
