Overview

Both the Executive and Traditional tracks of the the MBA program require:

  • A bachelor's degree from an accredited college or university.
  • Official transcripts from all institutions attended including both undergraduate and graduate, including the institution from which you obtained your undergraduate degree.
  • A completed CSUMB application form.
  • A statement of purpose for embarking on this degree program.
  • Minimum GPA 2.5 and above.

Executive Track

  • The Executive Track requires significant work experience. Approximately seven years of work experience, with approximately three of those years in a managerial/supervisory capacity.
  • A current résumé that details your work experience.
  • One letter of reference that directly relate to the student's potential performance in the MBA program. 

Traditional Track

  • One year of professional experience.

 

For more information, visit mba.csumb.edu.
