CSUMB’s Tanimura & Antle Family Memorial Library provides access to more than 50,000 full-text electronic and print journals, magazines, and newspapers, and about 185,000 print and electronic books.  The library’s print book collections are located on the two stack floors with quiet and group study areas located throughout the three floors of the building.

The library also contains auditoria and smaller classroom and seminar rooms, a café and 24-hour study space, relevant student support programs focused on tutoring, undergraduate research, writing, advising, and first year seminar, and group study rooms and classroom spaces well-equipped with media technology.

The first-floor Reference Desk area and a second floor open computer lab offer computers with printers to access the library's online catalog and other computing software to complete course assignments.  Reference librarians are available for research assistance throughout the week, and there is an IT Help Desk near the reference area and the café for hardware and software support.
