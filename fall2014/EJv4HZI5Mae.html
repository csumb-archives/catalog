In VPA 300: VPA Major ProSeminar, you will prepare your Individual Learning Plan. The department chair will oversee VPA advising. Any full-time faculty member can assist in your advising. You meet with an advisor every semester to track progress.

Lower Division Advising is handled by the Center for Advising, Career & Student Success.

VPA students also help each other through peer mentoring.
