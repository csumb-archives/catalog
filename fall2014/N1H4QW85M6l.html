The College of Science, Media Arts and Technology houses several academic units and administers a variety of academic offerings including undergraduate and graduate degrees, minors, and certificate programs as listed below. The academic units within the college organize learning experiences thematically and topically with an emphasis on finding workable and creative solutions to important local, regional, national, and global problems. Through their studies, students gain the knowledge and practical skills necessary to adapt and succeed while making useful contributions in a rapidly changing world.

Full descriptions of the divisions, schools, and departments may be found on the SMART college website or the individual unit websites as listed below. Official catalog descriptions of their academic offerings may be reached directly by clicking on the title of a program in the list below.
