In order to graduate, you will also need to complete your general education and university requirements.

Cinematic Arts and Technology Major Required Courses

Complete all of the following courses:
  • CART 237: Intro to Video
  • CART 230: The Art of Storytelling
  • CART 228: Cinematic History 1849-1960
  • CART 300: Major ProSeminar
  • CART 301: Practical Application
  • CART 329: Contemporary Media History
  • CART 330: Cinematic Storytelling
  • CART 361: Intro to Non-Linear Editing
  • CART 399S: Community-Based Media
  • CART 490: Research & Development
  • CART 491: Capstone Completion & Asmnt
Complete FOUR UNITS from the following courses:
  • CART 203: Pre-Prod Research Methods
  • CART 206: Marketing Strategies
  • CART 207: Distribution Research Methods
  • CART 208: Bus Fund for Creative Prod
  • CART 223: DVD Distribution Workshop
  • CART 225: Web Distribution Workshop
Complete ONE of the following courses:
  • CART 340: Screenwriting
  • CART 315: Visual Design
Complete ONE of the following courses:
  • CART 339: Advanced Video Prod Lect
  • CART 341: Directing for the Camera
  • CART 376: Documentary Making
  • CART 422: Animation

In addition, students must complete 12 units of upper-division CART courses not already taken to fulfill the requirements listed above.
