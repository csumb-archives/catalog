Semester Credits Required

Total credits in the minor: 16

To enter the minor, you must:

  • Have an overall GPA of 2.0 or better
  • Have completed at least 45 semester units

To graduate with a Latin American Studies Minor, you must have received a grade of C or higher in at least four courses approved by one of the LAS faculty advisors. The courses must come from both the humanities and social sciences, with a minimum of two courses in each area. A list of approved courses is provided below.

You must also demonstrate that you have Intermediate-High language proficiency in either Spanish or Portuguese by:

  • Completing an upper-division Spanish language course at CSUMB
  • Spanish or Portuguese language study in an overseas program
  • Transferring equivalent course credits
  • Take an approved assessment at the Intermediate-High level
Outcomes, Courses and Assessment

You must achieve all of the Learning Outcomes listed below by successfully completing the listed courses or alternative assessment options.

You must complete with a grade of "C" or higher one of the following learning experiences early in your program of study

  • SPAN 306: Cultur/Civ Span Ltn Amer
  • SPAN 309: Hist & Polit Of Latn Amer

You must have received a grade of C or higher in at least four courses approved by one of the LAS faculty advisors. The courses must come from both the humanities and social sciences, with a minimum of two courses in each area. A list of approved courses is provided below.
