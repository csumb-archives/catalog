Faculty members work with community organizations to find the appropriate fit between course learning outcomes and the needs and resources of each organization. Instructors provide you with a list of sites from which to choose, and they may even arrange class visits or field trips to the sites to guide you toward finding an organization that will appreciate your talents and skills. SLI houses MYSLP, a database which contains information on over 500 organizations interested in working with YOU!

In service learning courses, the community is your classroom. Each semester CSUMB students are actively engaged in schools and community based organizations.  Below is a list of our Core partners from the 2012-2013 academic year:
