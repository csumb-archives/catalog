CSU Monterey Bay has partnerships with universities in much of the world. The partnership are available to qualified CSUMB students but may be competitive based on qualifications, academic record, or personal suitability.  Eligibility for all programs is listed with the program information.

Unless otherwise noted as CSUMB-only, all locations are adopted at the California State University system level and mandated for inclusion in all CSU campus catalogs.
