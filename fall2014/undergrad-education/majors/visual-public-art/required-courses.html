In order to graduate, you will also need to complete your general education and university requirements.

Major Requirements are identified below. In order to graduate, students must also meet all general education and university requirements. 

Complete ALL of the following courses:
  • VPA 300: VPA Major ProSeminar
  • VPA 302: VPA Visiting Artist Series  (to be taken twice)
  • VPA 307: Diverse Hist Contemp Art  OR  VPA 308: Ways Of Seeing
  • VPA 310: Mixed Media
  • VPA 313: Regional Art History
  • VPA 320S: Museum Studies  OR  VPA 306S: Digital Public Art
  • VPA 309: Materials & Methods 
  • VPA 405: Senior Capstone
Following completion of the VPA Core Curriculum, students select an additional 26 units of VPA coursework at either the upper- or lower-division level in order to complete the major coursework.
