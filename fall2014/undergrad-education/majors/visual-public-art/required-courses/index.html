In order to graduate, you will also need to complete your general education and university requirements.

Complete ALL of the following courses:
  • VPA 300VPA 300: VPA Major ProSeminar
  • VPA 302VPA 302: VPA Visiting Artist Series  (to be taken twice)
  • VPA 307VPA 307: Diverse Hist Contemp Art  OR  VPA 308VPA 308: Ways Of Seeing
  • VPA 310VPA 310: Mixed Media
  • VPA 313VPA 313: Regional Art History
  • VPA 320SVPA 320S: Museum Studies  OR  VPA 306SVPA 306S: Digital Public Art
  • VPA 309VPA 309: Materials & Methods 
  • VPA 405VPA 405: Senior Capstone
Following completion of the VPA Core Curriculum, students select an additional 26 units of VPA coursework at either the upper- or lower-division level in order to complete the major coursework.
