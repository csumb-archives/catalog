--- layout: archive title: Learning Outcomes | CSUMB Catalog year: fall2014 ---

MLO 1 Professionalism:  Articulate and demonstrate professional values and ethics, commitment to caring and continued learning as the foundation for practice in the baccalaureate generalist role.

MLO 2 Professional Communication:  Employ critical listening, speaking and writing skills to achieve quality outcomes.  Utilize critical thinking skills in effective interdiscliplinary collaboration.

MLO 3 Population Health:  Evaluate social determinants of health and genomics on the health status of diverse individuals and communities.  Identify how these issues impact health promotion.

MLO 4 Health Care Systems Management:  Analyze the financial structure and leadership concepts utilized by health care systems and organizations.  Recognize the impact of regulatory agenices on health care systems.

MLO 5 Information Technology in Nursing:  Demonstrate the ability to effectively utilize technology to access and organize data, and communicate critical information necessary in contemporary nursing practice.  Articulate the impact of technology on patient safety.

MLO 6 Knowledge of Health Care Policy:  Critique financial and regulatory policy issues, politics and sources of power which impact social welfare, individual, community and global health.

MLO 7 Evidence Based Practice:  Exercise integration of evidence-based best practices and research knowledge in preparation for leadership roles and advocacy in professional nursing.

MLO 8 Cultural Humility:  Display cultural humility when designing and delivering care to diverse individuals and populations across the lifespan.

MLO 9 Leadership:  Exhibit leadership roles using leadership theory and concepts to facilitate quality, safety and collaborative problem solving.

MLO 10 Service in Nursing:  Participate in Service Learning locally and globally at CSUMB through social justice awareness, responsibility, and civic engagement by linking clients across the lifespan to healthcare services in culturally, linguistically, and economically diverse communities.
