In order to graduate, you will also need to complete your general education and university requirements.

Complete ALL of the following core courses:
  • LS 277S: Schooling in Modern Society
  • LS 300: Major ProSeminar
  • LS 398: Soc Found of Multicult Ed
  • LS 398S: Soc Found of Multicult Ed
  • LS 400: Senior Capstone
 Complete ALL of the following courses in Content Area 1
(Reading, Language, Literature, History, and Social Science):
  • GS 214: Global History I: to 1500
  • SBS 252: Wrl/Reg/Geo/Cul/Soc/Sp:On-line
  • LS 394: Multi Lit Chld & Yng Adults
  • LS 394S: Mlti Lit Child & Yng Adults
  • LING 392: Nat Lang/Lang Acquis
  • HCOM 251: Introduction to US History  OR  SBS 111: Reading/Writing US History
  • SBS 385: Environmental History of CA  OR  SBS 386: Social/Pol/Hist/CA
Complete ALL of the following courses in Content Area 2
(Math and Science):
  • PHYS 121: Integrated Physical Science
  • PHYS 121L: Integrated Phy Science Lab
  • GEOL 210: Introduction to Earth Science
  • BIO 204: Introduction to Life Science
  • MATH 308: Elementary Math - Adv Viewpt A
  • MATH 309: Elementary Math - Adv Viewpt B
 Complete ALL of the following courses in Content Area 3
(Physical Education, Human Development, Visual and Performing Arts):
  • LS 233: Arts in the School & Comm
  • HDEV 260: Intro to Child Development
  • KIN 374: Phys Ed for Elem Sch Child
  • KIN 374L: Phys Ed for Elem Sch Child Lab

 

To complete the requirements of the Liberal Studies degree, students select either
the Traditional Pathway or the Integrated Teacher Preparation Pathway (iTEP)

Traditional Pathway
Complete ALL of the following ADVANCED INQUIRY IN EDUCATION courses:
  • LS 380: Teaching for Social Change
  • LS 383: Innov Approach to Schooling  OR  LS 384: Issues in Comp & Intl Ed
  • LS 391: Diversity in Ed Settings  OR  LS 362: Immgrnts & Equity Iss in Ed  OR LS 310: Ed Issues & Latino Comm
  • complete at least one additional course from this list
Complete one MINOR or EMPHASIS
