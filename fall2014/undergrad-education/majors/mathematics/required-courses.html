In order to graduate, you will also need to complete your general education and university requirements.

Complete All of the following courses:
  • MATH 150: Calculus I
  • MATH 151: Calculus II
  • MATH 170: Discrete Mathematics
  • MATH 250: Multivariate Calculus
  • MATH 265: Diff Equs & Linear Algebra
  • CST 231: Problm-Solving/Programng
  • MATH 300: Major Proseminar
  • MATH 320: App Prob and Stat
  • MATH 322: Foundations of Modern Math
  • MATH 330: Advanced Linear Algebra
  • MATH 400: Capstone Seminar
  • MATH 410: Modern Algebra I
  • MATH 420: Mathematical Analysis I

Complete the general requirements for a Mathematics B.S. or select either the PURE MATHEMATICS or the  MATHEMATICS SUBJECT MATTER PREPARATION concentration:

Mathematics  B.S. (no concentration - 21 units)
Complete the following courses:
  • MATH 411: Modern Algebra II OR  MATH 421: Mathematical Analysis II
  • MATH 361S: Mathematics Tutors OR  MATH 362S: Mathematics Consultants
Complete THREE of the following MATH or STAT Electives not counted above:
