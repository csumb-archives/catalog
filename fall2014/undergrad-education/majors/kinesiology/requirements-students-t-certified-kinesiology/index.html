If you transferred into CSUMB as an AS-T-certified student in Kinesiology, you must complete the following courses for your bachelor's degree in Kinesiology at CSUMB. If you are unsure about your transfer status, please talk to a Kinesiology faculty advisor as soon as possible.

Complete ALL of the following:
  • Any approved GWAR course 
  • Any approved American Institutions course 
  • Any language courses necessary to complete the CSUMB Language Proficiency Requirements
Complete ALL of the following courses:
  • KIN 300KIN 300: Major Pro-Seminar
  • KIN 360KIN 360: Nutritional Science
  • KIN 360LKIN 360L: Nutritional Science Lab
  • KIN 420KIN 420: Essential of Exercise Science
  • KIN 497KIN 497: Senior Capstone I
  • KIN 499KIN 499: Senior Capstone II
  • KIN 345KIN 345: Sports Ethics
  • KIN 471SKIN 471S: Kinesiology Service Learning
Select ONE Concentration and complete the associated coursework
Exercise Science Concentration
Complete ALL of the following courses:
  • KIN 310KIN 310: Athletic Strength & Condition
  • KIN 340KIN 340: Personal Training
  • KIN 421LKIN 421L: Essential of Exercise Sci Lab
  • KIN 430KIN 430: Phys & Biomech Aerobic Exer
  • KIN 440KIN 440: Phys & Biomech Anaerobic Exer
  • KIN 450KIN 450: Exer Prescript for Special Pop
Complete ONE of the following courses:
  • KIN 320KIN 320: Sports Nutrition
  • KIN 395KIN 395: Special Topics
  • KIN 469KIN 469: Kinesiology Internship
  • KIN 490KIN 490: Evolutionary Medicine

Students must also complete 5 units of elective coursework

 Wellness Concentration

  • KIN 367KIN 367: Consumer Advocacy

  • KIN 450KIN 450: Exer Prescript for Special Pop
  • KIN 461KIN 461: Stress Management

  • PH 417PH 417: Global Women's Health Issues

  • PSY 310PSY 310: Biological Psychology
