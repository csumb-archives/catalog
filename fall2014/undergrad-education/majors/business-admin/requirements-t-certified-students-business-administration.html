--- layout: archive title: Requirements for AS-T Certified Students in Business Administration CSUMB Catalog year: fall2014 ---

If you transferred into CSUMB as an AS-T-certified student in Business, you must complete the following courses for your bachelor's degree in Business Administration at CSUMB. If you are unsure about your transfer status, please talk to an advisor.

Major Core Requirements

Complete ALL of the following courses:
  • BUS 299: Decision Making w/ Excel
  • BUS 300S: Business Ethics in Action
  • BUS 304: BUS Com, Ethics & Crit Thkg
  • BUS 305: Principles of Management
  • BUS 306: Fundamentals of Marketing
  • BUS 307: Finance
  • BUS 308: Computer Information Systems
  • BUS 309: Principles of Operations Mgmt  OR  BUS 311: Oprn Mgmnt -Service Orgs  OR BUS 312: Principles of Hosp Oper Mgmt
  • BUS 310: Entrepreneurship
  • BUS 499: Strategic Mgmt (Sr Capstone)
  • SBS 350: Domination & Resistance: US or SBS 385: Environmental History of CA (or any other upper division course approved to meet the D2 requirement)

Concentration or Elective Business Courses

Select from the following concentrations, or Business Administration majors with no chosen concentration must take a minimum of 16 units of upper division business electives in addition to the major core courses. 

Accounting  
Complete ALL of the following courses:
  • BUS 383: Intermediate Accounting I
  • BUS 384: Intermediate Accounting II
  • BUS 385: Income Tax I
  • BUS 484: Advanced Accounting I

Students with an Accounting Concentration are strongly encouraged to take BUS 482: Audit I (Financial Auditing) or BUS 582: Auditing & Assurance as further preparation for the CPA exam.

Agribusiness 

Complete ALL of the following courses:
  • CHEM 109: Introductory Chemistry **
  • ENVS 201: Intro to Environmental Science **
  • BUS 435: Supply Chain Mgmt: Agribus 
  • BUS 434: Policy/Reg in Env Agribus
Complete TWO of the following courses:
  • BUS 322: Integrated Mktg Com
  • BUS 330: Human Resources Management
  • BUS 421: Marketing Research
  • BUS 454: Adv Agribusiness Cr & Fin
  • BUS 425: Global Marketing

** Agribusiness students are required to have a foundation in Plant and Soil Science. Substitutions for these courses will only be approved if they relate to Plant and/or Soil Science and if they also meet General Education “B” area requirements.

Entrepreneurship/Intrapreneurship 
Complete ALL of the following courses:
  • BUS 451: Venture Formation/Finance 
  • BUS 322: Integrated Mktg Com OR BUS 471: Customer Service Management 

AND Students must take any two additional upper division BUS electives for 8 units.

Hospitality Management  
Complete ALL of the following courses:
  • BUS 311: Oprn Mgmnt -Service Orgs OR BUS 312: Principles of Hosp Oper Mgmt **
  • BUS 373: Hotel/Resort Sales, Adv, PR
  • BUS 478: Internship (Hospitality) 
  • BUS 471: Customer Service Management 
  • BUS 498: Internship/Hosp Prof Wk Exp

 * * Hospitality and Hotel/Resort Management students are required to take BUS 311: Oprn Mgmnt -Service Orgs or BUS 312: Principles of Hosp Oper Mgmt to meet the Principles of Operations Management Upper Division Major Requirement. Substitutions for these courses will only be approved if the student took 309 prior to declaring Hospitality or Hotel/Resort Management concentration.

Hotel/Resort Management 
Complete ALL of the following courses:
  • BUS 311: Oprn Mgmnt -Service Orgs OR BUS 312: Principles of Hosp Oper Mgmt**
  • BUS 373: Hotel/Resort Sales, Adv, PR
  • BUS 478: Internship (Hospitality) 
  • BUS 471: Customer Service Management 
  • BUS 498: Internship/Hosp Prof Wk Exp

 * * Hospitality and Hotel/Resort Management students are required to take BUS 311: Oprn Mgmnt -Service Orgs or BUS 312: Principles of Hosp Oper Mgmt to meet the Principles of Operations Management Upper Division Major Requirement. Substitutions for these courses will only be approved if the student took 309 prior to declaring Hospitality or Hotel/Resort Management concentration.

Information Technology/eCommerce 
Complete ALL of the following courses:
  • BUS 460: E-Com: Emerg Bus Models-Iss
  • BUS 469: Database Management

AND Students must take any two additional upper division BUS electives 

Management and International Business 
Complete ALL of the following courses:
  • BUS 330: Human Resources Management
  • BUS 331: Organizational Behavior 
  • BUS 493: Int'l Comparative Mgmt
Complete ONE of the following courses:
  • BUS 356: Personal Fin Mgmt & HR Stat 
  • BUS 425: Global Marketing
  • BUS 433: Mgmt of Nonprofit Organization
  • BUS 435: Supply Chain Mgmt: Agribus
  • BUS 434: Policy/Reg in Env Agribus
  • BUS 460: E-Com: Emerg Bus Models-Iss 
  • BUS 469: Database Management
Marketing 
Complete ALL of the following courses:
  • BUS 322: Integrated Mktg Com
  • BUS 421: Marketing Research 
  • BUS 425: Global Marketing
Complete ONE of the following courses:
  • BUS 323: Service Marketing
  • BUS 460: E-Com: Emerg Bus Models-Iss 
  • BUS 435: Supply Chain Mgmt: Agribus
