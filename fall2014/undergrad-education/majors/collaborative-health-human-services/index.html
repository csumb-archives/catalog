If you aspire to serve your community and fellow human beings, our CHHS major will prepare you well. It will open the door to career success and personal fulfillment in health, social work, public policy, public safety, nonprofit management and more.

You will find all of the official information about the Collaborative Health and Human Services major right here. Simply use the navigation or the matching links at the bottom of this page.

For more detailed information including a look at internship and fieldwork opportunities and faculty, please visit the Health, Human Services, and Public Policy Department website.
