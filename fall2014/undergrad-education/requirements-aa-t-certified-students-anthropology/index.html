--- layout: archive title: Requirements for AA-T Certified Students in Anthropology CSUMB Catalog year: fall2014 ---

If you transferred into CSUMB as an AA-T-certified student in Anthropology, you must complete the following courses for your bachelor's degree in Social and Behavioral Sciences with a concentration in Anthropology at CSUMB. If you are unsure about your transfer status, please talk to an advisor.

Complete ALL of the following courses:
  • SBS 300SBS 300: Major/ProSem:Theo/Meth/SBS
  • SBS 300LSBS 300L: Professional Skills Soc Sci
  • SBS 362SBS 362: Rsrch Mthd for SBS: Qual Emph
  • SBS 362LSBS 362L: Rsrch Mthd SBS: Qual Emph Lab
  • SBS 366SBS 366: Rsrch Mthd for SBS:Quant Emph
  • SBS 366LSBS 366L: Rsrch Mthd SBS:Quan Emph Lab
  • SBS 345SBS 345: Cultural Anthropology
  • SBS 379SBS 379: Tattoos Makeup & Burkas
  • SBS 384SSBS 384S: Anthrop of Education
  • SBS 388SBS 388: Crisis/Develop/Mexican/Society
  • SBS 400SBS 400: Senior Capstone Seminar I
  • SBS 402SBS 402: Senior Capstone Seminar II
  • SBS 405SBS 405: Assessment Lab/Grad/Srs
  • SBS 350SBS 350: Domination & Resistance: US  OR SBS 385SBS 385: Environmental History of CA
Students must also demonstrate second language proficiency at the 201 level. 

Read more about the CSUMB Language Profeciency Requirement 

If additional coursework is necessary to reach 60 units, students may select from the following courses:
  • SBS 397SBS 397: Independent Study
  • SBS 395SBS 395: Special Topics
  • SBS 485SSBS 485S: Applied Research/Rural Com
