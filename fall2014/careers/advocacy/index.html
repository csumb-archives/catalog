If you want to pursue a career in advocacy, we offer the following pathways:
  • Collaborative Health & Human Services BA (Major)
  • Education M.A. (Graduate Degree)
  • Environmental Health Policy (Minor)
  • Human Communication BA (Major)
  • International Health Policy (Minor)
  • Master of Social Work (Graduate Degree)
  • Social & Behavioral Sciences BA (Major)
  • Social Work (Minor)

Some pathways may require additional coursework outside the major. Talk to your advisor to find out how best to meet your personal, academic and career goals.
