If you want to pursue a career in business, we offer the following pathways:
  • Anthropology (Minor)
  • Business Administration (Minor)
  • Global Studies (Minor)
  • Global Studies BA (Major)
  • Human Communication (Minor)
  • Mathematics BS (Major)
  • Mathematics (Minor)
  • Nonprofit Management (Minor)
  • Spanish BA (Major)
  • Statistics (Minor)

Some pathways may require additional coursework outside the major. Talk to your advisor to find out how best to meet your personal, academic and career goals.
