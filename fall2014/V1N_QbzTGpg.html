In order to graduate, you will also need to complete your general education and university requirements.

Required courses  are identified below. In order to graduate, students must also meet all general education and university requirements. 

Students are required to take a Musicianship and Theory placement assessment and complete a diagnostic audition. MPA 100: Introduction to Music will be required for any student who does not successfully pass the Musicianship and Theory diagnostic assessment with a C- or higher.

Complete ALL of the following courses:
  • MPA 110: Keyboard I
  • MPA 210: Keyboard II
  • MPA 101: Musicianship & Theory I
  • MPA 102: Musicianship & Theory 2
  • MPA 300: Major ProSeminar
  • MPA 306: Music & Computers
  • MPA 335: Reflections on America
  • MPA 337: MusHistory I 500-1750 ONLINE
  • MPA 338: MusHistory II 1750-1900 ONLINE
  • MPA 317S: Community Involvement SL
Take each of the following courses for TWO semesters:
  • MPA 475: Senior Capstone
  • MPA 440: Master Classes/Lecture Demos
Complete TWO of the following courses:
  • MPA 341: Sight Reading for Singers
  • MPA 345: Improvisation
  • MPA 370: Composition/Arranging I
  • MPA 371: Composition & Arranging II
  • MPA 401: Conducting
Complete FOUR UNITS from the following course list:
  • MPA 211: Beginning Guitar
  • MPA 212: Intermediate Guitar
  • MPA 213: Jazz Blues Guitar
  • MPA 214: Guitar Ensemble
  • MPA 309: Global Percussion
  • MPA 310: Gospel Choir
  • MPA 313: Music & Performance
  • MPA 315: Performance Study Intermediate
  • MPA 320: Chorale
  • MPA 330: Voice Training
  • MPA 332: Voice Training II
  • MPA 340: Music Prod & Artistic Dev
  • MPA 350: Advanced Performing Band
  • MPA 405: Ensembles I
  • MPA 406: Ensembles II
  • MPA 415: Performance Study Advanced
  • MPA 420: Advanced Voice Ensemble

Select one of the following concentrations to complete the requirements of the Music degree:

Contemporary Studies Concentration
Complete ALL of the following courses:
  • MPA 336: Jazz History
  • MPA 345: Improvisation
  • MPA 365: Survey of Global Music
  • MPA 401: Conducting
Recording Technology Concentration
Complete ALL of the following courses:
  • MPA 307: Audio Production
  • MPA 308: Mixing & Editing
  • MPA 408: Advanced Audio Production
