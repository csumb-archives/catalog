CSUMB awards a variety of credit and non-credit certificate programs which are available through both campus and Extended/Continuing education units. Many certificate programs are designed in cooperation with professional societies and industries to provide either entry level knowledge and skills or professional growth and development. Most certificate programs are comprised of multiple courses, and the certificate is awarded upon successful completion of all required coursework.

  • Science Illustration
  • Applied Behavioral Analysis
  • Environmental Ed & Environmental Service (CEU Certificate)

 

The MA in Education program offers the following certifications that teachers can add to their teaching credential:

Croscultural, Language and Academic Development (CLAD)

National Board of Professional Teaching Standards (NBPTS)

For specific information visit the (CLAD®/BCLAD®) Examinations California Teacher of English Learners (CTEL™) Examination Web site at http://www.ctel.nesinc.com/ or the NBPTS web site at http://www.nbpts.org/
