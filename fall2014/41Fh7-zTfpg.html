If your goal is to positively impact the lives of children, share your love of learning, and/or contribute to beneficial social change through education, Liberal Studies will provide you with an opportunity to gain the knowledge, skills and perspectives you will need to succeed.

You will find the official information about the major right here in the Catalog.

For more detailed information, including a look at department news and faculty bios, please visit the Liberal Studies Department website. You can also have your advising questions answered by contacting the Liberal Studies Advising Center at liberal_studies@csumb.edu or (831)582-4560.
