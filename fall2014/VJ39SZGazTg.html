Questions prior to admission will be handled by MSW admissions at 831-582-5315, by contacting the MSW Program office at 831-582-5316, or by e-mail to msw@csumb.edu.
 
Potential applicants still working towards completion of their bachelor’s degree can visit www.assist.org to assess which courses will transfer to CSUMB.
 
Once you are admitted into the Master of Social Work program, you will be assigned a faculty advisor who will help you complete an Individual Learning Plan (ILP). The ILP is a personalized schedule of courses required for the Master of Social Work degree.
 
Faculty advisors conduct ongoing advising on an as-needed basis. They post and maintain office hours for academic advising either in person, by telephone or via email or video chat.
