If you transferred into CSUMB as an AA-T-certified student in Political Science, you must complete the following courses for your bachelor's degree in Social and Behavioral Sciences with a concentration in Political Economy at CSUMB. If you are unsure about your transfer status, please talk to an advisor.

Complete ALL of the following courses:
  • SBS 300SBS 300: Major/ProSem:Theo/Meth/SBS
  • SBS 300LSBS 300L: Professional Skills Soc Sci
  • SBS 328SBS 328: Social Science Theory Module
  • SBS 366SBS 366: Rsrch Mthd for SBS:Quant Emph
  • SBS 366LSBS 366L: Rsrch Mthd SBS:Quan Emph Lab
  • One additional SBS research methods course
  • SBS 339SSBS 339S: Sweat/Service/Solidarity SL
  • SBS 342SBS 342: Economic Thought
  • SBS 350SBS 350: Domination & Resistance: US  (Fulfills American Institutions requirement)
  • SBS 351SBS 351: Global Econ:Theory, Ethics 
  • SBS 400SBS 400: Senior Capstone Seminar I
  • SBS 402SBS 402: Senior Capstone Seminar II
  • SBS 405SBS 405: Assessment Lab/Grad/Srs
Complete TWO more advisor-approved 300-level 4-unit courses in SBS or GS
Students must also demonstrate second language proficiency at the 201 level. 

Read more about the CSUMB Language Proficiency Requirement.

If additional coursework is necessary to reach 60 units, students may select elective coursework.

Total units to degree after transfer: 60
