You can get into law school with virtually any undergraduate major, and the Pre-Law Minor will give you the edge you need. Even if you don't plan to become a lawyer, this minor will give you deeper understanding of legal matters and enhance communication and advocacy skills that will empower you in any career endeavor.

For additional information, please visit the Division of Humanities and Communication website.
