This pathway is one example of how a student might complete all the requirements for their degree and takes into account pre-requisites required.  A student’s actual pathway may be different given their unique situation.  There is flexibility built into the curriculum.  A student will want to work closely with an advisor and use the academic advisement report to develop a customized pathway.  

Use this sample pathway as a starting point to choose classes or discuss your plans with an advisor.
