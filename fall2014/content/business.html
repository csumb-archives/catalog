Today's employers in all career fields place high value on management skills and entrepreneurial attitudes. This minor provides you with the opportunity to develop both. These competencies also enhance your leadership, analysis, and decision-making capabilities, which will benefit you on the job and in life in general.

You will find all of the official information about the minor right here. For more detailed information including a look at related student work, faculty bios, partnerships, and school events, please visit the College of Business website.
