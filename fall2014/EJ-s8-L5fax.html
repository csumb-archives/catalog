This pathway is one example of how you might complete all the requirements for your degree and takes into account the prerequisites.  This pathway assumes that you have taken courses equivalent to: MATH 150: Calculus I and MATH 151: Calculus II, CST 231: Problm-Solving/Programng and CST 238: Intro to Data Structures.

Your actual pathway may be different given your unique situation.  We encourage you to follow this suggested pathway as closely as possible to ensure that you graduate on time. You will want to work closely with an advisor and use the academic advisement report to develop a pathway for a timely graduation date. Your advisor is your best resource when it comes to courses selection and sequences, and your personal academic plan.
