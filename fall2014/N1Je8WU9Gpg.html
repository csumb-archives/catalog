Complete ALL of the following core courses:
  • KIN 300KIN 300: Major Pro-Seminar
  • KIN 360KIN 360: Nutritional Science
  • KIN 360LKIN 360L: Nutritional Science Lab
  • KIN 370KIN 370: Anatomy & Physiology I
  • KIN 370LKIN 370L: Anatomy & Physiology I Lab 
  • KIN 371KIN 371: Anatomy & Physiology II 
  • KIN 371LKIN 371L: Anatomy & Physiology II Lab
  • KIN 420KIN 420: Essential of Exercise Science
  • KIN 345KIN 345: Sports Ethics OR KIN 355KIN 355: Ethics PA and Health 
  • KIN 471SKIN 471S: Kinesiology Service Learning
  • KIN 497KIN 497: Senior Capstone I
  • KIN 499KIN 499: Senior Capstone II
  • STAT 100STAT 100: Introduction to Statistics  OR  CHHS 203CHHS 203: Stats for Social Science

Exercise Science Concentration
