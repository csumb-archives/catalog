For information and academic advising related to the environmental science minor, please contact the Division of Science & Environmental Policy(SEP) by stopping by our office, calling, or emailing.

The SEP office is located in room E318 of the Chapman Science Academic Center. We are open throughout the year, and you are welcome to drop in any time to ask questions or schedule an advising appointment. The friendly people at the front desk can provide expert advice about the environmental science minor, its courses, and its learning outcomes as well as other academic programs offered through SEP.
