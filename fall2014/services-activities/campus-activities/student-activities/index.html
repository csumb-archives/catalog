Student Activities & Leadership Development fosters student development through co-curricular programming, community involvement, leadership opportunities and student advocacy. The department promotes learning outside the classroom, holistic wellness and engagement through a wide array of events, services, leadership positions and student employment opportunities.  Student Activities & Leadership Development encompasses the following functional areas: Associated Students, Otter Student Union, Student Organizations, Greek Life, Orientation & First-Year Programs, Otter Cross Cultural Center, Leadership Programs, Student Recreations, Student Center Operations and the Catalyst Center.  Get involved in everything from Otter Days, leadership conferences, student organizations, cultural enrichment programs, Multicultural Greek Council, student government and much, much more!
