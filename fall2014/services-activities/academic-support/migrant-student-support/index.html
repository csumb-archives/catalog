Providing support to students from a migrant background, MSSS fosters the success of its students by working closely with the Educational Opportunity Program and Student Support Services to enhance and supplement the services provided by those two programs. MSSS assumes the role of advocate and develops collaborative arrangements across campus to create a welcoming and supportive community.
