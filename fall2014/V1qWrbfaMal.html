The Otter Sports Center (OSC) houses a 10,000 square-foot gym, one racquetball court, a 2,500 square-foot fitness room with cardiovascular and weight training equipment, locker/shower facilities and an athletic training room.  The OSC is home of Otter Athletics' men & women's basketball and volleyball teams.  All indoor intramural activities also take place in the OSC.
