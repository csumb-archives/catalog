Credits Required

Total credits in the minor: 16 credits of upper-division coursework (grade "C" or above)

Students must complete

Two outcomes and their corresponding core courses (2 courses; 4 units each)
One elective course in one of your selected outcomes (4 units)
One more elective course from any of the outcomes (4 units)

Leadership and Management (Outcome)

Core course: BUS 305BUS 305: Principles of Management
Electives: BUS 331BUS 331: Organizational Behavior, BUS 330BUS 330: Human Resources Management, BUS 372BUS 372: Hotel/Resort Operations, BUS 493BUS 493: Int'l Comparative Mgmt


Marketing (Outcome)

Core course: BUS 306BUS 306: Fundamentals of Marketing
Electives: BUS 322BUS 322: Integrated Mktg Com, BUS 323BUS 323: Service Marketing, BUS 421BUS 421: Marketing Research, BUS 425BUS 425: Global Marketing


Finance (Outcome)

Prerequisite: BUS 203BUS 203: Financial Accounting
Core course: BUS 307BUS 307: Finance
Electives: BUS 383BUS 383: Intermediate Accounting I, BUS 384BUS 384: Intermediate Accounting II, BUS 385BUS 385: Income Tax I


Information Technology (Outcome)

Prerequisite: BUS 299BUS 299: Decision Making w/ Excel (If BUS 200BUS 200: Intro to Business Computing not taken for tech/info ULR)
Core Course: BUS 308BUS 308: Computer Information Systems
Electives: BUS 469, BUS 460BUS 460: E-Com: Emerg Bus Models-Iss


Entrepreneurship (Outcome)

Prerequisite: BUS 203BUS 203: Financial Accounting  (required)
Prerequisite: BUS 306BUS 306: Fundamentals of Marketing (required)
Prerequisite: BUS 307BUS 307: Finance (required)
Core Course: BUS 310BUS 310: Entrepreneurship
Electives: BUS 322BUS 322: Integrated Mktg Com , BUS 451BUS 451: Venture Formation/Finance (prereq BUS 310BUS 310: Entrepreneurship)
