Tim Angle, Dean

As Dean of the College of Extended Education and International Programs (EEIP) at California State University, Monterey Bay (CSUMB), Dean Timothy (Tim) Angle oversees all international activities at the university including international student recruitment, study abroad programs and building strong partnerships with institutions overseas.  He is responsible for EEIP's Special Sessions degree and certificate programs, online programs, the American Language and Culture Program, the Science Illustration Program, Summer Session, Winter Session and Open University programs.

Before moving to CSUMB, Dean Angle was the Assistant Provost for the Division of Outreach and Continuing Studies at the University of Mississippi.  During his tenure there he significantly expanded Study Abroad, ESL programs, as well as the offerings at the Universities for Regional Campus at Tupelo, DeSoto, Boonville and Granada.

Dean Angle received his Juris Doctorate Degree and Bachelors of Business Administration from the University of Mississippi, in Oxford.  Professionally, Dean Angle is a member of the University Professional Continuing Education Association (UPCEA), NAFSA: Association of International Educators, North American Association of Summer Sessions (NAASS), Association for Continuing Higher Education (ACHE) and has previously served as Section Chair of Continuing Legal Education Section of the Association of American Law Schools.
