CSUMB is accredited by the Accrediting Commission for Senior Colleges and Universities of the Western Association of Schools and College, 985 Atlantic Avenue, #100, Alameda, CA 94501, 510-748-9001.

For complete accreditation information, please visit the CSUMB.EDU/accreditation website.

Degree Approval

All CSUMB undergraduate and graduate degree programs have been approved by the California State University Chancellor's Office and endorsed by the California Postsecondary Education Commission as required by law. Every student graduating from CSUMB has been awarded a degree approved by the CSU and CPEC .
