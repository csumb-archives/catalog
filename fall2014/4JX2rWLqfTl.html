Required
  • PSY 100: Introduction to Psychology and PSY 100L: Intro to Psychology Lab
  • PSY 200: Intro/Research/Methd/Data/Anal and PSY 200L: Research Method Lab

  • PSY 110: Human Biology & Behavior or another GE B2 certified course
    Recommended
    • It is strongly recommended that you complete ALL lower-division General Education requirements prior to your junior year and enrolling in PSY 300: Expermntl/Psy & Human/Assesmnt.
    • Read on your own as much as you can about psychology and related fields.
