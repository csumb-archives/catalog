Nonprofit organizations in our society address a broad range of needs and issues, including art, education, environmental protection, healthcare, politics, recreation, religion, scientific research, and social welfare services. The minor in Nonprofit Management was designed to help meet the demand for leadership in this sector by providing you with the knowledge and skills needed to effectively lead and manage community-based nonprofit organizations.

The Nonprofit Management Minor is a collaboration between the Department of Health, Human Services & Public Policy and the College of Business. For more detailed information about the minor in Nonprofit Management, visit the Department of Health, Human Services & Public Policy website.
