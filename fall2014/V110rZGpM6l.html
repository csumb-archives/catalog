In order to graduate, you will also need to complete your general education and university requirements.

Complete ALL of the following courses:
  • MIT 600: Graduate ProSeminar
  • MIT 610: Info System Analysis&Design
  • MIT 663: eCommerce: Design & Manag
  • MIT 660: Org Behav Knowledge Org
  • MIT 651: Venture Form & Finan Strat
  • MIT 630: Info Tech Project Manag
  • MIT 699: Graduate Capstone

In addition, students must complete an additional 8 units of elective courses approved by the faculty advisor.
