Tuition & Fees ~ Spring 2008 Payment/Refund Deadlines

January 10, 2008 ~ Last day to receive mailed registration payments to avoid disenrollment ~ Last day to sign up for the Installment Payment Plan to avoid disenrollment (first installment due at time of signup)

 

January 11, 2008 ~ Last day to pay registration fees in person at the Campus Service Center to avoid disenrollment ~ Last day to apply for financial aid and have fees deferred to avoid disenrollment

 

January 13, 2008 ~ Last day to pay registration fees online to avoid disenrollment

 

January 14, 2008 ~ Starting day for registration payment to be required before registering (Includes students who were previously disenrolled for nonpayment.)

 

January 25, 2008 ~ Last day to register without a $25 late registration fee ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses and receive a full refund of mandatory fees

 

February 8, 2008 ~ Last day to drop from fulltime to part-time and receive a full refund of the state fee difference (nonresident students also receive full refund of tuition) ~ Second payment due for spring 2008 Installment Payment Plan

 

February 18, 2008 ~ Last day for Open University students to drop and receive a prorated refund

 

March 10, 2008 ~ Third and final payment due for spring 2008 Installment Payment Plan

 

April 1, 2008 ~ Last day of Spring 2007 fee deferment for financial aid students ~ Last day to have fees paid without a $20 administrative late fee and/or a $20 late housing fee ~ Last day to have fees paid without a Business & Finance hold placed on account

 

April 10, 2008 ~ Last day to officially withdraw or cancel all courses and receive a prorated refund of mandatory fees
