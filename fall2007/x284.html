Liberal Studies B.A. Teaching Pathway ~ Distinguishing Features

Field-based service learning prepares you for a future career through hands-on service and learning in diverse educational settings, with community partners and social service institutions.

Our Liberal Studies major is a state-approved Elementary Subject Matter Competency teacher preparation program.

Our multidisciplinary and multicultural program focuses on serving the local community and promoting educational opportunities for underrepresented populations.

Broad faculty expertise will prepare you in a wide range of areas related to education.

Opportunity for earning both a Liberal Studies bachelor's degree and the Multiple Subjects Teaching Credential in an abbreviated amount of time (four years and one semester OR five semesters for junior transfer students, when all lower-division requirements are fulfilled).
