Music Minor ~ Careers and Graduate Study

Graduates who have completed the Music Minor in conjunction with a CSUMB major have entered and achieved notable success in the following careers: audio engineering, copist, music composition, music education (K-12 and college), music production, performance musician, songwriting, and sound design.
