Service Learning Leadership Minor ~ Faculty and Student Quotes

CSUMB is the first university in the country to offer an academic program in Service Learning Leadership. We are national leaders in this field, and so a minor in Service Learning Leadership from CSUMB truly means something!  We're training the next generation of community educators and social-change agents. 
~ Dr. Seth Pollack, Associate Professor, Director, Service Learning Institute
