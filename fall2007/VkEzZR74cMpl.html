Information/Technology ULR ~ Approved Courses

Courses currently approved to fulfill the Information/Technology ULR are listed below .To ensure continued alignment between courses and learning outcomes, the Information/Technology Learning Community reevaluates course approvals regularly. Courses no longer satisfying the ULR can be viewed in the archive of previously approved courses.

The listing of a course as approved does not indicate that it will be offered in any given semester. Check current offerings on the CSUMB.EDU/schedule website or check the course descriptions to see how frequently a course is offered.
