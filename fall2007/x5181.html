Community Participation ULR ~ Approved Courses

Courses currently approved to fulfill the Community Participation ULR are listed below. To ensure continued alignment between courses and learning outcomes, the Community Participation learning community reevaluates course approvals regularly. Courses no longer satisfying the ULR can be in the archive of previously approved courses.

The listing of a course as approved does not indicate that it will be offered in any given semester. Check current offerings on the CSUMB.EDU/schedule website or check the course descriptions to see how frequently a course is offered.
