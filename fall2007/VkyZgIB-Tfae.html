Tuition & Fees ~ Fee Payment

You must pay all fees prior to registration. Fee payments may be made by cash, check, money order, or credit card at the Campus Service Center. Payments by check will also be accepted by mail (please do not mail cash or money orders). The cancelled check serves as your receipt. Payments should be mailed to:

  • Cashier’s Office
    CSUMB
    100 Campus Center, Building 84C
    Seaside, CA 93955

A dropbox is also available at the front of Building 84C for payments at any time. Please do not use the dropbox for cash payments.

Credit Cards

VISA, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover credit cards may be used for payment of student fees in person at the Campus Service Center.

Online Payments

For a nominal additional fee, you can make credit card payments online using SmartPay. With no fee, you can use your regular checking account to make an online payment using eCheck. Please refer to the CSUMB.EDU/payments webpages for further information and instructions on both options.
