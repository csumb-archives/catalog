Integrated Studies Special Major, B.A. ~ Recommended Preparation

The best preparation depends on which academic programs you plan to combine to create your integrated major. Thus, you should consult the Recommended Preparation information listed in the CSUMB Catalog for each CSUMB academic program that you plan on drawing from to create your Integrated Studies Special Major.
