Tuition & Fees ~ Summer 2007 Payment/Refund Deadlines

May 17, 2007 ~ Last day to receive mailed registration payments to avoid disenrollment ~ Last day to sign up for the Installment Payment Plan to avoid disenrollment (first installment due at time of signup)

 

May 18, 2007 ~ Last day to pay registration fees in person at the Campus Service Center to avoid disenrollment ~ Last day to receive Third Party contracts to avoid disenrollment (contracts must be in the hands of the Student AR staff)~ Last day to receive a Financial Aid Fee deferment to avoid disenrollment

 

May 20, 2007 ~ Last day to pay registration fees online to avoid disenrollment

 

May 21, 2007 ~ Starting day for no web registration and required payment before in-person registration (Includes students who were previously disenrolled for nonpayment.)

 

June 1, 2007 ~ Last day to register in the eight-week and first four-week summer sessions without a $25 late registration fee ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses from the eight-week summer session and receive a full refund of mandatory fees ~ Last day to drop courses from the first four-week summer session and receive any refund

 

June 15, 2007 ~ Second payment due for summer 2007 Installment Payment Plan

 

June 29, 2007 ~ Last day to register in the second four-week summer session without a $25 late registration fee ~ Last day to drop courses from the second four-week summer session and receive any refund

 

July 5, 2007 ~ Last day to officially withdraw or cancel all courses from the eight-week summer session and receive a prorated refund of mandatory fees

 

July 17, 2007 ~ Third and final payment due for summer 2007 Installment Payment Plan
