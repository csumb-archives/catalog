Career Development

Career Development coordinates on-campus student employment, internship opportunities, individual resume critique evaluations, career exploration resources, a career resource library, and graduate & professional school resource information. CDO maintains on-campus and off-campus job listings both in hard copy and online, and sponsors career development workshops/events, and career and graduate fairs.
