Human Performance and Wellness B.S.

The human performance and wellness professions continue to rate among the fastest growing in the world as people, corporations, and government entities have come to recognize that the wellbeing of individuals creates the foundation for the wellbeing of everything else, including our society and economy. By providing you with broad knowledge, skills, abilities, and attitudes, our  HPW major will prepare you to pursue a number of the highly demanded professions in the human performance and wellness field.

You will find the official information about the Human Performance and Wellness major right here. Simply use the left-side navigation or the matching links at the bottom of this page.

For additional information including a look at faculty bios, please visit the Human Performance and Wellness Education Department website.
