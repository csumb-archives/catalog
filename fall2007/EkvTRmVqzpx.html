Telecommunications, Multimedia, and Applied Computing B.S. ~ Typical Course Pathways

Because we have built our academic programs on an outcomes-based education model, you have lots of latitude to design an Individual Learning Plan to achieve your personal target outcomes while also satisfying University Learning Requirements, Graduation Learning Outcomes, and Major Learning Outcomes. Given that, you should consider the following pathways only as possibilities:

Incoming Freshman 4-Year Pathway

Transfer Student 2-Year Pathway

These pathways serve as recommendations, not prescribed programs of study. Before enrolling, you can use them to get a feel for what you can do in this program. After enrolling, you and your advisors can use them as a starting point for building your personalized outcomes-based ILP.

If you have any questions regarding the TMAC program, feel free to email the ITCD Peer Advisor at itcd_advisor@csumb.edu or visit his office in Building18, Room 150.
