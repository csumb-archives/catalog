Mathematics, B.S. ~ Learning Environment

We are committed to serving individuals with diverse backgrounds and goals by providing an innovative curriculum and dynamic learning environment that prepares students for a wide variety of careers where quantitative skills are used.

You and your peers will:

  • Work together in cooperative groups.
  • Use technology to model real-world situations.
  • Solve problems that have application in everyday life and in the world around you.
  • Communicate mathematical ideas in a common language.
  • Learn to make meaning out of mathematics.
