Computer Science and Information Systems Minor ~ Home

Technological sophistication will not only allow you to take advantage of all that the digital age offers. It will also catch the eye of employers. Whether you want to learn to develop or adapt software, create networks, or master information systems including databases, the Computer Science & Information Systems Minor provides the opportunity.

You will find all of the official information about the minor right here. Simply use the left-side navigation. For more detailed information including a look at student work and faculty bios, please visit the School of InformationTechnology and Communications Design website.
