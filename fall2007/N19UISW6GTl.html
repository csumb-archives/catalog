Do you offer a program in Latin American studies?

If you want to pursue a career in Latin American studies, we offer the following pathways:

  • Chicana/o Studies Minor
  • Hispanic Cultures and History Minor
  • Human Communication B.A.
  • World Languages and Cultures B.A.
