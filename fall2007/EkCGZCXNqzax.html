Literature and Popular Culture ULR

The Literature and Popular Culture ULR requires you to analyze, interpret, and appreciate literature and diverse forms of popular culture as artistic and cultural representation.

Popular culture includes film, music, television, folklore, crafts, mass media, youth culture, and other forms of communication.
