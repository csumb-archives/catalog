Human Development Minor ~ Faculty & Student Quotes

This minor provides students with deeper knowledge of the physical, cognitive, psychological, and emotional changes from conception to death.  Students learn practical skills in how to address the needs of individuals across the lifespan.
~Dr. Rob Weisskirch, Associate Professor of Human Development

This program helps prepare students for dynamic classroom experiences and helps them engage in a continuing discussion of how humans interact and make meaning.
~ Dr. Linda J. Rogers, Professor (retired '06), Liberal Studies Department
