Technology and Information ULR ~ Assessment

Course-Based Assessment

In each course satisfying the Technology and Information ULR, evidence is assessed to determine if you have satisfied the outcomes for the ULR. For a description of the evidence and the criteria that guide the faculty member assessing the evidence, email the Technology and Information Learning Community chair.

Alternative Assessments

You can also meet the requirements for this ULR through assessment of prior learning. To complete alternative assessment, you must submit a portfolio demonstrating achievement of the learning outcomes.

Alternative assessment assumes that you have already attained mastery of the outcomes. It is not an independent study. For information on how to qualify for alternative assessment, email the  Technology and Information Learning Community chair.
