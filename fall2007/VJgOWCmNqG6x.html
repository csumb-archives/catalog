Teaching Credential Programs

CSUMB offers academic programs leading to the Multiple Subject Teaching Credential with ELA/BCLAD, the Single Subject Teaching Credential with ELA/BCLAD, and the Education Specialist Mild/Moderate Needs Level I and Level II Credentials. This section of the catalog provides specific information regarding admission and curriculum requirements for these programs.
