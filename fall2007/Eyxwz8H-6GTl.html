International Health Policy Minor ~ Faculty and Student Quotes

The International Health Policy Minor’s interdisciplinary coursework in Collaborative Health & Human Services and Global Studies is a perfect combination of applied learning experiences for students who are considering careers in international health and advocacy agency settings. This minor will prepare you for a career that will make a difference in people’s lives in the global community.
~ Kim Judson, Director, Collaborative Health and Human Services Department
