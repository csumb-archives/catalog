Tuition & Fees ~ Fall 2007 Payment/Refund Deadlines

August 9, 2007 ~ Last day to receive mailed registration payments to avoid disenrollment ~ Last day to sign up for the Installment Payment Plan to avoid disenrollment (first installment due at time of signup)

 

August 10, 2007 ~ Last day to pay registration fees in person at the Campus Service Center to avoid disenrollment ~ Last day to apply for financial aid and have fees deferred to avoid disenrollment

 

August 12, 2007 ~ Last day to pay registration fees online to avoid disenrollment

 

August 13, 2007 ~ Starting day for registration payment to be required before registering (Includes students who were previously disenrolled for nonpayment.)

 

August 24, 2007 ~ Last day to register without a $25 late registration fee ~ Last day to officially cancel registration or drop all courses and receive a full refund of mandatory fees

 

September 10, 2007 ~ Second payment due for fall 2007 Installment Payment Plan~ Last day to drop from fulltime to part-time and receive a full refund of the state fee difference (Nonresident students also receive full refund of tuition.)

 

September 20, 2007 ~ Last day for Open University students to drop and receive a prorated refund

 

October 9, 2007 ~ Third and final payment due for fall 2007 Installment Payment Plan

 

November 1, 2007 ~ Last day of Fall 2007 fee deferment for financial aid students ~ Last day to have fees paid without a $20 administrative late fee and/or a $20 late housing fee ~ Last day to have fees paid without a Business & Finance hold placed on account ~ Last day to officially withdraw or cancel all courses and receive a prorated refund of mandatory fees
