Interdisciplinary M.A. with Instructional Science and Technology Emphasis ~ Home

An interdisciplinary program that integrates information technology, instructional design, and educational technology, MIST responds to the ever-increasing demand for professionals who understand learning theory, instructional design and technology, interactive multimedia, assessment, and research methods. Whether a corporate trainer, classroom educator, or a person developing coursework for those professionals, the largely online MIST curriculum will empower you to make a difference for those you serve.

You will find all of the official information about the Interdisciplinary Master of Arts with Instructional Science and Technology Emphasis right here. Simply use the left-side navigation.

For more detailed information including a look at student work and faculty bios, please visit the MIST pages on the  School of Information Technology and Communications Design website.
