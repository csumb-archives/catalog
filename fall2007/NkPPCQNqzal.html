Liberal Studies B.A. Liberal Learning Pathway ~ Overview

This pathway is designed for students who want to prepare themselves broadly to function effectively in the technological and diverse society of the 21st century. The curriculum ranges from business and technology to math and science to the humanities and social sciences. Many studies have established that this liberal arts focus provides you with the important critical thinking and communication skills needed in every professional occupation. You may choose to go directly to the workplace or pursue further study in graduate programs. Examples of graduate school career destinations from this pathway include law, medicine, the arts and sciences, social work, history, business, theology, criminology, journalism, and many others.
