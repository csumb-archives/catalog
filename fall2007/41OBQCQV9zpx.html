Peace Studies Minor ~ Careers and Graduate Study

Related Careers

Graduates from the Peace Studies Minor program will likely succeed in post-conflict international development fields and legal studies. Potential employers are most likely peacemaking non-governmental organizations (NGOs), such as:

  • American Friends Service Committee
  • Amnesty International
  • The Fund for Peace
  • International Alert
  • CARE
  • Carter Center
  • Peace Brigades International
  • Search for a Common Ground
  • TransAfrica
Graduate Studies

Graduate work in peace and conflict studies, mediation, and international relations is available. For examples of possible fields, please consult the Peace and World Security Studies Program's website.

Examples of graduate programs in peace studies include:

  • George Mason University, Institute for Conflict Analysis and Resolution
  • Eastern Mennonite University, Graduate Program in Conflict Transformation
  • School for International Training, Graduate Program in Conflict Transformation
  • Bradford University in England, Peace Studies Program
  • Syracuse Universities, Maxwell School
  • Clark University, International Development, Community and Environment
  • Monterey Institute of International Studies
