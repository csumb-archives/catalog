Technology and Information ULR ~ Course Archive

Courses that once fulfilled the Technology and Information ULR but no longer do are listed below along with the timeframe during which each was approved. Successful completion of a course within the timeframe indicated fulfilled the ULR.

ASMT 430 ~ Assessment of the Information Technology ULR  ~ 4 credits
Approved from Fall 1997 through Spring 2002

PROS 100 ~ First Year Seminar Science/Technology ~ 6 credits
PROS 100 became FYS 100 in Fall 2003.  Only the Science/Technology  PROS 100  section and Technology PROS 100 section fulfilled the ULR. No other PROS 100 section fulfilled this ULR.  Approved from Fall 1997 through Spring 2003.
