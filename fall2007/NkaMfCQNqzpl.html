World Languages and Cultures, B.A. ~ Careers and Graduate Study

Related Careers

WLC graduates have entered and achieved notable success in education, international marketing, and translation and interpretation.

Graduate Study

WLC graduates have attended a variety of graduate institutions including San José State ; CSU Sacramento; San Francisco State; CSU Fresno; and UC Los Angeles. 

They have entered and achieved notable success in the following fields of graduate study: Spanish language and Hispanic cultures; Latin American studies; and Spanish language and linguistics.
