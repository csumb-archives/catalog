Instructional Science and Technology, M.A. ~ Faculty and Student Quotes

There is an increasing demand for the blend of knowledge and skills offered by  the Masters in Instructional Science and Technology. This program provides an exciting opportunity in a variety of careers—educator, instructional designer, corporate trainer, curriculum developer, educational technologist, and a host of related occupations.
~ Dr. Marsha Moroh, Dean, College of Science, Media Arts and Technology

The Masters in Instructional Science and Technology program prepares learners to remain professionally up-to-date and in demand in the fast-changing landscape of modern education and training fields. The unique distributed learning experience—integrating intensive face-to-face seminars with state-of-the-art online learning—will not only enable our graduates to advance their current careers, but it will position them to assume leadership positions of education and training into the future. Successful educators and e-learning developers in the knowledge-based, global economy require greatest command of all the technological tools and methodologies available to them, and our MIST program is designed precisely for that.
~ Dr. Eric Tao, Chair, School of Information Technology and Communications Design

While the MIST program is preparing me for postgraduate studies that address the use of technology in education, it is also providing me with skills I can use to improve the courses I am teaching right now. MIST faculty are enthusiastic and engaging, and share a wealth of expertise in the fields supported by this program. I find my interactions with the faculty and within my cohort to be enlightening and often exhilarating.
~ Chris Khan '06
