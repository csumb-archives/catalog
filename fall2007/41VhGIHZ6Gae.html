Admissions ~ Hardship Petitions

CSUMB has established procedures for consideration of qualified applicants who would be faced with extreme hardship if not admitted. To petition, you should write to CSUMB Admissions & Recruitment regarding specific policies governing hardship admission. Requests are considered on a case-by-case basis.
