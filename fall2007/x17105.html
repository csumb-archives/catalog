Liberal Learning Pathway ~ Related Careers and Graduate Study

Careers

The Liberal Learning Pathway prepares students to function effectively in the dynamic, technological, and diverse society of the 21st Century. Many studies have shown that a Liberal Arts focus such as this provides students with the communication skills needed in every professional occupation.  

Graduate Study

A student may choose to go directly to the workplace or pursue further study in graduate programs anywhere in the world. Some examples of graduate school and career destinations are: law, medicine, business, theology, criminology, oceanography, journalism, social services, recreation services, senior services, education, counseling, and many more. We prepare the well-rounded scholar in the fine tradition exemplified by Sir Thomas More, "A Man (person) for All Seasons."
