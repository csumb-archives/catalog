Liberal Studies B.A. Liberal Learning Pathway ~ Academic Advising and Support Services

The Liberal Learning Coordinator, a specialist trained to advise you during your matriculation, works one-on-one with you to develop an Individual Learning Plan that outlines how you will achieve the Major Learning Outcomes. In addition, the Liberal Learning Coordinator offers career advice and guidance in future graduate school options.
