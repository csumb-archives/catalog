Library

The CSUMB Library provides access to more than 12,000 journals, magazines, and newspapers in electronic format to take advantage of the web’s enormous capacity for obtaining and sharing information. It also houses more than 52,000 books, 350 print journals, and 1,500 videos. The reference desk area offers over 30 computers to access Voyager—the online CSUMB Library catalog system—and reference librarians assist with database searches, library catalogs, and using the Internet. The library also offers ample quiet study space, group-study rooms, a listening/viewing room, and self-service photocopying.
