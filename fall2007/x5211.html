Catalog Archive

Archived versions of the CSUMB Catalog serve a number of purposes:

  • Students wishing to determine catalog rights can refer to program offerings and requirements in the appropriate archive version.
  • Program directors can refer back to ensure that students receive access to listed offerings and are held accountable to listed requirements from their entry through timely graduation.
  • Advisors can refer to the requirements listed in an archive version to help students determine what requirements they have met and how best to satisfy others in the future.
  • Employers and others wishing to verify coursework can refer to the course descriptions in the archive for the semester when a course was taken.
CSUMB.EDU/catalog Archive
