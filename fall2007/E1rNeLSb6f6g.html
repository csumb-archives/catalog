Liberal Studies B.A. Teaching Pathway ~ Overview

The Liberal Studies B.A. Teaching Pathway prepares you with a solid professional career foundation for entry into teacher education credential studies and graduate study. Our outcomes-based, multidisciplinary major integrates the arts and humanities, natural and social sciences, and service learning.  We emphasize breadth across a variety of disciplines with a subject area emphasis in at least one academic discipline.

Sponsored By

College of Professional Studies > School of Education > Liberal Studies Department

Included Academic Fields

Elementary school teaching, Special Education, and other educational settings

Online Components

Parts of this major may be completed  through courses offered completely or partly online.

The Liberal Studies Distributed Degree Completion Program allows completion of the major by offering some courses completely online and some hybrid courses that require one to four on-campus meetings in addition to online work.
