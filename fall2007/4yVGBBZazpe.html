Dan Kubiak

Interim Vice President for Administration and Finance

Dan Kubiak has over 35 years of business experience—much of it with financial institutions—in the areas of strategic and tactical planning, project management, business process evaluation and redesign, and technology systems development.

Mr. Kubiak is no stranger to higher education—while employed with Price Waterhouse, he worked with University of California-Berkeley and UCLA. He holds a master's degree in business administration from CSU-Long Beach.
