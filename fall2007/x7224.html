ASAP 95

  • Last updated in Charlotte: 10/09/06
  • Last posted to Banner: 10/10/06

Provides supplemental language development support for students enrolled in a WRT 95 Writing Workshop course. Students work in small groups or in an individual semester-long appointment with a tutor from the Academic Skills Achievement Program (ASAP) and receive assistance on writing and reading course assignments and study skills. Credit/no credit only. (Offered every semester.)

ASAP 400

  • Last updated in Charlotte: 10/09/06
  • Last posted to Banner: 10/10/06

Provides supplemental support for senior capstone courses. Students work in small groups  or in an individual semester-long appointment with a tutor from the Academic Skills Achievement Program (ASAP), and receive assistance on parts of the writing process including: organizing research, finding a topic, formulating a thesis, developing content, and proofreading strategies . Must be taken concurrently with a Senior Capstone 400 course. Credit/no credit only. (Offered every semester.)
