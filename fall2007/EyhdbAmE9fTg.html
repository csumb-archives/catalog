Student Disability Resources (SDR)

With a goal of providing academic accommodations and other support services that promote success, SDR assists students who have learning, mobility, vision, or psychological disabilities; are deaf or hard of hearing; have other chronic medical conditions that result in disability; and experience temporary disabilities.
