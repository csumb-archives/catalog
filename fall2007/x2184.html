Admissions ~ Admission Procedures and Policies

As one of the 23 member campuses in the California State University system, CSUMB follows standardized admissions policies, procedures, and requirements adopted at the CSU system level.

Requirements for admission to CSUMB are in accordance with Title 5, Chapter 1, Subchapter 3, of the California Code of Regulations. Complete information is available on the CSUmentor.edu/planning webpages.

 

Note: Adopted at the California State University system level and mandated for inclusion in all CSU campus catalogs , gray-colored text applies to  all 23 campuses in the system. Black text pertains uniquely to CSUMB.
