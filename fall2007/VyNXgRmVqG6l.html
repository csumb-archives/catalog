Do you offer a program in journalism?

If you want to pursue a career in journalism, we offer the following pathways:

  • Creative Writing and Social Action Minor
  • Human Communication B.A.
  • Human Communication Minor
  • Journalism and Media Studies Minor
  • Mathematics B.S
  • Mathematics Minor
