College of Science, Media Arts & Technology ~ Divisions, Schools & Departments

The College of Science, Media Arts and Technology houses several academic units that administer a variety of academic offerings including undergraduate and graduate degrees, minors, and certificate programs as listed below. The academic units within the college organize learning experiences thematically and topically with an emphasis on finding workable and creative solutions to important local, regional, national, and global problems. Through their studies, students gain the knowledge and practical skills necessary to adapt and succeed while making useful contributions in a rapidly changing world.

Full descriptions of the divisions, schools, and departments may be found on the SMART college website or the individual unit websites as listed below. Official catalog descriptions of their academic offerings may be reached directly by clicking on the title of a program in the list below.

School of Information Technology and Communications Design (ITCD) website
  • Telecommunications, Multimedia, and Applied Computing B.S.
  • Instructional Science and Technology M.A.
  • Management and Information Technology M.S.  [in conjunction with the School of Business within the College of Professional Studies]
  • Communication Design Minor
  • Computer Science and Information Systems Minor
  • Certificate in System Administration
  • Certificate in Instructional Design Technology
  • Certificate in Computer Networking
Division of Science & Environmental Policy (SEP) website
  • Coastal and Watershed Science & Policy M.S.
  • Earth Systems Science & Policy B.S. 
  • Marine Science M.S. [administered through Moss Landing Marine Laboratories (MLML) and CSUMB]
  • Earth Systems Science & Policy Minor
  • Environmental Health Policy Minor [in conjunction with the Health, Human Services, and Public Policy Department within the College of Professional Studies]
  • Certificate in Environmental Education
Mathematics and Statistics Department (MATH) website
  • Mathematics B.A.
  • Mathematics Minor
Teledramatic Arts and Technology Department (TAT) website
  • Teledramatic Arts and Technology B.A.
