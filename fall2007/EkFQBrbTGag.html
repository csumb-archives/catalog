Creative Writing and Social Action Minor ~ Distinguishing Features

This minor brings together the study of creative writing, culture, communication, and community involvement.

You gain practical experience in the craft of creative writing.

You learn to use creative writing as a form of critical inquiry and an act of social responsibility. 

By focusing on your particular social action concerns, you learn their social, cultural, and political contexts. 

You apply your craft in service learning and community writing projects.
